FDD extraction rules:
- One PDF per run.
- Preserve uncertainty and contradictions.
- Tables, notes under tables, exhibits, addenda, financial statements, and franchisee lists are mandatory evidence objects.
- No field without supporting evidence.
- If something exists but is not directly surfaced, say so explicitly.
- Prefer writing output files over long inline responses.
- Do not run broad retries unless a coverage audit identifies a material gap.
