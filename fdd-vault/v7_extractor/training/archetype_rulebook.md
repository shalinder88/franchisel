# Archetype Rulebook — Training Asset (Not Runtime Code)

## Purpose
Brand-specific lessons for archetype tuning, regression tests, and reviewer hints.
Do NOT hardcode these into the extractor runtime.

## Trained Archetypes

### QSR / Restaurant
- **McDonald's**: Rent-engine / real-estate-control / co-investment / strong Item 19
- **Papa John's**: Three formats (Standard, Small-Town, Non-Traditional), single-unit still signs development agreement, QCC distribution
- **Zaxby's**: High-capex quick-casual, securitization lineage, 143 company restaurants, rich Item 20 exhibits

### Fitness / Membership
- **Planet Fitness**: EFT-membership, high-capex, re-equip/remodel cycle, vendor-controlled
- **F45 Training**: Single-studio, heavy supplier control, mandatory minimum payments, sales-performance risk
- **Anytime Fitness**: 24/7 access, development agreement for 2+ centers, Purpose Brands/SEB structure, reciprocity mandatory
- **Orangetheory**: Studio model, 3-5 studio area development, affiliate-owned studios, 2024 family expansion (Anytime, Waxing the City, Bar Method, Basecamp)

### Captive-Site License
- **Chick-fil-A**: License program, NOT standard operator-franchise. Company selects site, owns real estate, operator is licensee.

### Home Services / Restoration
- **SERVPRO**: Securitized platform, program-driven, broad Gross Volume definition, layered fees, no traditional Item 19

### Personal Care
- **Great Clips**: Multi-path development, heavy supplier/tech control, closed-loop technology, strong-but-subset-sensitive Item 19

### Full-Service Restaurant
- **Panera Bread**: Core and Small Box formats, optional development/construction services ($300K), deep tech/IP layer, patents on ordering

### Jersey Mike's (Cross-Archetype Lessons)
- Entity-name mismatch between parent and franchisor
- Mandatory ADA with no refund on missed deadlines (hidden Item 11 kill switch)
- Affiliate supplier economics
- Spousal liability
- Item 19 excludes non-traditional restaurants
