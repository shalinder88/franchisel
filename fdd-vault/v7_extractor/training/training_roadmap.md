## Phase 2: Controlled Batches of 10
After gold brands pass, process batches of 10 with heavy QA.
Each batch should include diverse archetypes.

## Phase 3: Top 100 Premium Layer
High-trust extraction with confidence tiers.
Publish with standard gates.

## Phase 4: Scale
Rule library + confidence tiers for broader coverage.
Automated pipeline with reviewer queues.

## Confidence Tiering Policy
- Gold: all major tables imported, all exhibits followed, human-reviewed
- Silver: most tables imported, financial exhibits parsed, automated QA passed
- Bronze: universal extraction complete, some tables missing, needs review
- Draft: extraction started, major gaps remain
