## Key Financial Observations

**Auditor:** Deloitte & Touche LLP — unqualified opinion.

**Revenue:** Total revenues of $381.0M in FY2025 (full year) vs $304.3M in the initial 10-month period (Nov 28, 2023 to Sept 29, 2024). Company restaurant sales of $282.8M represent 74% of total revenue. Franchise royalties and other of $55.7M represent 15%.

**Debt structure:** Long-term debt (net of current portion) of $244.1M at FY2025, down from $253.4M. The securitization transaction involved issuance of Class A-2 Notes of $234.5M. A revolving credit facility was drawn $8.0M (FY2025) and $25.0M (initial period), with $20.0M repaid. Interest expense was $25.3M in FY2025.

**Equity:** Total member's equity of $136.0M. Net affiliate distributions exceeded net income: $55.8M distributed vs $32.8M earned in FY2025. Member's contribution declined from $92.5M to $74.0M, meaning the parent is extracting capital faster than the entity earns it.

**Operating cash flow:** $56.2M generated from operations vs $56.0M used in financing (primarily affiliate distributions). The entity is essentially cash-flow neutral after distributions — operating cash flow funds affiliate distributions rather than being retained for reinvestment.

**Asset composition:** Total assets of $555.6M dominated by operating lease ROU assets ($145.1M), tradename ($167.0M, carried at cost with no impairment), goodwill ($132.4M), and property/equipment ($65.8M). The tradename and goodwill together represent $299.4M or 54% of total assets.

**Leverage:** Total liabilities of $419.7M against equity of $136.0M — a debt-to-equity ratio of approximately 3.1:1. Long-term debt of $244.1M plus operating lease liabilities of $145.1M represent combined obligations of $389.2M.

**Management fee:** The entity pays $22.7M in management fees to QRC annually, representing 6.0% of total revenues — a material related-party cost.
