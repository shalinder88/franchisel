## Key Observations
- All 23 items present and classified
- Exhibits A through Z with extensive sub-exhibits
- Financial statements are RBI consolidated (parent guarantor), not BKC standalone
- RBILP provides guaranty for CA, IL, MD, ND, RI, VA, WA residents; RBI for all others
- Carrols Refranchise Program exhibits present (F1-F3) — significant 2024 acquisition activity
- Extensive state addenda in Exhibit P
- Item 19 includes both sales distributions and remodel uplift data
- Item 20 shows massive Carrols reacquisition in 2024 (1,023 restaurants)
- No EBITDA or cost build-up in Item 19 (gross sales only)
