(b) 
Net of Closures Requirement.  Developer acknowledges and agrees that all Cumulative Opening 
Targets must be achieved net of closures (as set forth in this Development Schedule and in Article III) so 
that such targets represent net restaurant growth (or NRG) in the Territory for each Development Year or 
portion thereof, as the case may be, and (ii) in the event of the closure of any Developer Restaurant in the 
Territory (including without limitation Developer Restaurants owned and operated by Developer and/or its 
Affiliates prior to the Commencement Date and Developer Restaurants not developed or opened pursuant 
to the Agreement), and without distinction as to the reason for such closure (i.e., expiration, early 
termination or otherwise), such Restaurant must be replaced by a new Franchised Restaurant by the end 
of the Development Year in which the closure occurred, as necessary, in order to achieve the Cumulative 
Opening Targets net of closures.
