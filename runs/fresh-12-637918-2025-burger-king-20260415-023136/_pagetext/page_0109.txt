REMODELED RESTAURANT SALES UPLIFT RESULTS BY ENROLLMENT IN RECLAIM 
THE FLAME PROGRAM 
The Reclaim the Flame remodel program was launched in 2022 and provides franchisees the 
opportunity to earn incentives by completing the remodel of certain Burger King Restaurants by certain 
deadlines.  The chart below provides information on the results of certain of the Restaurants in the program.  
Of the Remodeled Restaurants, we have identified 118 of these Restaurants that were enrolled in the 
Reclaim the Flame remodel program that had completed a remodel on or before December 31, 2023 and 
that were open and operating for the entire 12 month period ended December 31, 2024.   Of the 118 
Remodeled Restaurants discussed above, we have identified 23 of these Restaurants that underwent a 
Scrape & Rebuild remodel.  These Restaurants have been operating between 13 and 57 years 
depending upon the Restaurant.  We also identified 95 of these Restaurants enrolled in the program that 
underwent a Full remodel. These Restaurants have been operating between 12 and 70 years depending 
upon the Restaurant. Uplifts for each group of these Restaurants are shown in the table below.
