Multi-year Sales Uplift Results for Remodeled Restaurants 
We reviewed the sales data of the Remodeled Restaurants for the 3 year period following the 
completion of the remodel for all Remodeled Restaurants that had at least one full year (12 months) of post 
remodel performance as of December 31, 2024, as measured from the remodel completion date. For each 
of the  years following the remodel completion date, we compared the cumulative same Restaurant sales of 
each of these Remodeled Restaurants against the Control Restaurants.  
We then grouped the Remodeled Restaurants by the total number of years of post-remodel sales 
performance.  

1 Year Post Remodel Performance: All Remodeled Restaurants that had only one full year (12 months) 
of post remodel performance as of December 31, 2024.  

2 Years Post Remodel Performance: All Remodeled Restaurants that had only two full years (24 
months) of post remodel performance as of December 31, 2024. 

3 Years or More Post Remodel Performance: All Remodeled Restaurants that had at least three full 
years (36 months) or more of post remodel performance as of December 31, 2024.
