### Material Gaps Requiring Retry
1. **Item 21 — Financial Statement Detail:** Only key P&L summary lines captured from Exhibit Q. Auditor name, opinion type, and full balance sheet/cash flow not extracted. **EXECUTE targeted retry.**

### Partial Coverage (acceptable)
2. **State addenda specifics:** Only Washington read in full. Other states (CA, IL, MD, MI, MN, NY, etc.) may have material franchise-protective overrides. Catalogued but not individually extracted. Acceptable for first-pass — would require exhibit deep-read for complete coverage.
3. **Franchise Agreement specific clauses:** D1/D2 sampled but not fully clause-extracted. Key terms are adequately covered via Item 17 summary tables.
4. **Franchisee location counts by state:** Available in Exhibit O1 but not individually counted. Item 20 Table 3 provides state-level status data.

### Missing (not available in FDD)
5. **EBITDA / cost data:** Not disclosed in Item 19. Only gross sales. The Fuel the Flame agreement references 4-Wall EBITDA thresholds ($175K, $230K) but this is not an Item 19 financial performance representation.
6. **Actual rent amounts:** BKL rent formulas disclosed but actual dollar amounts are site-specific and not standardized.
7. **Carrols Refranchise purchase price ranges:** Not disclosed — varies by transaction.
