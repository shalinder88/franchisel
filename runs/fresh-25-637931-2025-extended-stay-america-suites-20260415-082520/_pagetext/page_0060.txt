Table 19-2 Franchised Hotel Operating Metrics 
The second set is 93 Brand Hotels operated by franchisees for the entire 2024 calendar year (the “Franchised 
Hotels”). As of December 31, 2024, there were 107 hotels operated by franchisees as EXTENDED STAY 
AMERICA SUITES including 5 that were managed by one of our affiliates.  Four of the 5 managed hotels 
are included in this set as their results are not materially different from the results of other hotels operated 
by franchisees that are included in this set.  One of the managed hotels was excluded because it was closed 
during the year for renovations and thus had materially different results. One other franchised hotel was 
excluded as it was sold out for the entirety of the year pursuant to a railroad company contract and had 
materially different results. One hotel both opened and closed as a Brand Hotel operated by a franchisee in 
2024 after a franchisee purchased an Owned Hotel from our affiliate in August 2024 and then voluntary 
ceased operations in December 2024. We also excluded 9 additional Brand Hotels operated by franchisees 
that ceased operations in 2024. Of the 107 hotels operated by franchisees at the end of 2024, 93 had been 
open for the entire 12-month period ended December 31, 2024.
