The fair values assigned to tangible and intangible assets acquired and liabilities assumed are based on management’s estimates and assumptions, which
include Level 3 unobservable inputs, and are determined using generally accepted valuation techniques. The excess of purchase consideration over the fair
value of other assets acquired and liabilities assumed was recorded as goodwill. The resulting goodwill is primarily attributable to increased expansion for
market opportunities, the expansion of club membership and synergies from the integration of the clubs into the broader corporate-owned club portfolio.
Approximately $175,600 of the goodwill recorded is expected to be amortizable and deductible for tax purposes, the majority of which is deductible over 15
years.
