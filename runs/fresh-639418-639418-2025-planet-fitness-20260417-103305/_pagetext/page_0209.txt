                                                APPENDIX G
                                          TO FRANCHISE AGREEMENT

                                        2026 MARKETING REQUIREMENTS

For the calendar year 2026, the following marketing requirements apply:

1. Local Advertising: From January 1, 2026, to December 31, 2026, you will spend the greater of (i) $50,000 or (ii)
6% of cumulative Monthly EFT for the year. The minimum first quarter and monthly LAF requirements will remain
unchanged.

2. National Advertising: From January 1, 2026, to December 31, 2026, you will contribute to the NAF 2% of the EFT
Dues Draft plus 1% of Monthly EFT for the calendar year (the “Annual NAF Percentage”).

         a. If the BUSINESS has opened as of March 31, 2026:

         During each month, you will contribute to the NAF an amount equal to the following percentages (the “NAF
         Percentage”):

             Month                                    NAF Percentage

             January, February, March                 3.6% of the EFT Dues Draft plus 1.8% of Monthly EFT

             April, May, June                         1.5% of the EFT Dues Draft plus 0.75% of Monthly EFT

             July, August, September                  1.5% of the EFT Dues Draft plus 0.75% of Monthly EFT

             October, November, December              1.5% of the EFT Dues Draft plus 0.75% of Monthly EFT


         In addition, if required, we will adjust the NAF Percentage applicable to the December EFT Dues Draft (the
“December NAF Percentage”) so that your NAF contributions for the year are equal to the Annual NAF Percentage.
The December NAF Percentage will be based on the EFT Dues Draft, Monthly EFT and your NAF contributions to
date and our forecast of the EFT Dues Draft in December. The Annual NAF Percentage may fluctuate by up to 1 basis
point due to the estimation involved in calculating the December NAF Percentage.

         b. If the BUSINESS opens on or after April 1, 2026:

         You will contribute to the NAF an amount equal to 2% of the EFT Dues Draft plus 1% of Monthly EFT each
         month.
