       Notes to Revenue and Operations Statement: Due to rounding, totals may not equal the
sum of individual lines. Please keep in mind the following as you review the above table:

        1.      Revenue – The principal source of revenue for a PLANET FITNESS club is
membership fees. Monthly and annual membership fees are usually paid through electronic
transfer of funds (EFT). Annual fees are billed to a member once per year, the time of which is
dependent on their join date and membership type, as detailed in their membership agreement. A
fitness facility will also earn additional revenue included in “Other Membership Sales” above
through enrollment fees, prepaid memberships, beverage sales, sale of tanning goggles and lotions,
PLANET FITNESS apparel and headphones.

        A Classic membership and a Black Card membership must be offered by a club at all times.
Certain promotions are required or offered at different times during the year and a franchisee may
have the ability to tailor the promotion to their club. The Black Card membership may require a
12-month commitment, unless otherwise permitted by us or mandated by applicable law.
Generally included with the Black Card is use of tanning, half-priced beverages, massage
chair/hydromassage bed usage, access to any other PLANET FITNESS club, ability to bring a
guest for free to the member’s home club, digital fitness content and affinity program discounts.
