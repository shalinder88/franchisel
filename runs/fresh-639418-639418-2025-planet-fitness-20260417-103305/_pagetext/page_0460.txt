Section XIII -- Nonprofit Status

        The Co-op is not intended to be a profit-making organization, and is neither expected nor
intended to make any profit. The Co-op shall use its funds only for the objectives and purposes
specified in these Bylaws. Any incidental profit derived from the lawful activities of the Co-op
may be retained and utilized by the Co-op consistent with applicable law and these Bylaws. The
Co-op does not intend to make regular distributions of income to its Members.

Section XIV -- Consent

       By executing a Franchise Agreement for a Planet Fitness Club to be operated within the
Cooperative Area, the franchisee of that Planet Fitness Club has consented to being a Member of
this Co-op and is deemed to have agreed to the terms of these Bylaws.

Section XV – Franchise Agreement Not Affected

        Notwithstanding anything to the contrary herein, these Bylaws do not affect Franchisor’s
rights under each Member’s respective Franchise Agreement. If these Bylaws conflict with a
given Member’s Franchise Agreement, the Franchise Agreement controls.
