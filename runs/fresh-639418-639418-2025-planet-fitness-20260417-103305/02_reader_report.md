## A. Executive Snapshot

1. **Franchisor**: Planet Fitness Franchising LLC, a Delaware LLC formed June 13, 2018; predecessor Pla-Fit Franchise offered franchises since February 2003.
2. **Ultimate parent**: Planet Fitness, Inc. (publicly traded). Corporate headquarters: 4 Liberty Lane West, Floor 2, Hampton, NH 03842.
3. **System size (Dec 31, 2024)**: 2,568 total outlets — 2,298 franchised + 270 company-owned. Net growth of +113 in 2024 (+97 franchised, +16 company-owned).
4. **Investment range**: $1,525,000 – $5,221,500 (depends on equipment purchase vs. finance).
5. **Initial franchise fee**: $20,000 (currently waived under Area Development Agreements).
6. **Royalty**: 7% of EFT Dues Draft (monthly and annual membership fees).
7. **NAF**: 2% of EFT Dues Draft (can increase to 3%), plus LAF of greater of $60,000 or 7% cumulative Monthly EFT.
8. **Item 19 highlights**: Franchised clubs — average Annual EFT Revenue: Bottom Third $1,205,580, Middle Third $1,803,265, Upper Third $2,613,753 (2,197 clubs). Corporate EBITDA: Bottom Third $266,824 (21%), Middle Third $683,438 (35%), Upper Third $1,089,084 (42%).

10. **Territory**: No exclusive territory under Franchise Agreement. Area Development Agreement provides limited protection in a Development Area during term if in compliance.
11. **Non-compete**: 2 years post-term, within 15 miles of location and any PF business. 5 years if franchisor exercises ROFR.
12. **Dispute resolution**: Mediation and arbitration in Portsmouth, NH; NH law governs.
13. **Gag clause**: Yes — "some franchisees have signed confidentiality clauses... restricting their ability to speak openly."
14. **Franchisor financials**: Total revenue $350.1M (2024), net income $301.3M, dividends to parent $306.9M.

## B. Front Matter, Fees, and Investment (Items 1–7)

### Item 1 — Entity Structure
Planet Fitness Franchising LLC is a Delaware LLC formed June 13, 2018, structured within a securitization framework. Key affiliates:
- **Planet Fitness Master Issuer LLC** (immediate parent) and **Planet Fitness SPV Guarantor LLC** (grandparent)

- **Planet Fitness Australia Franchise Pty Ltd** (Australia since Aug 2019)
- **Planet Fitness, Inc.** (ultimate parent, publicly traded, majority owner of Topco)

The franchise offers fitness training facilities including exercise machines, free weights, fitness training services, related services, amenities, and ancillary goods. The system includes a low-cost "Judgement Free Zone" positioning. Digital content may be offered directly to members.

Franchise types offered: new location, conversion, acquisition of company-owned location, area development.

## D. Territory, Channels, Encroachment (Items 12–16)

### Item 12 — Territory
**No exclusive territory under Franchise Agreement.** Franchisor retains unrestricted rights to:
1. Open or franchise additional PF locations anywhere.
2. Sell products/services through any channel (Internet, wholesale, retail).
3. Operate non-PF fitness businesses under other marks, even near your location.
4. Dual-brand or acquire/merge with competitors.
5. Enter commercial partnership agreements requiring your participation.
6. Require you to sublease 10% of your space to franchisor.

**Area Development Agreement** provides limited protection: franchisor won't develop PF in Development Area during ADA term if you're in compliance, with 5 carve-outs:
1. Excluded Businesses (pre-existing PF locations in your area).
2. Non-traditional locations (airports, military, hotels, universities, corporate offices, housing — right of first refusal, 60 days to respond).
3. Refranchising after franchisee's breach.
4. Brand acquisitions/conversions.
5. Multi-Territory Acquisitions (right of first refusal, 30 days; 66% threshold override).

After ADA expires: limited right of first refusal if fully complied with Development Schedule.

No relocations without franchisor consent. Cannot use Internet to sell branded merchandise.

## E. Contract Burden, Legal Mechanics (Item 17)

### Franchise Agreement
- **Term**: 12 years from date operations begin. May request +1 year extension or -2 year reduction to match lease.
- **Successor franchise**: Must meet 9 conditions: substantial compliance, cure all deficiencies, remodel, pay $20,000 successor fee, sign then-current SA (may contain materially different terms), maintain premises, meet training requirements, sign general release, be current on all obligations. If expired without renewal but franchisor permits continued operation: month-to-month with up to 4% royalty increase.
- **Termination by franchisee**: (1) On any grounds available by law. (2) On 30 days notice if franchisor materially fails to comply and doesn't cure within 60 days. (3) On 12 months notice if open 3+ years, EFT in lowest 5% for 12 consecutive months, and can demonstrate unprofitability.
- **Termination by franchisor (no cause)**: Not applicable.
- **Termination by franchisor (with cause)**: Curable defaults (24h to 60 days cure): payment, opening, operational/conduct, unfair competition. Non-curable: solvency, abandonment, misrepresentation, cross-default, anti-terrorism.
- **Transfer**: All transfers require prior written consent. Internal/family/estate transfers: no transfer fee but must pay out-of-pocket costs, sign release. Third-party transfers: $10,000 fee + costs, transferee must complete training, sign then-current FA (may have materially different terms), update/remodel. ROFR: franchisor may purchase at bona fide offer price.
- **Death/disability**: Treated as transfer.
- **Non-compete (during term)**: No involvement in any Competitive Business (fitness, exercise, athletic services, digital fitness). Applies to franchisee, owners, immediate family.
- **Non-compete (post-term)**: 2 years at location, within 15 miles of location, within 15 miles of any PF business. If ROFR exercised: 5 years within 15 miles of any PF business sold to franchisor.
- **Modification**: Must be in writing; but franchisor can unilaterally change manuals. Can also modify FA with 70% franchised-business approval. Can change NAF/LAF limits with 66% all-business or 51% franchised-business vote. Same vote can accelerate remodel/re-equip timelines.
- **Dispute resolution**: Mediation and arbitration. Venue: Portsmouth, NH (or then-current HQ city). Governing law: NH. Injunctive relief from any court of competent jurisdiction.

### Area Development Agreement
- **Term**: Until last location opens or last required opening date, whichever first.
- **No renewal or extension**.
- **Transfer**: Must transfer all FA + ADA together. $5,000/unmet obligation + out-of-pocket costs. Minimum holding period: 2 years (or 1 year if controlling owner has 2+ years PF experience).
- **Non-compete**: Same as FA, expanded to Development Area and 15 miles of any PF business developed in Development Area.
- **ROFR**: Same as FA.
