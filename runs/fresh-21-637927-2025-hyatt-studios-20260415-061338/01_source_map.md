## Key Observations
- **Zero operating outlets**: Brand launched franchising May 2023, but no hotels are yet open as of Dec 31, 2024
- **No Item 19 FPR**: Explicitly declines to make financial performance representations
- **Unregistered primary trademark**: "Hyatt Studios" application pending (filed April 2023)
- **Parent guarantee**: Hyatt Hotels Corporation guarantees franchisor obligations
- **Auditor**: Deloitte & Touche LLP, clean opinion, auditor since 2003
- **Goodwill impairment**: $110M impairment charge in 2024 on a reporting unit within management and franchising segment
