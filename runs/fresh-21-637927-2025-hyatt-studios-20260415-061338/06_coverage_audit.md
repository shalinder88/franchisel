2. **Franchise Agreement clause-by-clause**: Exhibit C (~89 pages) has not been walked clause by clause for termination triggers, cure periods, insurance requirements, force majeure, etc. → Deferred to A2 Depth Pass 2.

3. **State addenda structured overrides**: The 14 state addenda have been identified and key material read, but not yet structured into per-state override families (forum selection, noncompete, termination, governing law, etc.). → Deferred to A2 Depth Pass 4.
