requiring the franchisee to waive specific provisions of the law is contrary to public 
policy and is void and unenforceable.  The law also prohibits a franchisor from 
disclaiming or denying (i) representations it, its employees, or its agents make to 
you, (ii) your ability to rely on any representations it makes to you, or (iii) any 
violations of the law.
