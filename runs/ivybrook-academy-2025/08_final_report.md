## Key Contract Terms
- **Term:** 10 years + up to **2 renewal terms of 10 years each** (FA §4.2 operative; Item 17 summary under-stated — see adjudication in 17_conflict_adjudication.md)
- **Territory:** Lesser of 125,000 pop or 3-mile radius (partial exclusivity)
- **Noncompete:** 2 years / 10-mile radius post-term
- **Transfer fee:** $10,000 + $10,000 training fee
- **Renewal fee:** Greater of $10,000 or 10% of then-current franchise fee
- **ROFR:** Yes — 30-day match right, 60 days to close
- **Guaranty:** Unlimited personal (all owners + spouses/domestic partners)
- **Dispute resolution:** Mediation then arbitration in Charlotte, NC
- **Governing law:** North Carolina
- **Damages cap:** Franchisee limited to refund of franchise fee + royalty fees; 1-year statute of limitations
- **No fiduciary duty:** FA §21.2 explicitly disclaims fiduciary relationship
- **Time to open:** 6–18 months; termination right if not open within 18 months
- **Required certifications:** NAEYC (after 2 years), NAREA, BBB
- **Typical facility:** 6,000 sq ft, 180–250 students

## Special Risks and Red Flags
1. **Financial condition concern** — Special Risk #2 and Illinois addendum both flag franchisor financial condition. Illinois AG imposed fee deferral.
2. **42 signed-but-not-opened franchises** — Special Risk #4 warns of potential opening delays.
3. **High supplier control** — Nearly all purchases from designated/approved suppliers. Prices may exceed market (Special Risk #3).
4. **Affiliate supplier** — The Student Hub software exclusively from affiliate Catapult.
5. **Out-of-state dispute resolution** — Mediation/arbitration only in NC (Special Risk #1).
6. **Brand Fund increasing** — Expected to rise from 1% to 2% in 2026, raising total minimum recurring to 10%+ of GR.
7. **Territory modification on breach** — Franchisor may modify territory protections if franchisee breaches.
8. **Data ownership** — All customer data, enrollment data, and business data owned by franchisor.
