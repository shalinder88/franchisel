### 3. Renewal Term (Contradiction C-02)
- **Current run:** Flagged as contradiction — Item 17 says 1 renewal of 10 years; FA §4.2 says maximum of 2 renewals of 10 years each
- **Gold:** not tracked
- **PDF sources:**
  - Item 17(b), p. 38: table summary says "one additional 10-year term"
  - FA §4.2, p. 63: "Franchisee's right to a successor franchise is for a maximum of two renewal terms of 10 years each (each a 'Renewal Term'). Upon conclusion of the final Renewal Term, Franchisee will have no further right to operate the Franchised Business unless Franchisor grants Franchisee another franchise or agrees to further renewals, in Franchisor's sole discretion."
- **Adjudication:** **FA §4.2 is the governing operative language.** Item 17 tables are summaries; the FA is the binding contract. The operative provision grants up to **2 renewal terms of 10 years each** (total possible 30 years: 10 initial + 10 + 10). Item 17 summary is under-stated.
- **Classification: 5 — current extraction faithfully preserved the contradiction; no extraction error. Item 17 summary in the FDD itself is internally inconsistent with Exhibit A §4.2.** This is an FDD drafting inconsistency, not an extraction defect.
- **Action:** Keep C-02 preserved. Use FA §4.2 value (max 2 renewals) as the operative answer in canonical; flag Item 17 as a summary inconsistency.
