## Notable items for retry/audit

- **Exhibit J (financial statements, p168–197) is image-only** via text extraction. The audited financials for FY2022–FY2024 exist but are not directly surfaced in the extractable text layer. Only the Virginia-addendum risk factor (p137) discloses a structural number: stockholder's equity = $345,862 as of 12/31/2024. Item 8 (p22) discloses franchisor 2024 total revenue = $2,171,363.
- **Exhibit label inconsistency (contradiction preserved):** TOC (p7) lists Exhibit K = "Current Franchisees" and Exhibit L = "Franchise Disclosure Questionnaire". The physical exhibit pages p198–201 (current/former franchisees) and p202–204 (questionnaire) both carry the header "EXHIBIT L TO THE DISCLOSURE DOCUMENT". The franchisee list is therefore mislabeled on its own header pages vs the TOC. Item 20 body text (p53) further refers to "Exhibit K" as the franchisee list, while the page 2 "How to Use" Q&A refers to "Exhibit L" as the franchisee list.
- **Page 2 "How to Use" additionally says financial statements are in "Item 21 or Exhibit K"** — this contradicts the TOC and Item 21 body, which place financials in Exhibit J.
- **Limited state addenda coverage:** Only Illinois, Minnesota, and Virginia addenda are included in Exhibit B, although Ivybrook has open outlets in AR, CO, FL, GA, KY, NE, NC, OH, PA, SC, TN, TX, VA. No addenda for registration states such as CA, HI, MD, MI, NY, ND, RI, SD, WA, WI (per State Effective Dates page these are "Not registered" or "Pending"; IN shows 3/28/2025 effective, MN shows 5/9/2025).
- **No operations manual TOC extracted detail:** Exhibit I is one page and is a pointer to the Brand Standards Manual TOC; detailed structure not directly surfaced in text layer beyond the exhibit page itself.
- **No digital services / technology sublicense agreement exhibit**: The Student Hub (Catapult) sublicense is described in Item 8/11 body but no standalone technology agreement exhibit exists in this FDD.
- **No loyalty / fuel / stored-value / delivery / marketplace exhibits**: Not applicable to the concept (preschool).
