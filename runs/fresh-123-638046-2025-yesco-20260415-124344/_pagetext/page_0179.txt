RHODE ISLAND ADDENDUM TO FRANCHISE DISCLOSURE DOCUMENT 
YESCO FRANCHISING LLC 
The following paragraph is added to the end of ITEM 17 of the disclosure document: 
19-28.1-14 of the Rhode Island Franchise Investment Act provides that “A provision in a franchise 
agreement restricting jurisdiction or venue to a forum outside this state or requiring the application of 
the laws of another state is void with respect to a claim otherwise enforceable under this Act.”
