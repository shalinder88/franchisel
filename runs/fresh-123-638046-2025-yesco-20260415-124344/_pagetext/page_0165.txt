Notwithstanding anything to the contrary set forth in the Franchise Disclosure Document, the 
following provisions shall supersede and apply to all franchises offered and sold in the state of Indiana.

paragraphs are added: 
1. 
No release, waiver or estoppel language set forth in this document or the Franchise 
Agreement will relieve us from liability imposed by the laws concerning franchising in the state of 
Indiana.  
2. 
Any provision in this Disclosure Document or in the Franchise Agreement which 
limits litigation brought for breach of the Agreement, including waiver of the right to a trial by jury, 
shall not be enforceable in the state of Indiana.
