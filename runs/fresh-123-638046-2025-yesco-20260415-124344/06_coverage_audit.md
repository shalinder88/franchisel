## Identified Gaps

### Material Gaps
1. **Franchise Agreement clause-by-clause depth**: Exhibit B is 96 pages. Key provisions surfaced via Item 17 table but detailed clause language (termination triggers, cure periods, default provisions, liquidated damages, force majeure) not yet individually extracted. **Severity: Medium** — key provisions are surfaced but detailed burden language would strengthen analysis.

### Minor Gaps
2. **Item 20 Table 3 state-by-state detail**: Full state-by-state franchised status table was read but data is extensive. All states covered.
3. **Franchisee list individual records**: Exhibit H names visible but not individually extracted (PII block on public output).

## Contradictions
1. **Outlet count (Item 19 vs Item 20 vs Financial Statements)**: Item 19 says 50 included out of 57 franchised. Item 20 says 57 franchised at end of 2024. Financial statement Note 2 says 57 franchised. These are consistent — the 50 vs 57 difference is due to the 7 exclusions documented in Item 19. **Status: Resolved.**
2. **Royalty revenue decline vs stable outlet count**: Royalty revenue fell from $3.37M (2023) to $3.21M (2024) despite unchanged franchised outlet count (57). This could reflect lower per-unit revenue, or different revenue recognition timing. Not explicitly explained in FDD. **Status: Unresolved — minor.**

## Open Questions
1. Loan to YESCO Administration LLC ($7.47M) — no repayment schedule disclosed. Represents 78% of total assets. What happens if affiliate cannot repay?
2. National Advertising Fund implementation timeline — "when implemented" but currently no US fund. What triggers establishment?
3. Business density data based on 2012 Census — now over 12 years old. How well does this reflect current territory potential?
