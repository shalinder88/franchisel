 New Generation Comparable Hotels 
 
Of the 220 Comparable Hotels described above, 47 were opened during or after 2015, indicating 
the most recent generation or ‘prototype’ of Embassy Suites Brand hotels (“New Generation 
Comparable Hotels”). Of these 47 New Generation Comparable Hotels, 3 were Company-
Managed and 44 were Franchisee-Managed. The financial performance results detailed in this 
section for 2023 and 2024 are for the 2024 New Generation Comparable Hotels. 
 
The following charts show Average Room Rate and Average Occupancy for New Generation 
Comparable Hotels and the number and percentage of Company-Managed and Franchisee-
Managed New Generation Comparable Hotels that met or exceeded the average. Average Room 
Rate and Average Occupancy are calculated based on information routinely reported to Hilton 
Worldwide by individual System Hotels.
