As of December 31, 2024, there were a total of 258 Embassy Suites branded hotels operating in 
the US. Of these 258 hotels, 220 were classified as Comparable Hotels. Of the 220 Comparable 
Hotels, 32 were Company-Managed and 188 were Franchisee-Managed. The financial 
performance results detailed in this section for 2023 and 2024 are for the 2024 Comparable 
Hotels, defined above to provide a year-over-year comparison.
