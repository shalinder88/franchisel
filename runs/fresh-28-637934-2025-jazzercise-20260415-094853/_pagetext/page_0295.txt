The Franchise Agreement provides that the Franchisee must sign a General Release in 
a form satisfactory to Jazzercise as a condition to renewal, transfer or a move to a new 
state.  Following is the form of Agreement and General Release that Jazzercise uses as 
of the date the Franchise Disclosure Document was issued.  It is subject to change at 
any time by Jazzercise without notice.
