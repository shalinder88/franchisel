ADDENDUM 
TO THE FRANCHISE AGREEMENT OF 
JAZZERCISE, INC. 
REQUIRED BY THE STATE OF NEW YORK 
 
The following language is added to Subparagraph 16J(1) of the Franchise Agreement 
for Class Owners and the Franchise Agreement for Business Owners and to 
Subparagraph 15J(1) of the Franchise Agreement for Associates: 
“However, this choice of law should not be considered a waiver of 
any rights conferred by the provisions of Article 33 of the New York 
State General Business Law.” 
The provisions of this Addendum only apply if the jurisdictional requirements of the New 
York Franchises Law are met independently without reference to this Addendum and to 
the extent they are then valid requirements of the statute.
