# Reader Report — Jazzercise, Inc. (637934-2025)

## Pass A: Executive Snapshot

1. **System:** Jazzercise is a dance fitness franchise system operating since 1982 (predecessor since 1969), with 5,251 franchised outlets at year-end 2024 and 0 company-owned outlets. System has been declining (5,844→5,251 over 2022–2024) but net loss nearly flattened in 2024 (−2 net).
2. **Entity:** Jazzercise, Inc. is a California corporation, sole shareholder Judi Sheppard Missett (founder). CEO is Shanna Missett Nelson (daughter). CFO Clarissa Zulick joined 2024. Family-controlled entity with no parents, no affiliates that franchise.
3. **Investment:** Associate franchisees: $2,170–$2,780. Class Owner/Business Owner: $3,935–$40,745. Initial franchise fee: $1,250 for all. Very low entry cost compared to typical franchise systems.
4. **Fee burden:** 20% royalty on gross member enrollment (10% for certain programs). Minimum continuing fee: $250/month (class owners, may rise to $500), $500/month (business owners, may rise to $1,000). Associate fee: $130/year. Performance royalties for music licensing (ASCAP/BMI/SESAC/GMR). Insurance: $245–$345/year. No advertising fund established; technology fee not yet established.
5. **Item 19:** No financial performance representation is made. Jazzercise explicitly declines to make any FPR.
6. **Financial health:** Revenue $20.53M (2024), $19.38M (2023), $19.63M (2022). Operating loss of $(86,965) in 2024 due to increased costs. Net income $419K (2024) saved by investment gains. Total assets $21.2M; total equity $14.1M. No debt. Cash $10.6M. Healthy balance sheet but thin operating margin trend.
7. **Auditor:** RSM US LLP — clean/unqualified opinion. No going concern.
8. **Territory:** Non-exclusive. Franchisor may license additional franchisees in your territory. No protected territory. Franchisor operates Jazzercise On Demand (online) and Revel Dance Fitness (another trademark).
9. **Renewal:** 5-year term, renewable for additional 5 years if conditions met. Must sign then-current agreement (may be materially different). Business owners have no right to renew.
10. **Noncompete:** None during term, none post-term. Unusual — no noncompete covenant at all.
11. **Dispute resolution:** Mediation then arbitration in Carlsbad, CA (franchisor HQ). California law governs. Subject to state-specific overrides.
12. **Litigation:** Two old state actions (NY 1987, WI 1985) — both resolved via consent decree/stipulation. No material current litigation.
13. **Special risks:** Out-of-state dispute resolution (CA venue). Declining system size. Family-controlled entity. Operating loss in 2024.

## Pass B: Front Matter, Fees, Investment (Items 1–7)

### Item 1 — The Franchisor
Jazzercise, Inc. is a California corporation incorporated September 26, 1979. Predecessor was Judi Sheppard Missett operating as sole proprietorship since 1969. No parents, no affiliates that franchise. HQ at 2460 Impala Drive, Carlsbad, CA 92010.

The franchise involves instructors conducting dance fitness classes. Three franchise types:
- **Class Owners:** Teach classes and own their classes. Sign Class Owner Franchise Agreement.
- **Associates:** Teach classes for other franchisees. Sign Associate Franchise Agreement.
- **Business Owners:** Offer classes but do not teach themselves; retain certified instructors. Sign Business Owner Franchise Agreement.

Additional programs: Junior Jazzercise (children), LO Jazzercise (low impact), Jazzercise On Demand (online streaming), Simply Plated (nutrition program).

Competitors: other dance fitness classes, boutique fitness studios, fitness clubs.

Social media policy: strict compliance required; violation may result in termination. Members' PII must not be copied, transferred, or provided to third parties.

## Pass C: Supplier Control, Operations, Training, Tech (Items 8–11)

### Item 8 — Restrictions on Sources
- **Routines:** Available only from Jazzercise (currently free, may charge in future). Delivered via video streaming.
- **Audio recordings:** Must purchase for each song; may use any source (iTunes, etc.). ~$200–$300/yr.
- **Jazzercise On Demand:** Must offer to members. Revenue sharing with franchisor.
- **Simply Plated nutrition program:** May offer; may be required in future. Revenue sharing.
- **Glofox:** Required third-party studio management platform. Franchisor may replace at any time.
- **Payment processing:** Must use designated vendor (currently Stripe). Processing fees apply.
- **OneTouchPoint (OTP):** Marketing platform. Base subscription $15/mo; email plan $25/mo additional. Not currently required but may be in future.
- **Insurance:** $1M comprehensive general liability required. May obtain through Jazzercise or another company. Jazzercise earned $605,956 from insurance program in 2024 (~3% of total revenue).
- **Lease provisions:** If leasing facility, lease must contain conditional assignment to Jazzercise, Jazzercise signage consent, notification of default, etc.
- **Jazzercise Apparel:** May purchase branded clothing/accessories for resale. No other approved suppliers. May not sell via Internet.
- Jazzercise apparel revenue: $603,993 in 2024 (~2.9% of revenue), with COGS averaging 50.5%.
- Estimated 85%+ of establishment/operating costs come from required purchases/leases.

Must use only Jazzercise-approved routines and class structure. May sell Jazzercise merchandise (not online). May not vary program or add unauthorized products/services. May engage in similar retail business if separate premises, not Jazzercise-identified, with prior consent, and sufficient insurance. Franchisor can change authorized goods/services via The Studio.

## Pass E: Contract Burden, Legal Mechanics (Item 17)

### Term and Renewal
- **Term:** 5 years
- **Renewal:** One additional 5-year term if conditions met. Business owners have no renewal right.
- **Renewal conditions:** Sign then-current Franchise Agreement (may be materially different), be in compliance, meet current requirements, complete training if required, sign release, no 3+ default notices in 24 months, Jazzercise hasn't withdrawn from market, right to continue at current location, no repeated trademark misuse. Does not apply if moved to another state.

### Termination
- **By franchisee:** Give 60 days' notice, be in good standing.
- **By franchisor without cause:** Not applicable.
- **By franchisor with cause — curable:** 5 days for nonpayment; up to 30 days for other noncompliance.
- **Non-curable defaults:** Bankruptcy, insolvency, abandonment, material misrepresentation, conduct reflecting unfavorably on Jazzercise, failure to obtain location consent within 3 months, failure to teach first class within 30 days, failure to open location within 3 months, failure to complete training within 6 months, repeated noncompliance, felony conviction, unauthorized transfer, termination of other Jazzercise agreement, eviction, seizure, unsatisfied $5,000+ judgment for 30 days, unauthorized IP use, failure to treat The Studio as confidential, noncompliance with law after 10 days' notice, intentional failure to report money, 3+ late payments in 12 months, unsatisfactory performance evaluations, imminent danger to public health/safety, SDN/blocked person, social media policy violation, not authorized to work in US, fraud, failure to submit local licensing reports within 7 days.

### Transfer
- Only to another franchisee with Jazzercise's consent.
- May transfer class to existing franchisee in territory by notifying franchise business advisor.
- Conditions: good standing, lessor consent, transferee completes training, meets qualifications, signs current agreement, franchisee signs release, transferee has licenses, transferee not SDN.
- **ROFR:** Jazzercise can match any offer.

### Death/Disability
- **Death:** Executor may sell within 90 days to qualified buyer; associate conducts classes meanwhile.
- **Disability:** Must secure associate; may not extend beyond 24 weeks.

### Noncompete
- **During term:** Not applicable.
- **Post-term:** Not applicable.
(Unusual — no noncompete at all.)

### Post-termination obligations
Pay all amounts due, discontinue trademarks, de-identify, return/destroy/delete all proprietary information, stop using phone numbers/email, assist in transition, refrain from soliciting members or personnel, refrain from disparaging remarks, obtain tail insurance.

### Dispute Resolution
- Mediation first, then arbitration if mediation unsuccessful (except certain claims).
- **Venue:** City of Jazzercise HQ (currently Carlsbad, CA). Subject to state law.
- **Governing law:** California law. Subject to state law.
- **Integration/merger clause:** Only terms of FA and Addendum binding. Nothing disclaims anything in FDD.

### Assignment by Franchisor
No restriction on Jazzercise's right to assign.

### Modification
No modification without writing signed by both parties, except Jazzercise may change policies on The Studio.
