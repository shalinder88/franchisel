## 7. Carrols Acquisition (Note 3)

### Transaction summary
- **Date:** May 16, 2024
- **Target:** Carrols Restaurant Group, Inc. -- largest BK franchisee
- **Pre-existing interest:** RBI held 15% equity interest (equity method)
- **Acquisition:** Remaining 85% for $9.55/share, all cash
- **Strategic rationale:** Accelerate reimaging of restaurants before refranchising to smaller franchise operations
- **Total consideration:** $648M (cash $543M + settlement of pre-existing balances $15M + fair value of existing 15% equity $90M)
- **Step acquisition gain:** $79M recognized on remeasurement of existing 15% interest
- **Carrols debt assumed and extinguished:** ~$431M, fully extinguished by June 30, 2024
- **Transaction costs:** ~$11M (professional fees, compensation)
- **Accounting:** Business combination, acquisition method

| Item | RBI | RBILP |
|---|---|---|

### RBILP condensed consolidating financials
Provided for Borrowers vs RBILP vs Eliminations, showing that substantially all debt sits at the Borrower entities level, and RBILP's own separate balance sheet is largely intercompany.
