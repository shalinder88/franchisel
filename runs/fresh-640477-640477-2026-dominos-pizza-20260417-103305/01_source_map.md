## Key Observations
- Text layer is complete across all 548 pages; no OCR needed.
- Exhibit B (franchisee list) is the largest exhibit spanning ~175 pages.
- Two sets of financial statements: DPF (Exhibit C, the franchisor) and DPL (Exhibit D, the servicer/parent).
- DPF is the legal franchisor but DPL provides all services under a management agreement.
- State addenda are embedded in the body (pp. 89–95), not in a separate exhibit. States covered: CA, HI, IL, MD, MN, WA (plus multi-state no-waiver).
- No special risks page content (page 5 is effectively blank — no state-mandated risks listed).
- Item 19 provides robust FPR with 5-year AWUS and EBITDA tiers by sales band.
