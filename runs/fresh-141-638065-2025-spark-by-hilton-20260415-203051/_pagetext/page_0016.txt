Senior Vice President, Global Owner Relations: Dianna Vaughan 
Ms. Vaughan has served as Senior Vice President, Global Owner Relations, since September 
2024. She previously served as Senior Vice President Brand Management, Americas from June 
2020 to September 2024. Prior, Ms. Vaughan served as Hilton’s Senior Vice President and Global

Vice President and Brand Leader – Spark by Hilton: Alissa Klees
