## Unresolveds
1. **Marketing fund transparency** (severity: medium) — Monthly Program Fee (4% GRR) not separately audited; no spending breakdown disclosed. Source: Item 6, p. 38.
2. **No FPR despite 5+ open hotels** (severity: low) — Understandable for small system but leaves prospective franchisees without performance benchmarks. Source: Item 19, p. 88.
3. **Early-stage brand risk** (severity: medium) — Only 5 open hotels after 6+ years. Pipeline of 8 is modest. Brand viability depends on Hilton support. Source: Item 20, p. 88-90.
4. **Restricted Area Provision opacity** (severity: low) — No disclosure of what % of franchisees receive one or typical scope. Source: Item 12, p. 68-69.
5. **Hilton debt guarantee** (severity: high) — Entity guarantees parent debt maturing 2025-2033; all assets pledged. Source: Note 7, p. 109.
6. **Subsequent events date discrepancy** (severity: low) — Note 9 says "March 18, 2024" which appears to be a typo for 2025. Source: Note 9, p. 109.
7. **Gag clause** (severity: medium) — Some franchisees restricted from speaking about experience. Source: Item 20, p. 90.

## Contradictions
1. **Item 6 Note 22 / brand newness:** Item 7 Note 22 states "because it is a new brand, we do not yet have actual investment information for a Motto by Hilton hotel" — yet the brand has been franchising since 2018 and has 5 open hotels. The "new brand" language may be outdated boilerplate. Source: p. 44.
