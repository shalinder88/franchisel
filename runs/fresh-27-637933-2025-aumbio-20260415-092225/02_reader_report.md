## Pass A — Executive Snapshot

1. **AumBio Franchising, LLC** is an Illinois LLC formed August 28, 2023, offering franchises for alternative holistic wellness centers since October 17, 2023. The franchisor does not itself operate any centers; its predecessor/affiliate **Aum-Bio, Inc.** (formed July 2, 2003) operates 3 company-owned centers in Illinois.
2. **System size: Zero franchised outlets.** No franchised units have ever opened. The system consists solely of 3 company-owned centers operated by the predecessor affiliate. One franchise agreement was signed (Table 3 shows 1 franchised outlet at start of 2024) but no franchised outlet opened.
3. **Sole principal:** Kamil Czubacki, President since August 2023, also President of Aum-Bio, Inc. since 2010. He is the only individual disclosed in Item 2.
4. **Total initial investment:** $159,320 to $267,620 (single unit); $234,320 to $342,620 (3-unit MUDA). Initial franchise fee is $50,000.
5. **Ongoing fee burden:** 6% royalty on Gross Revenues + 1% Brand Fund + $100/month technology fee + $1,500/month minimum local marketing spend. Effective ongoing load is approximately 7% of revenue plus $1,600/month in fixed fees.
6. **No Financial Performance Representation (Item 19).** The franchisor makes no earnings claims.
7. **Financial condition is a critical risk.** The franchisor has **zero revenue**, total assets of $0 (2024), a bank overdraft of $9, and a net loss of $97 in 2024. Members' equity is in deficit at ($9). The Illinois Attorney General imposed a fee deferral requirement due to the franchisor's financial condition.
8. **Regulatory action:** An Agreed Order to Cease and Desist was entered April 16, 2024 by the Illinois DFPR against Aum-Bio, Inc. and Kamil Czubacki for marketing health-related claims not supported by medical literature.
9. **Territory:** Exclusive, not more than 1 center per 100,000 population. Franchisor may not solicit or accept orders within the franchisee's territory.
10. **Term:** 10 years, with two 5-year renewals. Renewal requires signing the then-current franchise agreement, which may be "materially different."
11. **Noncompete:** During term — no competing business anywhere in the U.S. Post-term — 2 years within the territory or 50 miles of the center.
12. **Dispute resolution:** Mediation and arbitration in Illinois (JAMS Commercial Arbitration Rules). Illinois law governs.
13. **Special risks:** Out-of-state dispute resolution, short operating history, questionable financial condition. Virginia addendum adds: "THIS IS A DEVELOPMENT STAGE COMPANY WHICH ENTAILS ADDITIONAL RISK OF FINANCIAL LOSS."
14. **Equipment dependency:** Core business relies on THERA WELLNESS therapy devices (FDA "General Wellness Product," not a medical device) and Oberon consultation devices. Franchisor receives 10-15% on THERA WELLNESS purchases and 60% on Oberon purchases.

## Pass E — Contract Burden, Legal Mechanics (Item 17)

### Term and Renewal
- **Initial term:** 10 years from FA date.
- **Renewal:** Two (2) additional 5-year terms if franchisor is still franchising and franchisee is in good standing. Must sign **then-current franchise agreement, which may be materially different.**
- **Renewal requirements:** Timely notice, sign new agreement, general release, payment of fee.

### Termination
- **By franchisee:** 60 days' notice of franchisor's default, if uncured.
- **By franchisor without cause:** Not applicable.
- **By franchisor with cause (curable):** Failure to pay (5-day cure), failure to obtain signed covenants (5 days), failure to procure insurance, lease default, loss of possession, failure to open within 120 days, breach of any covenant/warranty not listed as non-curable.
- **By franchisor (non-curable):** Misrepresentation, false books/records, failure to submit reports, failure to pay fees, 2+ failing inspections in 12 months, unauthorized products/services, abandonment, felony conviction, trademark misuse, violation of covenants, unapproved transfers, failure to complete training, repeated defaults (even if cured), fraud, bankruptcy/insolvency, selling sound-wave equipment at wholesale/retail.
- **Cross-default:** Default under one agreement may result in termination of all agreements.

### Transfer
- No transfer without prior written consent (not unreasonably withheld).
- **Transfer fee:** 50% of then-current IFF (includes initial training for transferee).
- **Conditions:** New franchisee qualifies, all amounts paid, transferee not in competitive business, training completed, signs then-current FA, pays training fee, franchisee signs release, guaranty signed, non-compete agreed.
- **No right of first refusal or option to purchase by franchisor.**
- **Death/disability:** Representative must transfer within 6 months (same conditions as any transfer).

### Noncompete
- **During term:** No competing business anywhere in the U.S. No diversion of business.
- **Post-term:** 2 years, within the territory or within any territory where an AumBio center is located at time of signing, or within 50 miles of franchisee's location at termination/expiration.
- **Non-solicitation:** 2 years post-term on clients of the center.
- Applies to franchisee, all stockholders/members/partners, guarantor, and their spouses and children.

### Dispute Resolution
- **Mediation and arbitration** required (except for franchisor actions for monies owed, injunctive/extraordinary relief, or real estate).
- **Forum:** City where franchisor headquarters is located (Rolling Meadows, IL).
- **Rules:** JAMS Commercial Arbitration Rules.
- **Governing law:** Illinois (except federal law). Subject to state law overrides.

### Post-Termination Obligations
- Pay outstanding amounts, de-identify, return confidential information, cancel assumed name registrations, cease use of advertising materials, comply with all post-termination covenants.

### Liquidated Damages
- If franchisee terminates without right or franchisor terminates for default: royalty + Brand Fund for lesser of 2 years or remaining term. Calculated as average monthly royalty for prior 12 months x 24 months.
