## Special Risk Flags (from page 5)

1. **Out-of-State Dispute Resolution** — Mediation/arbitration/litigation only in Illinois.
2. **Short Operating History** — Franchisor at early stage of development; limited operating history. Riskier investment.
3. **Financial Condition** — Franchisor's financial condition calls into question its ability to provide services and support.

## Key Structural Notes

- **No FPR:** Item 19 explicitly states no financial performance representations are made.
- **No franchisees:** Zero franchised outlets in any year (2022-2024). One franchisee listed in Table 3 for 2024 start but 0 opened.
- **Predecessor:** Aum-Bio, Inc. (formed July 2, 2003) owns 3 company-owned holistic wellness centers in IL.
- **Financial statements are image-only** in the PDF; text extraction failed. Rendered via pdftoppm for reading.
- **Illinois fee deferral:** Initial franchise fees deferred until franchisor meets initial obligations and franchisee opens for business — imposed by IL Attorney General due to franchisor's financial condition.
- **Regulatory risk:** Ceased and desisted by IL DFPR (2016-3225, agreed order April 16, 2024) regarding unapproved health claims.
- **THERA WELLNESS device:** Core equipment is marketed as "General Wellness Product" per FDA — not a medical device. Misrepresenting as medical is grounds for termination.
