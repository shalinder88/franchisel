Multi-Unit Development 
If you and we agree that you will develop multiple franchises, then you will sign our Multi-Unit 
Development Agreement (“MUDA”) in the form of Exhibit C to this disclosure document.  Instead of an 
Initial Franchise Fee, you will pay a fee (the “Development Fee”) based on the number of franchises you 
will develop, as follows: (i) first franchise - $50,000; (ii) two franchises - $90,000; and (iii) three franchises 
- $125,000. You will pay the total Development Fee upon signing the MUDA and the first Franchise 
Agreement. The Development Fee is not refundable. 
The Initial Franchise Fee is fully earned by us on signing the Franchise Agreement and is not 
refundable under any circumstances.
