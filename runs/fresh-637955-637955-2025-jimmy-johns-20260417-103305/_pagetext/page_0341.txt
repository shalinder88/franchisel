RELATED-PARTY TRANSACTIONS
Pursuant to the terms of a management agreement, an affiliate, Jimmy John's Franchise, LLC, manages the assets of the
Company and performs certain franchising, marketing intellectual property, reporting, operating and support services to
franchisees on behalf of the Company. In exchange for these services, management fees incurred are presented within
“Management fee to affiliate” in the Company’s statements of income. Amounts payable under the management agreement
are presented within "Accounts payable to affiliates" in the Company's balance sheets. As a result of the related party
management agreement, results of operations of the Company may not be indicative of those that would have been achieved
had the Company operated on a stand-alone basis.
