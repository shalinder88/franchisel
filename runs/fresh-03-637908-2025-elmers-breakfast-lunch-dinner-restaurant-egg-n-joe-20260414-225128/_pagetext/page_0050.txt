Section 6.A in Area 
Development Agreement 
Franchisor must have paid all Royalty and Brand Promotion Fund 
contributions and other amounts owed; Franchisor may not have 
violated any provision of the Franchise Agreement, the Lease or 
any other agreement during the 60-day period before requesting 
consent and between the request and the effective date of the 
transfer; transferor must provide Franchisor all information or 
documents Franchisor requests regarding the transfer, the 
transferee, and its owners; transferee may not have an interest in or 
perform services for a Competitive Business; transferee must 
complete training to our satisfaction; landlord must permit transfer 
or sublease of Lease; transferee agrees to upgrade Restaurant 
within 120 days; transferee must sign current form of franchise 
agreement and related documents; Franchisee or transferee must 
pay a transfer fee; Franchisee must sign a general release; 
Franchisor must approve purchase price, debt and payment terms 
and, if the price is being financed, the obligations must be 
subordinate to the transferee’s obligation to pay Royalty, Brand 
Promotion Fund contributions and other amounts; Franchisee will 
not engage in any proscribed activities; Franchisee will not identify 
itself as a current or former Restaurant. 
 
Franchisee must submit an application requesting consent to the 
transfer and providing information about the transferee, its owners, 
and the transfer; transferee must satisfy application and 
certification requirements; Franchisee must provide executed 
versions of any documents between Franchisee and transferee; 
Franchisee and transferee must sign Franchisor’s then-current 
consent to transfer agreement, which includes a general release and 
a non-competition covenant; all representations and warranties 
made in the Consent to Transfer must be true; Franchisee and 
transferee may not have violated any provision of the Area 
Development Agreement, Consent to Transfer or any other 
document during the 60-day period before requesting consent and 
between the request and the effective date of the transfer; all 
persons required to complete training must complete training; 
transferee must sign Franchisor’s then-current form of area 
development agreement and related documents; Franchisee must 
pay transfer fee of $2,500; Franchisee must pay Franchisor’s 
expenses incurred in connection with the sale up to a maximum of 
$3,000 plus disbursements and taxes; Franchisee must pay an 
amount equal to the greater of 4% of the purchase price or $20,000; 
Franchisor must provide evidence that appropriate measures have 
been taken to effect the transfer.
