Non-curable defaults under the Franchise Agreement include: 
material misrepresentations or omissions; failure to secure site for 
Premises within 180; failure to open Restaurant on time; 
insufficient number of Designated Managers complete initial 
training; failure to operate Restaurant for more than 3 days or other 
abandonment; conviction of a felony; engage in conduct which 
adversely affects reputation or the Marks; misuse of the Marks; 
unauthorized transfer; loss of right to occupy Premises; 
unauthorized use or disclosure of any Confidential Information; 
failure to pay taxes when due unless contesting in good faith; 
underestimate Gross Sales 3 or more times during the Term; failure 
on 3 or more occasions within any 12 month consecutive period to 
comply with the Agreement or fail on 2 or more occasions within 
any 6 consecutive month period; assignment for the benefit of 
creditors or admit insolvency in writing; file for bankruptcy; failure 
to comply with anti-terrorism laws; allow to exist any condition 
that presents a health or safety concern; failure to pay any third-
party, including the lessor of your premises, any amounts owed in 
connection with your Restaurant when due.

Section 7.B of Area

Under the Franchise Agreement, Franchisee must: pay Franchisor 
within 15 days all amounts due, including lost revenue damages, if 
applicable; close the Restaurant and cease conducting business 
under the Marks; not identify yourself or any business as a current 
or former franchisee or Restaurant and cancel all fictitious names 
relating to use of the Marks; deliver to Franchisor within 30 days 
all materials containing any Mark; de-identify the Restaurant; cease 
using any Online Presence, telephone number, internet listings and 
other contact identifier and either disable or instruct the registrar to 
transfer them to us or our designee; comply with all other System 
Standards in connection with de-identification and disposing of 
personal information; give evidence of compliance within 30 days; 
cease using Confidential Information; and comply with non-
competition provisions. 
 
 
Under the Area Development Agreement, Franchisee must: cease 
conducting business granted under the Area Development 
Agreement; cease holding itself out as a developer of Restaurants; 
deliver all documents provided pursuant to the Area Development 
Agreement; cease using all telephone numbers and listing services; 
pay all amounts owed; comply with non-competition provisions.
