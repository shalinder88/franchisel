# Reader Report — Paul Davis Restoration, Inc. (637929-2025)

## Pass A: Executive Snapshot

1. **System**: Paul Davis Restoration, Inc. (PDRI), a Florida corporation incorporated in 1967, operates an insurance restoration and general contracting franchise system. As of December 31, 2024, PDRI had 272 franchises (266 operating) in the U.S. plus 26 Paul Davis Emergency Services franchises (all operating) and 64 Canadian Paul Davis Systems franchises (63 operating).
2. **Parent chain**: PDRI → FSB Holdings, Inc. (DE) → FS Brands, Inc. (DE) → FirstService CAM Holdings, Inc. (DE) → FirstService Corporation (Ontario, Canada — publicly traded).
3. **Affiliates**: California Closet Company, CertaPro Painters, Floorcoverings International, Pillar To Post, Paul Davis Restoration Inc. (Canada), Tele-Link Services.
4. **Investment range**: $298,800 to $804,900 total, including franchise fee of $65,000–$208,000 ($0.26/person × territory population, max 800,000). Minimum working capital of $500,000 recommended.
5. **Fee burden**: 4% royalty on Gross Sales; minimum royalty escalating to $6.00/person by year 9; SMP fee of $500/month + 0.75% of invoiced sales; guarantee fund 0.5% until $25,000; indemnity fund 0.10%–0.04% tiered; software fees ~$600+/month; call center fees; and numerous technology/software license fees.
6. **Item 19 highlights (2024, 2+ year franchises)**: Total reported gross sales $1,099,240,532 across 183 franchises. Median $3,976,094; Average $6,006,779. Only 30% (55/183) attained or surpassed the average. Range: $83,873 to $55,516,949.
7. **Item 19 highlights (2024, <2 year franchises)**: Total $79,430,092 across 45 franchises. Median $1,006,593; Average $1,765,113. 31% (14/45) surpassed average. Range: $6,899 to $10,557,726.
8. **Growth trajectory**: Strong net growth — franchised outlets went from 211→224→245→266 over 2022–2024 (+55 net in 3 years). 28 new openings in 2024 against only 6 terminations and 1 non-renewal.
9. **Territory**: Non-exclusive. Zip-code defined, 500K–800K population typical. PDRI reserves rights for national accounts, alternative distribution, M&A, and affiliate competition.
10. **Renewal**: 5-year initial term, renewable for additional 5 years. Must sign then-current FA (may have materially different terms). Must meet minimum sales thresholds.
11. **Dispute resolution**: Binding arbitration under PDRI's internal arbitration system; choice of law Florida; arbitration forum selected by Arbitration Committee (franchisee-operated).
12. **Special risks**: (1) Out-of-state arbitration, (2) Personal guaranty required for all owners.
13. **Financial health (FS Brands)**: Revenue $850.4M (2024), up from $782.2M (2023). Net income $60.1M (2024). Total assets $591.2M. No external debt. Cash $82.5M. $30M dividend paid in 2024. PwC clean opinion.
14. **Confidentiality clause flag**: Some franchisees have signed confidentiality clauses restricting their ability to speak about their experience.

## Pass E: Contract Burden, Legal Mechanics (Item 17)

### Renewal
- 5-year initial term; 5-year renewal.
- Renewal conditions: 90–180 days written notice; sign then-current FA (may have **materially different** terms); bring equipment to current standards; compliance throughout term; minimum sales for each of prior 2 years = greater of national median or $6.00 × territory population; meet minimum royalty; pay $5,000 renewal fee; execute renewal within 90 days of presentation.

### Termination
- **By franchisee**: Not applicable (no provision).
- **By franchisor without cause**: Not applicable.
- **Curable defaults** (15 days to cure): Nonpayment of fees, failure to pay suppliers, failure to comply with Operations Manual, failure to maintain licenses/permits.
- **Non-curable defaults** (immediate termination): Failure to timely begin operations; material/repeated misrepresentation of gross sales; insolvency; unapproved transfer; felony conviction; 3+ defaults in 12 months; abandonment (including failure to report sales for 2+ months); disclosure of confidential information; non-renewal of FA.

### Transfer
- PDRI must approve all transfers (same criteria as new franchisees).
- Conditions: Qualified transferee; transfer fee ($10K majority, $5K minority); 2.5% resale fee (capped $40K); training; new FA at current terms; equipment upgrade; accounts settled.
- PDRI right of first refusal at same terms as third-party offer.
- On termination: PDRI right to purchase at fair market value.
- Death/disability: Heir can inherit if approval conditions met.

### Non-Competition
- **During term**: Cannot participate in competing business, disrupt PDRI/affiliate employment, or divert business.
- **Post-term**: 2 years, within protected territory and other PDRI territories. Cannot contact former suppliers/customers for competitive purposes.

### Dispute Resolution
- All disputes submitted to **binding arbitration** under PDRI's internal arbitration system (arbitrators are franchisees).
- Forum selected by Arbitration Committee.
- Subject to state law, disputes may not be litigated in court.
- Choice of law: Florida (subject to state law).
- Integration/merger clause: Only FA terms binding; representations outside FDD/FA unenforceable.
