 
 
PAUL DAVIS RESTORATION, INC. 
MARYLAND ADDENDUM TO THE FRANCHISE AGREEMENT 
 
THE FRANCHISE AGREEMENT TO WHICH THIS ADDENDUM IS ATTACHED AND 
INCORPORATED IS HEREBY AMENDED AS FOLLOWS: 
 
1. Despite anything to the contrary contained in the Franchise Agreement, the general 
release required as a condition of the resale of an existing franchise by a franchisee shall 
not apply to any liability under the Maryland Franchise Registration and Disclosure Law. 
 
2. Despite the provisions of Article 29 and Article 23, the Franchisee may sue in the State 
of Maryland for claims arising under the Maryland Franchise Registration and Disclosure 
Law. 
 
3. The acknowledgements and representations contained in the Franchise Agreement 
are not intended to nor shall they act as a release, estoppel or waiver of any liability 
incurred by PDRI under the Maryland Franchise Registration and Disclosure Law. 
 
The undersigned hereby acknowledge and agree that this addendum is hereby 
made part of and incorporated into the foregoing Franchise Agreement.
