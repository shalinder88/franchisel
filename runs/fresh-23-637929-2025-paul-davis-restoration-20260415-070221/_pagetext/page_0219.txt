The following are revisions to Item 4 of the disclosure document: 
 
Neither PDRI, nor its affiliates, officers, or directors during the 10 year period immediately 
preceding the date of the offering prospectus have (a) filed as debtor (or had filed against 
it) a petition to start an action under the U.S. Bankruptcy Code; (b) obtained a discharge 
of its debts under the bankruptcy code; or (c) was a principal officer of a company or a 
general partner in a partnership that either filed as a debtor (or had filed against it) a 
petition to start an action under the U.S. Bankruptcy Code or that obtained a discharge of 
its debts under the U.S. Bankruptcy Code during or within one (1) year after the officer or 
general partner of the franchisor held this position in the company or partnership.
