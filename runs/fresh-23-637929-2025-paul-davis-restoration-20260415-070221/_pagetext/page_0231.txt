WISCONSIN ADDENDUM TO THE FRANCHISE AGREEMENT 
 
ALL FRANCHISE AGREEMENTS EXECUTED IN AND OPERATIVE WITHIN THE 
STATE OF WISCONSIN ARE HEREBY AMENDED AS FOLLOWS: 
 
The Franchisor and Franchisee hereby acknowledge that the Franchise Agreement shall 
be governed by The Wisconsin Fair Dealership Law (Wisconsin Statutes, 1979-1980, 
Title XIV-A, Chapter 135, Sections 135.01 through 135.07) which makes it unlawful for a 
franchisor to terminate, cancel or fail to renew a franchise without good cause, as well as 
providing other protections and rights to the franchisee. To the extent anything in the 
Franchise Agreement is contrary to the laws in the State of Wisconsin, said laws shall 
prevail. 
 
The undersigned hereby acknowledge and agree that this addendum is hereby made part 
of and incorporated into the foregoing Franchise Agreement.
