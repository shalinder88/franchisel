of 500,000.  All fees paid to PDRI are not refundable unless otherwise indicated.  You should be 
aware that fees paid to third parties may not be refundable.  We note that the higher range estimated

such as California, or a large urban market.   Your result will vary depending on the geographic 
location of your business.  
 
(1)  
For territories, the franchise fee is $0.26/person of population. The franchise fee is payable 
with at least 50% in cash upon signing the franchise agreement and the balance may be financed 
in the form of a four-year promissory note with an APR of 7%. See Item 10 for more on financing. 
PDRI does not set a maximum franchise fee, as the franchise fee is based on the size of the 
population in the territory.  However, PDRI does not sell a single franchise unit with a territory 
having a population in excess of 800,000. PDRI reduces the initial franchisee fee by 25% for 
qualified veterans. In addition, for VetFran approved franchisees, PDRI may finance up to 67% of 
the reduced initial franchise fee for approved franchisees, as described in Item 10 of this disclosure 
document.
