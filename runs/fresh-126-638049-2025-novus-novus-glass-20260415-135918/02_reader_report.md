# Reader Report — Novus Franchising 2 LLC (638049-2025)

## Pass A: Executive Snapshot

1. **Franchisor:** Novus Franchising 2 LLC, a Delaware LLC formed April 13, 2017, HQ in St. Paul, MN. Wholly owned by Mondofix USA LLC → Mondofix Inc. (Canadian parent, Blainville, QC).
2. **Predecessor:** Novus Franchising, Inc. (NFI), which operated the Novus Glass system from 1993 until May 2017 acquisition.
3. **Concept:** Retail and mobile automotive glass repair and replacement franchise under the "Novus Glass" brand. Also offers residential and commercial glass services.
4. **System size:** 128 franchised outlets as of 12/31/2024, 0 company-owned. Down from 151 in 2022 (significant 2023 decline of -23 units due to 18 non-renewals, largely in Minnesota).
5. **Investment range:** Retail: $90,000–$284,590; Mobile: $69,500–$169,390. Initial franchise fee: $10,500.
6. **Fee burden:** 6% royalty (or minimum monthly $350/$500), 2% marketing contribution, 4% minimum local advertising = 12%+ effective fee load on gross revenues.
7. **Mandatory minimum payments:** Minimum monthly royalty fees apply regardless of sales levels — flagged as a special risk on page 4.
8. **Item 19:** Gross sales only (no cost data). Mobile 1-unit average: $427,168 (median $276,856, n=33). Retail 1-unit average: $791,251 (median $702,787, n=21). Multi-unit retail (2 units): average $1,501,431 (n=12).
9. **Financial health (FY2024):** Revenue $6.44M, net income $800K, total assets $3.24M, net membership interest $1.22M. Audited by Ernst & Young, unqualified opinion. Distributions to parent: $500K (2024), $1.4M (2023).
10. **Term:** 10 years with re-franchise option (which may have materially different terms). ProColor and 5-Year NAPA Co-Location options reduce to 5 years.
11. **Territory:** Protected for Novus glass repair/replacement using the Marks, but subject to existing franchisee/licensee rights, Speedy stores, TAG Network referrals, and broad reservation of rights by franchisor.
12. **Disputes:** Litigation in Minnesota (Ramsey County). Mediation required first, location at least 200 miles from either party's offices. Flagged as special risk for out-of-state franchisees.
13. **Post-term noncompete:** 2 years, within territory or 10 miles of any Novus business. Subject to state law overrides.
14. **Confidentiality clause alert:** Item 20 discloses that franchisees/former franchisees have signed confidentiality clauses restricting their ability to speak openly.
15. **Vehicle leasing program:** Mandatory lease from franchisor at $1,300–$1,600/month, 60-month term. Franchisor retains all ownership. Delay addendum available at franchisor's sole discretion.

## Pass C: Supplier Control, Operations, Training, Tech (Items 8–11)

### Item 8 — Supplier Restrictions
- All vehicles, equipment, insurance, fixtures, supplies, inventory must meet Novus specs
- **Sole-source items:** Resins (from Novus), windshield repair bridges/equipment (from Novus 2), urethane adhesives (from SRP/Novus 2 affiliate), vehicle insurance (designated provider), POS software
- Alternative supplier requests allowed for non-sole-source items; $250 minimum approval fee; ~30 day turnaround
- Approximately 90% of initial purchases and 50% of ongoing annual expenses must meet specs
- Franchisor earns profit on purchases: $1,263,967 in 2024 from franchisee purchases (19.6% of total revenue). Novus 2 earned additional $212,714.
- Buying program provides rebates of 0%–11% of price; franchisor passes part back to franchisees quarterly. Program may be changed/eliminated at any time.

### Item 9 — Franchisee's Obligations
- Standard 24-obligation table with cross-references
- Personal guaranty required from all owners of entity franchisees
- Guaranty extends to confidentiality and noncompete provisions

## Pass D: Territory, Channels, Encroachment (Items 12–16)

### Item 12 — Territory
- Designated territory consisting of zip codes; typically ~120,000+ vehicles
- Protected for Novus glass repair/replacement businesses using the Marks
- **Major carve-outs:**
  - Prior existing franchisees/licensees (including renewals and transfers)
  - Speedy Auto Glass stores (operated by affiliate Speedy Novus Glass)
  - TAG Network referrals to non-Novus businesses
  - Novus 2 product sales to competitors (non-Novus branded)
  - Any acquisition, merger, or affiliation by franchisor
  - National/regional accounts if franchisee fails to service them
- No right to conduct business or solicit outside territory without consent
- Franchisor may NOT unilaterally change territory (franchisee may request change for $2,500)
- Quarterly Minimum Gross Revenue thresholds required ($40,000 annual for Repair Only)
- No exclusive territory statement: "You will not receive an exclusive territory. You may face competition from other franchisees, from outlets that we own, or from other channels of distribution or competitive brands that we control."

### Item 13 — Trademarks
- 21 registered marks owned by Mondofix, licensed to Novus under perpetual license
- Key marks: NOVUS, NOVUS GLASS, NOVUS GLASS REPAIR & REPLACEMENT, CRACK MOUSE
- If Mondofix terminates license to Novus, Mondofix will honor franchisee's license directly (if not in default)

### Item 14 — Patents, Copyrights, Proprietary Information
- 12 patents/applications owned by Mondofix (scratch removal, crack curing, windshield repair, LED curing)
- Same perpetual license structure with Mondofix honoring franchisee rights on termination
- **AI restriction:** Explicit prohibition on using confidential information for machine learning, AI training, or input into generative AI platforms without written consent

### Item 15 — Participation
- Full-time devotion required by owner or manager
- Owner/manager must complete training before operating
- At least 1 full-time employee performing/marketing glass services
- At least 50% of employees must complete training programs
- All vehicle drivers must have valid license and meet insurance/requirements

### Item 16 — Restrictions on Sales
- Cannot operate competing business during term
- Must sell all required products/services; franchisor can add requirements without limit
- Cannot perform services outside territory without consent
- Cannot resell Novus resin products or repair equipment

## Pass E: Contract Burden, Legal Mechanics (Item 17)

### Term and Renewal
- **Term:** 10 years (5 years with ProColor Amendment or 5-Year NAPA Co-Location)
- **Re-franchise (renewal):** Sign new franchise agreement that may contain "materially different" terms; 210 days' notice required; pay re-franchise fee ($4,000); modernize business; satisfy all obligations
- **No renewal of Area Development Agreement**
- **Materially different terms risk:** Explicit — new agreement at renewal may have materially different terms

### Transfer
- Transfer fee: $7,500 ($5,000 for existing franchisees)
- Franchisor right of first refusal before any sale
- Conditions: pay all debts, transferee meets standards, signs current form agreement, completes training, not a competitor
- Must sign general release (subject to state law)
- Vehicle leases: cannot be transferred at all

### Termination
- **By franchisee:** Only if franchisor materially breaches and fails to cure (subject to state law)
- **By franchisor without cause:** Not permitted (except Vehicle Lease subordination to master lease)
- **Curable defaults (30 days):** Training completion failure, failure to open within 6 months, material breach, failure to pay (15 days for payment defaults)
- **Non-curable defaults:** Felony conviction, insolvency/bankruptcy, abandonment, material trademark impairment (24 hrs), failure to meet Annual Minimum Gross Revenues (Repair Only), withholding access to financial systems, revoking EFT authorization
- **Quarterly Minimum Gross Revenue:** 90 days to cure shortfall
- **Liquidated damages on abandonment:** 60 months × average monthly gross revenues

### Post-Termination
- Cease use of all Marks
- Alter business location and vehicles to distinguish from Novus
- Provide customer list to franchisor
- Return all materials
- Franchisor may take phone numbers, directory listings, domain names, websites, social media accounts
- Area Development: $10,000 per undeveloped franchise as liquidated damages

### Death/Disability
- Heirs can assume rights but must meet transfer requirements

### Noncompete
- **During term:** Cannot participate in any competing business (subject to state law)
- **Post-term:** 2 years within territory, territory of another franchisee, or 10 miles of any Novus business (subject to state law)

### Dispute Resolution
- **Mediation required first** (non-binding), at location at least 200 miles from either party's offices
- **Litigation:** Ramsey County, Minnesota (subject to state law overrides)
- **Governing law:** Minnesota (subject to state law)
- **Jury trial waiver:** Not specifically mentioned in Item 17 summary
- **Class action waiver:** Not specifically mentioned in Item 17 summary
