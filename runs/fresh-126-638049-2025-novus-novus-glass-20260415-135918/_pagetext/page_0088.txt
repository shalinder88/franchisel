Sec. 19-28.1-14 of the Rhode Island Franchise Investment Act provides that any provision 
of the Franchise Agreement which restricts jurisdiction or venue to a forum outside Rhode Island 
is void with respect to a claim otherwise enforceable under the Rhode Island Franchise Investment 
Act.
