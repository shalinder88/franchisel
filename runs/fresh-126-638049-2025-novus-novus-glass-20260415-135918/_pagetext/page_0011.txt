franchisees in various parts of the world since May 2001.  As of December 31, 2024, there were

Fix Auto Canada Inc. and its predecessors have franchised the Fix Auto Body system in Canada 
since 1992.  The principal address for Fix Auto Canada Inc. is the same as the principal address
