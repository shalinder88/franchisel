5-YEAR NAPA CO-LOCATION ADDENDUM TO 
NOVUS® FRANCHISE AGREEMENT 
This 5-Year NAPA Co-Location Addendum (this “Addendum”) is entered into as of the ____ day 
of _______, 20___, by and between Novus Franchising 2 LLC, a Delaware limited liability company (the 
“Franchisor”, “our”, “us” or “we”) and _________________, a _______________________________ 
(the “Franchisee”, “you” or “your”). 
INTRODUCTION
WHEREAS, you are signing a Novus Glass Franchise Agreement (the “Franchise Agreement”) 
for the operation of a Novus® retail business located at ________________________ (defined as the 
“Business” in the Franchise Agreement) contemporaneously with the signing of this Addendum. 
WHEREAS, we and National Automotive Parts Association, a Georgia corporation (“NAPA”) 
have entered into a Master Promotion Agreement (“MPA”) to promote the Novus® retail franchise 
offering to certain operators of NAPA Auto Care Service Centers located in the United States. 
WHEREAS, the Business will be operated in connection with the operation of other business(es) 
offering other automotive-related goods and services (in conjunction with the “NAPA Auto Care” 
designation and program), certain provisions of the Franchise Agreement will not be applicable to the 
Business and its operation, and certain other provisions need to be added to the Franchise Agreement to 
govern the Business and its operation. 
NOW, THEREFORE, in light of this Introduction and in consideration of the mutual promises set 
forth in this Addendum, you and we agree as follows: 
1.
Definitions.  Capitalized terms used but not defined in this Addendum have the meaning given 
them in the Franchise Agreement. 
2.
Your Related Parties.  The term “Your Related Parties” shall mean all of your affiliates, and 
other related individuals and entities that, either directly or indirectly, own, are owned by, or share 
common ownership interest with you.  For purposes of this Addendum, the term Your Related 
Parties is meant to include, without limitation, those individuals and entities that will operate 
Other Businesses (as defined below). 
3.
Term. Section 3.1 of the Franchise Agreement is hereby deleted and replaced with the following: 
3.1 Term.  The term of this Agreement will be for 5 years (the 
“Term”) and will begin on the date of this Agreement and end on the 
date that is 5 years from the date of this Agreement (the “Expiration 
Date”). 
4.
Your Option to Re-Franchise. Section 3.2 of the Franchise Agreement is hereby modified to 
replace “one additional 10-year term” with “one additional 5-year term”.
