Initial Marketing Start-Up Package 
We will prepare an initial marketing start-up package for you.  It will include an initial direct mail marketing 
campaign, an initial fleet and commercial account prospect list, and a local area search marketing program.  
As part of this package, we will also establish a home page for you on our website.  The cost for this 
package is $7,000, or $4,000 if you sign the ProColor Amendment to your Franchise Agreement.  Part of 
the cost of this package will be forwarded to our designated digital marketing supplier to set up a minimum 
media spend with them for the first 6 months of the operation of your Novus® business.  These fees are due 
when you sign your Franchise Agreement.  There may also be a monthly hosting fee for your home page. 
(See Item 6 for additional information on the hosting fee and the referral website fees.).  These fees are 
nonrefundable.   
Software Conversion Fee 
If you are already operating a Novus® business and therefore are signing a Re-Franchise Addendum, you 
may also need to pay $350 to convert your software from your existing software program to our new one.  
This fee is due when you sign the Re-Franchise Addendum and is not refundable.
