reduced Initial Franchise Fee of $2,500.  If you sign the 5-Year NAPA Co-Location Addendum to 
your Franchise Agreement, you will pay us a reduced Initial Franchise Fee of $7,000.  If purchasing 
a second Novus® business in a different territory than your current Novus® business, the Initial 
Franchise Fee will be reduced to $5,250, or if purchasing a second Novus® business in the same 
territory as your current Novus® business, the Initial Franchise Fee of $10,500 will be waived.
