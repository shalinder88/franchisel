10. Subsequent events
The Company considers events or transactions that occur after the balance sheet date but prior to the issuance of
the financial statements to provide additional evidence relative to certain estimates or to identify matters that
require additional disclosure. Subsequent events have been evaluated through March 28, 2025, the date these
financial statements are available to be issued, and the financial statements reflect those material items that arose
after the balance sheet date but prior to this date that would be considered recognized subsequent events.
