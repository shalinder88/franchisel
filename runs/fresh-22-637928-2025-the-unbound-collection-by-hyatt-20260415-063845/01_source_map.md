## Key Observations
- Item 6 is extremely long (16 pages) reflecting the complex fee structure of a full-service hotel franchise
- Item 11 is 16 pages with detailed training tables (7 training programs)
- Item 19 provides 3 FPRs plus channel mix data — revenue-only, no cost/profit data disclosed
- Financial statements are parent-level (Hyatt Hotels Corp), not franchisor-level
- Franchisor-level unaudited balance sheet appears in state addenda (California requirement, p356-357)
- 13 exhibits (A through M), with Exhibit M containing state-specific riders
- State addenda cover: California, Hawaii, Maryland, Minnesota, North Dakota, Rhode Island, Virginia
- Michigan provisions appear in front matter (pp 5-6), not in Exhibit M
