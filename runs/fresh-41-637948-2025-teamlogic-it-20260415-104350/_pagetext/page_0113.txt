franchise, the Franchisee shall be compensated for the fair market value, at the time of the termination or

have no value to the Franchisor need not be compensated for. If the Franchisor refuses to renew a franchise 
for the purpose of converting the Franchisee’s business to one owned and operated by the Franchisor, the 
Franchisor, in addition to the remedies provided in this paragraph, shall compensate the Franchisee for the 
loss of goodwill. The Franchisor may deduct from such compensation reasonable costs incurred in removing, 
transporting and disposing of the Franchisee’s inventory, supplies, equipment, and furnishings, and may 
offset from such compensation any moneys due the Franchisor.
