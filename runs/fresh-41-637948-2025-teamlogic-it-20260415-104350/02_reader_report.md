## Pass A: Executive Snapshot

1. **Franchisor**: TeamLogic, Inc., a California corporation incorporated September 1, 2004. Wholly owned subsidiary of Franchise Services, Inc., which is wholly owned by KOAH, Inc. (Delaware).
2. **Brand**: TeamLogic IT — outsourced IT managed services for small and medium-sized businesses (SMBs) via onsite qualified technicians.
3. **System size**: 311 franchised outlets as of December 31, 2024 (192 franchisees). Zero company-owned. Growth trajectory: 243→266→282→311 (2022–2024), net +68 over 3 years.
4. **Investment range**: $109,490–$144,742 total initial investment, including $40,000–$49,500 franchise fee.
5. **Fee burden**: CFF 7% of Gross Sales with $1,000/month minimum after month 12; Ad Fund 1.2% (min $200/mo) after month 12; mandatory $2,500/month local marketing; RMM fees $3–$6/agent/month; software/tech fees ~$500–$650/month.
6. **Item 19**: 161 qualified franchisees (operating 24+ months) generated $204M total network sales in 2024. Q1 avg $3.34M (median $2.39M), Q2 avg $1.00M, Q3 avg $463K, Q4 avg $210K. Top franchisee: $22.4M. Multi-territory operators dominate Q1.
7. **Financial health**: Total revenue $20.3M (2024), net income $8.1M. Distributions to parent: $7.8M in 2024. Retained earnings $4.97M. Clean audit opinion from CliftonLarsonAllen. QSSS (S-corp pass-through) for federal taxes.
8. **Litigation**: Single matter — CA DFPI consent order (Jan 2024) for 4 franchise broker registration violations, $8,000 penalty paid. No material litigation.
9. **Territory**: Protected territory based on 1,500–2,000 target businesses, but not exclusive. Other franchisees may serve clients in your territory. Minimum sales threshold: $2,500/week for 12 consecutive weeks or risk termination.
10. **Renewal**: 10-year initial term, 10-year renewal. Must sign then-current agreement which may be "materially different." $2,000 renewal fee.
11. **Noncompete**: 1 year/25 miles during term; 2 years/25 miles post-term. CA: generally unenforceable per state law.
12. **Dispute resolution**: Arbitration in Orange County, CA. CA law governs. Multi-state addendum overrides for 14 registration states.
13. **Special risks**: Out-of-state dispute resolution, spousal liability, sales performance requirements, mandatory minimum payments.
14. **Projected openings**: 44 new outlets projected for 2025, 2 agreements signed but not yet open.
15. **Parent derives significant revenue from franchisees**: Managed services revenue = $6.8M (37% of franchisor total revenue); gross margin from managed services = $5.04M (32% of total gross margin).

## Pass E: Contract Burden and Legal Mechanics (Item 17)

### Term and Renewal
- **Initial term**: 10 years from execution date.
- **Renewal**: 10-year terms.
- **Renewal conditions**: 6 months advance written notice; not in default; sign then-current franchise agreement (which **may contain materially different terms**); satisfy current qualifications/training; pay $2,000 renewal fee.
- **"Materially different" risk**: Explicitly disclosed. This is a significant risk — renewal is not guaranteed to be on same terms.

### Termination
- **By franchisee**: Under any grounds permitted by law.
- **By franchisor without cause**: Not applicable.
- **By franchisor with cause — curable**: 20 days to cure material breach or monetary default; site not acquired in 90 days; loss of possession or abandon premises >7 days; 3 defaults within 12 months even if cured.
- **By franchisor with cause — non-curable**: Bankruptcy, felony, false books, fraud/material misrepresentation, conduct likely to impair reputation.

### Transfer
- Includes transfer of franchise, assets, or ownership change. Requires franchisor approval.
- Conditions: good standing, transferee meets qualifications, amounts paid, training completed, purchase agreement approved, transfer fee ($10,000) paid, release signed, new agreement signed by transferee.
- **ROFR**: Franchisor can match any offer for your business.
- **Death/disability**: Franchise may be assigned to approved buyer who must complete training.

### Non-Competition
- **During term**: No competing business for 1 year within 25 miles of former business or another TLI business. Non-solicitation of clients for 1 year. Subject to state law.
- **Post-term**: No competing business for 2 years within 25 miles. Non-solicitation for 2 years. Subject to state law.
- **California override**: Non-competes generally unenforceable per CA law.

### Dispute Resolution
- **Arbitration**: All disputes (except certain claims) must be arbitrated in Orange County, CA.
- **Choice of law**: California law, except Section 10.3(g) — state where franchised business is located governs non-compete.
- **Multi-state addendum**: 14 registration states have overrides (see Exhibit E).
- **Jury trial waiver**: In franchise agreement (deleted for North Dakota).

### Post-Termination Obligations
Cease use of marks, website, telephone listing/number, franchisee intranet; pay debts; notify suppliers/clients; cannot operate competing business (subject to non-compete).
