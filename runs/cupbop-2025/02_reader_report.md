# 02 Reader Report — Cupbop Franchise, LLC (2025)

## A. Executive snapshot
1. Cupbop Franchise, LLC — Utah LLC formed 2014-11-14; began offering franchises March 2017. Wholly-owned subsidiary of Cupbop Co. since Jan 2021. Other parents: Gold Light Holdings LLC and MAK Holdings LLC.
2. Concept: Korean-style barbeque rice cups, salads, wings, potstickers; carry-out, on-premises and catering. 1,200–2,000 sq ft.
3. Total system EOY 2024: 59 outlets (30 franchised, 29 company-owned). Net adds in 2024: +5 (+3 franchised, +2 company-owned).
4. Initial investment range: **$296,400 – $664,400** (single unit). $41,250–$42,000 of this is paid to franchisor or affiliate.
5. Initial franchise fee: **$40,000** (first), $30,000 (subsequent). Royalty: **6%** of Gross Sales. Ad fund: up to 4.5%, currently 2%. Local advertising: 0.5% min.
6. Item 19: Three tables (company-owned, franchised, all). 50-store overall set. Average gross revenue $728,821; median $710,250; range $347,132–$1,585,417. Franchised average $658,208; median $669,260.
7. Bottom-quartile risk: Franchised low band (<$550K) averages **$400,962**, low $347,132 — paired with 6% royalty + 2% ad fund + sales-performance-default trigger = real kill-switch.
8. Sales performance kill switch: Year 2+ must hold 50% of trailing 12-month national average; failure over 2 years = default → termination.
9. Term: 10 years from opening; renewal "may be materially different" (10C(d)).
10. Auditor opinion: Forvis Mazars LLP — clean (unqualified) opinion on FY2024/FY2023/FY2022 statements. Net income FY2024 $1,025,658 (FY2023 $781,293).
11. Distributions FY2024: **$1,896,607** vs. net income $1,025,658 — distributions 1.85x net income; member's equity dropped from $892,301 to $21,352. Going-concern question raised (auditor flags evaluation requirement).
12. Item 21 financials are entity-only (Cupbop Franchise, LLC) — store-level economics live with the parent Cupbop Co.
13. Three franchised outlets ceased operations in 2024 (all Las Vegas, NV — Gold Bowl LLC). Zero transfers, zero terminations, zero non-renewals reported.
14. Master franchisee: Kevin Santiago / Gold Bowl LLC operates ~26 of the 30 franchised units (AZ, ID, NV, TX). Concentration risk.
15. Confidentiality clause warning: some franchisees signed clauses that may restrict open communication (Item 20 disclosure).

## C. Supplier control, operations, training, tech (Items 8–11)

Estimated franchisee purchases that flow through specifications = **22–40% of total at opening; 22–33% ongoing**. Cupbop Co. revenue from franchisees in FY2024: $36,920 (small). No rebates currently. **Marketing fund — 2024 spend mix:** 31% production / 29% placement / 40% admin. **40% admin is high vs. national casual-dining benchmarks of 10–15%.** No independent audit required.

## D. Territory, channels, encroachment (Items 12–16)
**No exclusive territory.** Protected territory granted but size varies by location density and not guaranteed. Online orders inside protected territory may be fulfilled by franchisor, affiliates, or another franchisee (no customer right). Franchisor reserves Non-Traditional Venues: arenas, stadiums, airports, hospitals, universities, military bases, food courts, etc., inside or outside the territory. Cupbop Co. already runs concessions at Vivint Smart Home Arena, Rice-Eccles Stadium, LaVell Edwards Stadium.

**Sales performance default:** Year 2+ must maintain 50% of trailing-12-month national average gross sales of all national restaurants. Failure over 2 years = default; franchisor may terminate at sole discretion.

**Trademarks:** Marks owned by Cupbop Co., licensed to Franchise LLC under 2020 License Agreement. Several USPTO marks (4757198, 5289813, 6720588, 7471375, plus pending HOYA marks). No infringement litigation disclosed.

**Item 16:** Must offer everything franchisor designates; menu items strictly per recipe and System Standards; franchisor may add new required items at any time; may set prices via Brand Standards Manual.

## E. Contract burden and legal mechanics (Item 17)
- **Term**: 10 years from opening (FA §1D). ADA term ends at last opening or last day of Development Schedule.
- **Renewal**: 10 years; notice 180–220 days before expiration; remodel; pay successor fee; sign **then-current FA which "may be materially different"** from current — material future-burden risk.
- **Termination by franchisee**: only if franchisor breaches and fails to cure (or per state law).
- **Termination by franchisor with cause**: extensive list (see noncurable defaults below).
- **Curable defaults**: 3 days health/safety/sanitation; 10 days monetary/insurance; 30 days third-party monetary; 90 days to relocate after losing lease.
- **Non-curable defaults** (FA §15B): site not secured/approved within 120 days; not opened in 16 months; opening without written approval; failed training; abandonment; unapproved transfer; misrepresentation; felony conviction; dishonest/unethical conduct; manual disclosure; tax failure; understating Gross Sales; recruiting violations; repeated defaults; bankruptcy; insolvency; trustee/receiver; anti-terrorism violation; default under any other agreement with franchisor or affiliates (cross-default).
- **Transfer**: $10,000 fee (waived for entity-control transfers); franchisor consent (not unreasonably withheld) plus full release; transferee assumes obligations, signs then-current FA, completes training. Franchisor has **right of first refusal** (FA §13E).
- **Death/disability**: 180 days to appoint replacement representative.
- **Non-compete during term**: cannot divert business, own/perform services for competing business, interfere with employees.
- **Post-term non-compete**: **2 years**, premises and within **20 miles of any Cupbop Restaurant** existing, operating, or under construction at termination. (Note: 20-mile radius from ANY Cupbop, not just yours — broader than typical.)
- **Franchisor option to purchase**: at fair market value upon termination/expiration (FA §16E).
- **Choice of law / forum**: Utah law, Salt Lake County Utah arbitration/litigation (subject to state law overrides).
- **Integration clause**: Only FDD + FA terms enforceable.
