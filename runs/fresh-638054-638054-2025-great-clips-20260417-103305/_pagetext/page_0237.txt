        This Three Star Program Agreement (this “Agreement”) is made by and between Great
Clips, Inc., a Minnesota corporation (“Great Clips”) and ________________________________
(“Franchisee”), in consideration of the covenants and agreements set forth below. All capitalized
terms not identified herein shall have their respective meaning as set forth in the Three Star
Franchise Agreement (as identified in Section 1 below).

        1.      Application and Area. Franchisee hereby applies for three GREAT CLIPS®
franchise agreements (collectively, the “Three Star Franchise Agreements”), to be located within
the following designated market area (“DMA”), __________________________________(each a “Three
Star Franchise Agreement”). This Agreement does not grant to Franchisee any exclusive rights
for any specific locations or territory whatsoever. Upon acceptance by Great Clips, Franchisee is
entitled to receive the benefits of the Three Star Program, as set forth below, for each of the Salons
to be opened under the terms and conditions of this Agreement.

        2.      Three Star Program Fee. Upon signing this Agreement, Franchisee will pay the
sum of $35,000 (the “Program Fee”) to Great Clips. The Program Fee paid by Franchisee to
participate in the Three Star Program is non-refundable upon acceptance by Great Clips. In the
event Franchisee fails to obtain fully-executed leases for the Three Star Franchise Agreements
within the Term of this Agreement, or if Franchisee fails to qualify for the benefits of the Three
Star Program for any other reason, no portion of the Program Fee will be refunded to Franchisee
or be applied to offset any future fees owed to Great Clips.

       3.     Market Development Ad Fund Contribution. Upon signing this Agreement,
Franchisee will also pay the sum of $5,000 to the Great Clips Market Development Advertising
Fund (“MDAF”). This MDAF contribution paid by Franchisee to participate in the Three Star
Program is non-refundable.

        4.      Term. The term of this Agreement will commence on the date of execution by
Great Clips (the “Effective Date”) and will be in effect for a term of 24 months from the Effective
Date (the “Term”). Franchisee must obtain a fully-executed lease (the “Lease”), as described
further in Section 5 of this Agreement, for each of the Three Star Franchise Agreements within
this time frame in order to qualify for the Three Star Program benefits. Franchisee will, upon Great
Clips’ acceptance of this Agreement, have the right to pursue three potential locations for the Three
Star Franchises. The 24-month period will not be extended under any circumstances. Great
Clips, its representatives and its agents make no assurances to Franchisee that suitable real estate
will be available. Except as provided in Section 7 below, if Franchisee fails to obtain the three,
fully-executed Leases within the 24-month period, then Franchisee shall forfeit all Three Star
Program benefits for any Three Star Franchise Agreement for which there is no corresponding
signed Lease, as defined in this Agreement.
