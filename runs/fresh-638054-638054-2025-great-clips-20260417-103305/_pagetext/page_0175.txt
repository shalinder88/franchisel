Note 1.     Nature of Business and Significant Accounting Policies
Nature of business: Great Clips, Inc. (the Company) is a franchisor within the hair care industry with its
corporate office located in Minneapolis, Minnesota. The Company has franchisees located throughout the
United States and Canada. The Company also leases and operates training facilities for meeting and
training purposes throughout the United States and Canada.

A summary of the Company’s significant accounting policies follows:

Cash and cash equivalents: The Company considers all highly liquid investments, such as money
market accounts, with original maturities of 90 days or less to be cash equivalents. Cash equivalents are
stated at cost, which approximates fair value. The Company maintains cash balances at several financial
institutions in the United States and Canada. Cash held in Canadian banks totaled approximately
$7,346,000, $5,934,000 and $6,324,000 at December 31, 2024, 2023 and 2022, respectively. Cash
accounts at each institution in the United States have coverage by the Federal Deposit Insurance
Corporation up to $250,000. Accounts at each institution maintained in Canada are insured by the
Canada Deposit Insurance Corporation up to $100,000 Canadian dollars. The Company has not
experienced losses from amounts in excess of insured limits.

Restricted cash and other current assets and accrued cooperative advertising: The Company
utilizes a systemwide marketing and advertising fund, the Great Clips, Inc. North American Advertising
Fund (the Ad Fund) to fund advertising costs. Franchisees are required to remit 5% of sales to the Ad
Fund, which is used for marketing and advertising efforts throughout the system. As of December 31,
2024, 2023 and 2022, the Ad Fund’s restricted cash and other current assets and the related liability were
approximately $33,066,000, $34,033,000 and $32,411,000, respectively. See also Note 2.
