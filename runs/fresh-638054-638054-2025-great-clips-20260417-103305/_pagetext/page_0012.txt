                                    BUSINESS EXPERIENCE

Chairman of the Board: Raymond L. Barton

Mr. Barton joined Great Clips in March 1983, and has held various executive positions since that
time, including serving as President (November 1987 to October 1998), as Chief Executive Officer
and Chairman (November 1998 until January 2011) and has served as Chairman of the Board since
February 2011, all located in Minneapolis, Minnesota.

Vice Chair of the Board: Rhoda C. Olsen

Ms. Olsen has been employed by Great Clips since December 1984, and has held various executive
positions since that time, including serving as President (October 1998 to January 2011), Chief
Executive Officer (February 2011 to December 2017), and has served as Vice Chair of the Board
since January 2018, all located in Minneapolis, Minnesota.

Chief Executive Officer and President: Robert D. Goggins

Mr. Goggins joined Great Clips as the Vice President of Franchise Development in July 2007 and
has held various executive positions since that time, including serving as Senior Vice President of
Real Estate and Development (September 2011 to December 2013), Executive Vice President and
Chief Operations Officer (January 2014 to December 2017), President (January 2018 to
December 2025), and he is our Chief Executive Officer and President as of January 2026,
all located in Minneapolis, Minnesota.

Chief Financial Officer: Rachelle M. Johnson

Ms. Johnson joined Great Clips in June 1999 and has held various positions since then, including
serving as Director of Finance (2011 to April of 2015), Vice President of Finance (May 2015 to
March 2016), Vice President of Finance and Treasurer (April 2016 to March 2024) and as of April,
2024, Ms. Johnson is the Chief Financial Officer, all located in Minneapolis, Minnesota.

Chief Operations Officer: Yvonne S. Mercer

Ms. Mercer joined Great Clips in November 1994 and has held various positions since that time,
including serving as Regional Vice President of Operations (November 2005 to December 2012),
Vice President of Operations since January 2013 and is now the Chief Operations Officer as of
June 2022, all positions located in Minneapolis, Minnesota.
