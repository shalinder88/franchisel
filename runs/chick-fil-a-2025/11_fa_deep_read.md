## Insurance Requirements (Section 6.11)

Licensee must procure and maintain at own expense:
- **Commercial General Liability** covering each Unit for premises, operations, personal injury, products/completed operations, and assumed liability under insured contract
- Must be maintained in full force at all times during License Term
- Cannot be cancelled, lapsed, suspended, or modified without CFA notification
- Specific coverage limits are not stated in the template — determined per individual agreement or manual

## Indemnification (Section 10.3)

- **Mutual indemnification**: Each party (Indemnitor) holds harmless the other (Indemnitee) and its officers, directors, employees, agents, affiliates, parent, subsidiaries, successors and assigns
- Covers: (i) untruth/breach of representations or warranties, (ii) breach of obligations, (iii) negligent or wrongful acts
- **Limitation**: To the extent applicable law prohibits either party from indemnification, that party's obligation is reduced so each party's obligation is roughly equal
- Practically asymmetric: licensee's operations generate most third-party claims; CFA's broader IP/system claims are less frequent

## Confidentiality Agreement (Exhibit 1 to License Agreement)

- Must be signed by **all licensee personnel working as manager or supervisor** in a Unit
- For FSP form: also signed by each Third Party Operator and all TPO personnel
- Obligations: comply with all CFA policies; treat operations manual, recipes, sales data, supplier lists, business plans as confidential trade-secret information
- Duration: period of work plus 3 years following
- Signatures with start/end dates tracked per individual

## Core Menu (Addendum B)

- Addendum B defines the Core Menu for Licensed Units
- Pages 103–142 in FSP form show menu pages (content is blank/template in the PDF template)
- Licensee must offer all Products on the Unit Core Menu approved by CFA
- May not sell any products not on the authorized menu

## Sales Reporting (Addendum D)

- **Daily**: submit all product sales reports via CFA Licensee Sales Portal after closing or before next business day opening
- **Monthly**: Gross Receipts reports due by 5th of each month with POS-generated PDF supporting documentation
- **License Fee remittance**: by mail or ACH, received by CFA by the 25th of each month
- Mail address: Chick-fil-A, Inc., P.O. Box 208913, Dallas, TX 75320-8806

## License Fee Structure (Addendum C)

- **Standard**: 10% of Gross Receipts per 4-week Accounting Period, payable by 25th of each calendar month
- **Transportation venues**: 7% of Gross Receipts (applies only to units at/within transportation facilities)
- **Training fee**: $250 per additional employee, payable 10 days before training start

## POS Systems (Section 6.12)

- CFA reserves right to require specific POS hardware/software systems
- POS must be able to: accept and process all transactions (including mobile ordering/payment), transmit POS data to CFA, facilitate gift card/loyalty/affinity programs
- CFA may designate single-source supplier for POS components (which may be CFA or affiliate)
- Licensee bears responsibility for proper functioning and ensuring POS is operational at all times including internet/extranet access
- CFA may poll for information including sales data, pricing, inventory, customer transactions

## Key Structural Terms from License Agreement

- **Separate and independent business owner** (Section 10.1): CFA does not employ, co-employ, or jointly employ Licensee's employees
- **Georgia choice of law** (Section 10.5): All rights/obligations governed by Georgia law
- **Georgia choice of forum** (Section 10.6): Exclusive jurisdiction in Fulton County Superior Court and N.D. Georgia, Atlanta Division
- **Equitable remedies** (Section 10.2): CFA entitled to injunctive relief and specific performance for material breaches
- **No implied waiver** (Section 11.1): Failure to require performance does not waive future enforcement
- **Entire agreement** (Section 11.9): Agreement plus addenda, exhibits, and guarantees constitute the complete agreement; supersedes all prior negotiations
