## 6. Key Observations

- This is the **License Program FDD**, separate from the Operator FDD. Licensed Units are non-traditional locations (universities, hospitals, airports, businesses).
- Exhibit E franchisee list pages (~189-200+) suffer from CID font encoding; the Operator restaurant listing starting ~page 201+ is readable plain text.
- Two forms of License Agreement: Self-Operators (Exhibit B-1) and Food Service Providers (Exhibit B-2).
- No guaranty exhibit identified — guaranties are not a separate exhibit in this FDD.
- No development agreement exhibit — Chick-fil-A does not offer area development for Licensed Units.
- No digital services / technology / loyalty / fuel / ACH exhibit as standalone — technology obligations are within the License Agreements and Item 11.
- State addenda cover 14 states with both FDD addenda and License Agreement amendments.
