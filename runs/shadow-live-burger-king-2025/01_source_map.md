## Early risk flags

- Out-of-state dispute resolution mandated (FL-only litigation venue)
- Mediation required for development disputes (delay to suit)
- RBI / 3G Capital parent structure (public market parent; 3G ~26% voting power)
- Internal Reorganization Aug 2022: BK Corporation → Burger King Company LLC; predecessor dissolved Dec 2022
- Large exhibit surface (A–Z; multiple successor/remodel/incentive programs = high contract complexity)
- Reclaim the Flame, Reclaim the Flame 2, Fuel the Flame, Crown Your Career: overlapping incentive programs
