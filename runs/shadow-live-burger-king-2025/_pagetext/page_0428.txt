KNOW ALL MEN BY THESE PRESENTS: 
That BURGER KING COMPANY LLC, Florida limited liability company (hereinafter

claims or actions pertaining to same.

Seller delivers possession of the Franchised Restaurant and the Purchased Assets to the Buyer.
