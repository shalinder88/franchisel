Fuel the Flame Co-Investment Agreement 
If you are a participant in the Ad Fund “Fuel The Flame” Program pursuant to a “Fuel the Flame” 
Co-Investment Agreement, you will pay an advertising contribution of 4.5% of Gross Sales through 
December 31, 2026, under a Fuel the Flame Co-Investment Agreement, the current form of which 
is attached as Exhibit Y1.  If the average  Burger King Restaurant 4-Wall EBITDA, as calculated 
by BKC in accordance with the terms of such agreement, is an amount equal to $230,000.00 as of 
December 31, 2026, your advertising contribution rate will remain at 4.5% through December 31, 
2028, but if the amount is less than $230,000.00 as of December 31, 2026, your advertising 
contribution rate will revert to 4.0% and remain at that rate through December 31, 2028, unless the 
required majority of participating franchisees vote in favor of maintaining the rate at 4.5% through 
December 31, 2028.  After December 31, 2028, unless you entered into a Reclaim the Flame 2 
Master Program Agreement (“RTF2 MPA”) prior to December 31, 2025, the current form of which 
is attached at Exhibit X1, your advertising contribution rate will be as set forth in the then-current 
Franchise Disclosure Document, which rate may be up to 4.5%. Your obligation to pay the 
advertising contribution in accordance with the Ad Fund “Fuel the Flame” program will be set forth 
in a “Fuel the Flame” Advertising Contribution Addendum to your Franchise Agreement in the 
form attached as Exhibit Y2.   If you are a new franchisee, you are not eligible to participate in the 
“Fuel the Flame” Program.  
If you are a participant in the Ad Fund “Fuel The Flame” Program pursuant to a “Fuel the Flame” 
Co-Investment Agreement and you entered into a RTF2 MPA prior to December 31, 2025, with 
respect to those Restaurants that you remodel pursuant to your RTF2 MPA, your obligation to pay 
the advertising contribution will be set forth in a Fuel the Flame/Legacy Rate Advertising 
Contribution Addendum to your successor Franchise Agreement in the form attached as Exhibit 
Y3.  You will pay an advertising contribution of 4.5% of Gross Sales through December 31, 2026.  
If the average Burger King Restaurant 4-Wall EBITDA, as calculated by BKC in accordance with 
the terms of such agreement, is an amount equal to $230,000.00 as of December 31, 2026, your 
advertising contribution rate will remain at 4.5% through December 31, 2028, but if the amount is 
less than $230,000.00 as of December 31, 2026, your advertising contribution rate will revert to 
4.0% and remain at that rate through December 31, 2028, unless the required majority of 
participating franchisees vote in favor of maintaining the rate at 4.5% through December 31, 2028.  
After December 31, 2028, your advertising contribution rate will be 4.0% and remain at that rate 
until the earlier of the date on which your predecessor Franchise Agreement would have expired or 
the date on which at least 50% of the BURGER KING® Restaurant operators are either paying an 
advertising contribution of at least 4.5% of Gross Sales or have signed a Fuel the Flame/Legacy 
Rate Advertising Contribution Addendum to their Franchise Agreements.  Thereafter, you will pay 
an advertising contribution of 4.5% of Gross Sales for the remaining term of your Franchise 
Agreement.
