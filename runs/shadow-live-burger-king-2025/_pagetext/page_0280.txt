CHOICE OF LAW; JURISDICTION AND VENUE.  This Guaranty shall be governed by 
and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Florida.  You hereby irrevocably submit to the 
jurisdiction of the U.S. District Court for the Southern District of Florida, or if such court lacks jurisdiction,

to the fullest extent you may effectively do so, the defense of an inconvenient forum to the maintenance of 
such action or proceeding and any right to jurisdiction on account of your place of residence or domicile.  
You agree that a final judgment in any such action or proceeding shall be conclusive and may be enforced 
in other jurisdictions by suit on the judgment or in any other manner provided by law.

enforceability of the remaining provisions contained herein shall not in any way be affected or impaired 
thereby.
