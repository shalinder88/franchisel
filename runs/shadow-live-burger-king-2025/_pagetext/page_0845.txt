Note 17. Commitments and Contingencies
Letters of Credit
As of December 31, 2024, we had $24 million in irrevocable standby letters of credit outstanding, which were issued primarily 
to certain insurance carriers to guarantee payments of deductibles for various insurance programs, such as health and commercial 
liability insurance. Of these letters of credit outstanding, $2 million are secured by the collateral under our Revolving Credit Facility 
and the remainder are secured by cash collateral. As of December 31, 2024, no amounts had been drawn on any of these irrevocable 
standby letters of credit.
Purchase Commitments
As of December 31, 2024, we have arrangements for information technology and telecommunication services with an aggregate 
contractual obligation of $84 million over the next five years, some of which have early termination fees and commitments to 
purchase advertising which totaled $192 million, most of which is due within the next 12 months. We also entered into commitments 
to purchase beverage and restaurant equipment which totaled $23 million over the next four years.
