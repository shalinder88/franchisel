Lastly, you may also qualify for the SDM incentive program if (a) you sign a Franchise Agreement
on or before March 31, 2026, to develop and operate a previously closed Dunkin’ Restaurant
(without relocating it) in the Select Developing Territory, (b) as of the date the Franchise Agreement
is signed, the Restaurant that you wish to re-open was permanently closed for at least 30 calendar
days, and (c) you are not signing the Franchise Agreement in connection with a transfer. Lastly, you
may qualify to participate in the SDM incentive program if you sign the Incentive Amendment
(Exhibit D-3) to an existing Store Development Agreement on or before March 31, 2026 to amend
your existing Store Development Agreement to add 1 or more new traditional Restaurants within the
Select Developing Territory to your existing development schedule.
