Pacific Northwest Incentive Program: If you qualify for the Pacific Northwest Incentive program,
then:
A. For the first traditional Dunkin’ Restaurant in the Pacific Northwest Territory that you or
your affiliates ever develop and open under the Pacific Northwest Incentive program in
compliance with the terms of the Franchise Agreement on or before the required opening
date (and, if applicable, the Store Development Agreement), we will provide you with a
CFF credit equal to $50,000 for that first Restaurant (the “One-Time Opening Incentive
Credit”);
B. For the first and each subsequent traditional Dunkin’ Restaurant in the Pacific Northwest
Territory that you or your affiliates develop and open under the Pacific Northwest Incentive
program in compliance with the terms of the Franchise Agreement on or before the required
opening date (and, if applicable, the Store Development Agreement) during the 2026 or
2027 calendar year, we will provide you with a CFF credit equal to $300,000 (the “2026–
2027 Opening Incentive Credit”); and
C. For each subsequent traditional Dunkin’ Restaurant in the Pacific Northwest Territory that
you or your affiliates develop and open under the Pacific Northwest Incentive program in
compliance with the terms of the Franchise Agreement on or before the required opening
date (and, if applicable, the Store Development Agreement) during the 2028 calendar year
or later, we will provide you with a CFF credit equal to $250,000 (the “2028+ Opening
Incentive Credit”).
Within 30 days from the opening date of the Restaurant, any applicable Continuing Franchise Fee credits will
