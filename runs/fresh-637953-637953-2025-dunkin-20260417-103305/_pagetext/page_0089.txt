Notes
(1)
“AUV” or “Unit Volume” for a 2024 Franchised Restaurant is the same as Gross Sales, which means
all revenue related to the sale of approved products and provision of services (including direct delivery, catering
and/or delivery services through third parties) through the operation of the franchised Restaurant, but does not
include money received for the sale of stored value cards and deposited into a central account maintained for
the benefit of the System; taxes collected from guests on behalf of a governmental body; or the sale of approved
products to another entity franchised or licensed by us for subsequent resale.
(2)

group and dividing the sum by the number of restaurants in the applicable group. We obtain the Unit Volumes
for franchised Dunkin’ Restaurants from sales reports that franchisees submit to us and from data that we
collected from franchisees’ POS systems. We have not independently audited that information. Prospective
franchisees and sellers of franchises should be advised that no certified public accountant has audited these
figures or expressed his or her opinion concerning their contents or form. Upon your reasonable request, we
