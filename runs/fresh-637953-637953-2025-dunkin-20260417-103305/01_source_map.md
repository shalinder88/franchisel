## Key Observations
- Dunkin' system: 9,768 US restaurants (9,734 franchised, 34 company-owned) as of Dec 29, 2024
- Standalone Dunkin': 8,480; Multi-Brand: 19; Combo (Dunkin' + BR): 1,269
- International: 4,328 single-branded Dunkin' restaurants
- Part of Inspire Brands (Roark Capital portfolio) — sister brands: Arby's, BWW, Jimmy John's, Sonic, Baskin-Robbins
- Two financial statement sets: DB Master Finance Parent LLC (guarantor) and Dunkin' Brands, Inc.
- KPMG auditor, unqualified opinion
- Securitization structure (2015) with DB Master Finance LLC
- Strong growth trajectory: +200 net franchised outlets in 2024, +278 projected new openings
- No exclusive territory under Franchise Agreement; limited SDA territory protection
- Extensive incentive programs for western/developing markets
