# Reader Report — Dunkin' Donuts Franchising LLC (637953-2025)

## Pass A: Executive Snapshot

1. **Franchisor**: Dunkin' Donuts Franchising LLC, a Delaware LLC formed March 15, 2006, HQ at Three Glenlake Parkway, Atlanta, GA 30328. Wholly-owned indirect subsidiary of DB Master Finance LLC and DB Master Finance Parent LLC. Part of Inspire Brands (Roark Capital).
2. **System Size**: 9,768 US Dunkin' restaurants (9,734 franchised, 34 company-owned) as of December 29, 2024. Includes 8,480 single-branded Dunkin', 19 Multi-Brand Locations, and 1,269 Dunkin'/Baskin-Robbins combos. Additionally, 4,328 international franchised Dunkin' restaurants.
3. **Growth**: Net +200 franchised Dunkin' outlets in 2024 (312 opened, 67 ceased, 28 non-renewals, 1 termination). 278 projected new franchised openings in next fiscal year. Strong positive trajectory across all 3 years: +77 (2022), +178 (2023), +200 (2024).
4. **Investment Range**: Freestanding $526,900–$1,832,500; Shopping Center/Storefront $437,500–$1,333,500; Gas & Convenience $210,900–$1,065,500.
5. **Initial Franchise Fee**: Tiered by DMA from $40,000 (Type 6) to $90,000 (Type 1, Northeast core markets). Gas/convenience and SDO locations at 50% of standard IFF, pro-rated by term.
6. **Fee Burden**: CFF 5.9% of Gross Sales + CAF 5.0% of Gross Sales = 10.9% ongoing royalty+advertising burden. Loyalty Program 1.4% (declining to 1.2% in 2027+). Total ongoing percentage burden: ~12.1–12.3% of Gross Sales.
7. **Item 19 AUV**: Overall $1,304,217 (7,190 restaurants). 1st quartile $2,073,245; 4th quartile $646,650. Traditional freestanding AUV $1,534,772. Drive-thru AUV $1,508,901 vs. no drive-thru $1,082,835.
8. **Financial Health**: DB Master Finance Parent total assets $9.73B, total revenue $1.65B, net income $585M (2024). Long-term debt $5.0B (securitized). KPMG auditor, unqualified opinion, no going concern.
9. **Territory**: No exclusive territory under individual Franchise Agreement. Limited SDA protection during Store Development Agreement term only.
10. **Contract Term**: 20 years, renewal available subject to conditions including then-current form of agreement (materially different terms risk), general release, renewal fee, no more than 3 defaults in prior 10 years.
11. **Noncompete**: During term, no interest in Competitive Business (>20% revenue from coffee and/or baked goods). Post-term 24 months within 5 miles of Premises or any other Dunkin' restaurant.
12. **Dispute Resolution**: No arbitration. Federal/state courts in Atlanta, Georgia. Georgia law applies. Subject to state addenda overrides.
13. **Special Risks**: Out-of-state dispute resolution (state of restaurant location, not franchisee's home state).
14. **Securitization**: Complex financing structure via 2015 securitization transaction. DB Master Finance Parent guarantees franchisee obligations. $5.0B long-term securitized debt.

## Pass E: Contract Burden, Legal Mechanics (Item 17)

### Renewal
- Term: 20 years
- Renewal: Available, subject to conditions
- Conditions: Written notice; maintained standards during term; no more than 3 defaults in prior 10 years; no uncured defaults; paid all amounts; obtain acceptable lease; sign **then-current form of Franchise Agreement** (may be materially different); pay renewal fee; execute termination and mutual general release

### Termination
- By franchisee: **No right** to terminate
- By franchisor without cause: **No right** to terminate without cause
- Curable defaults (with cure periods): 24 hours for health/sanitation/safety violations; 24 hours to resume operations; 7 days for payment default; 30 days for other defaults
- Non-curable defaults (immediate termination): Loss of premises; unauthorized transfer; misuse of confidential information; felony conviction; fraud; intentional Gross Sales underreporting; unauthorized use of premises; cross-default (another agreement terminated); 3+ same/similar defaults within 12 months; bankruptcy

### Transfer
- Broad "transfer" definition covering all direct/indirect ownership changes
- Control Transfer requires: transferee meets criteria + guaranty; satisfied all obligations; restaurant in compliance; reasonable sale price; general release; transferee bound by FA or sign then-current form; repair/refurbish restaurant
- Non-Controlling Transfer: good character, meets standards, guaranty
- Right of First Refusal: 60-day notice period for any sale; new 60-day period if terms change
- Option to Purchase: Within 30 days post-termination/expiration, franchisor may purchase Operating Assets at fair market value less indebtedness

### Death/Disability
Legal representative must propose transfer within 12 months; complete transfer within 18 months.

### Noncompete
- During term: No interest in Competitive Business (>20% revenue from coffee/baked goods); no customer diversion; no opposition to government approval of new Dunkin'; no acts injurious to goodwill
- Post-term: 24 months, within 5 miles of Premises or any other Dunkin' restaurant

### Dispute Resolution
- No arbitration or mediation required
- Forum: Federal, state, or local courts in Atlanta, Georgia
- Governing law: Georgia
- Integration/merger clause
- Subject to state addenda modifications
