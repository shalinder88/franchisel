25. Income taxes 
The Company accounts for income taxes in accordance with ASC 740. ASC 740 prescribes the use 
of the liability method whereby deferred tax asset and liability account balances are determined 
based on differences between the financial reporting and tax bases of assets and liabilities and are 
measured using the enacted tax rates. The effects of future changes in tax laws or rates are not 
anticipated. 
Under ASC 740, income taxes are recognized for the following: a) amount of tax payable for the 
current year and b) deferred tax liabilities and assets for future tax consequences of events that have 
been recognized differently in the consolidated financial statements than for tax purposes.
