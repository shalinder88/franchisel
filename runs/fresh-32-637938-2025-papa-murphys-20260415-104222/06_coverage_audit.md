## Material Gaps

1. **Item 21 FY2024/2023 financial statements (pp. 123–158):** Image-only pages. Cannot verify current-year auditor, opinion, balance sheet, income statement, or going concern status. This is the most material gap.

2. **Exhibit D — Franchise Agreement clause walk:** The 61-page FA has not been directly read. All contract burden information comes from Item 17 cross-references. Key provisions that may contain additional obligations (liquidated damages formula detail, guaranty scope, death/disability mechanics, cross-default triggers, ACH authorization scope) are not yet confirmed from primary source.

3. **Exhibit M — Successive Addendum:** The renewal terms are referenced in Item 17 but the actual addendum (pp. 315–318) has not been deep-read. This governs the economics and conditions of the successor franchise term.

4. **Exhibit N — Release of Claims:** Required for renewal and transfer. Scope not assessed — important to understand what rights are waived.

5. **State addenda structured promotion:** Exhibit F has been read but not yet structured into canonical format with per-state override entries.

## Unresolveds

1. **[HIGH]** FY2024/2023 financial statements unavailable for text extraction — current-year financial health of guarantor unknown.
2. **[HIGH]** Maryland special risk: "franchisor's financial condition calls into question financial ability to provide services." Fee deferral required in Maryland.
3. **[HIGH]** System contraction: 17.4% decline in 3 years. Is this stabilizing or accelerating?
4. **[MEDIUM]** Brand Marketing Fund deficit of $7.5M — what happens if deficit grows? Who bears the shortfall?
5. **[MEDIUM]** Gag clause — limits prospective franchisee's ability to conduct due diligence with former franchisees.
6. **[MEDIUM]** Item 19 provides only Net Sales — no cost data, no EBITDA. Cannot model unit-level profitability.
7. **[LOW]** Table 3 / Table 1 discrepancy: Table 1 shows 1,212 franchised start of 2022 but Table 3 sums to 1,210.
8. **[LOW]** Dan Rudolph (VP Operations) personal bankruptcy through JND Tropics LLC.

## Contradictions

1. **Table 1 vs Table 3 start count (2022):** Table 1 shows 1,212 franchised outlets at start of 2022; Table 3 state-by-state totals sum to 1,210. Difference of 2 units — possibly rounding or classification issue.

2. **Company-owned outlet count (Table 4 end of 2024):** Table 4 shows 44 at end of 2024. Item 1 states 43 company-operated stores at end of FY2024. The difference of 1 may reflect the temporarily closed Tennessee store.
