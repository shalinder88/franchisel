# Reader Report — Papa Murphy's International LLC (FDD #637938-2025)

## Pass A: Executive Snapshot

1. **System:** Papa Murphy's is a take-and-bake pizza franchise — no ovens, no delivery, no dining area, ~1,400 sq ft stores. 1,079 total US stores at end of FY2024 (1,001 franchised, 43 company-owned, plus 35 international).
2. **Entity:** Papa Murphy's International LLC (Delaware LLC), wholly-owned subsidiary of Papa Murphy's Company Stores, Inc. → Papa Murphy's Holdings, Inc. → MTY Franchising USA, Inc. → MTY Food Group Inc. (TSX-listed Canadian parent).
3. **Ownership history:** Founded 1995 (predecessors since 1982). Acquired by MTY Food Group in May 2019. MTY operates 50+ restaurant brands.
4. **Investment:** $367,428–$733,124 total. Franchise fee $25,000 (first), $15,000 (subsequent). Leasehold improvements $139,000–$325,000. Opening package $150,114–$230,000.
5. **Fee burden:** Royalty 5% Net Sales + Brand Marketing Fee 2% + Local Marketing min(5% Net Sales, $2,000/mo) + online ordering $0.35/txn + loyalty $53/mo + CRM $10/mo + tech fees ~$200/mo. Effective ongoing fee load is heavy at 12%+ of Net Sales.
6. **Item 19:** FPR provided. System average Net Sales $688,133 (2024), median $616,110. Only 40.2% exceeded average. Top 10% averaged $1,384,981; bottom 10% averaged $311,462. Regional West stores significantly outperform ($819,415 avg) vs. East ($488,609 avg).
7. **Contraction risk:** System is shrinking — 1,212 → 1,145 → 1,119 → 1,001 franchised stores over 3 years. 96 terminations in 2024, 28 reacquired by franchisor. Net loss of 118 franchised units in 2024 alone.
8. **Financial health:** Financial statements are for MTY Franchising USA, Inc. (guarantor). FY2022 data shows $1.18B total assets, $235M stockholder's equity. PwC audit, unqualified opinion. Note: FY2024/2023 statements are image-only (pp. 123–158) — not directly readable from text layer.
9. **Growth trajectory:** Declining. Only 8 new franchised openings in 2024 vs. 96 terminations. 26 FAs signed but not yet opened. Projected 9 new franchised + 1 company-owned in next FY.
10. **Territory:** No exclusive territory. Franchisor reserves all rights to compete, open nearby stores, use all channels including internet/ECS. Franchisee prohibited from using social media or internet marketing.
11. **Renewal:** 5-year successive term. Must sign then-current FA (may have different terms, higher fees). Must sign general release. Successive franchise fee = half of then-current franchise fee.
12. **Disputes:** Mediation first (US Arbitration & Mediation Service), then litigation in franchisor's principal place of business (currently WA). Washington law governs. Jury trial waiver. State addenda modify for 10 states.
13. **Special risks:** Out-of-state dispute resolution; guarantor liabilities can exceed tangible assets; Maryland special risk re franchisor financial condition; Brand Marketing Fund has $7.5M deficit.
14. **Gag clause:** "Current and former franchisees sign provisions restricting their ability to speak openly about their experience with the Papa Murphy's franchise system."

## Pass B: Front Matter, Formats, Fees, Investment (Items 1–7)

### Item 1 — The Franchisor
Papa Murphy's International LLC is a Delaware LLC formed June 2004, converted from Inc. to LLC May 2010. Headquarters in Vancouver, WA. Acquired by MTY Food Group (TSX: MTY) in May 2019. MTY USA (Tennessee corp, Scottsdale AZ) is intermediate parent. MTY owns 50+ restaurant brands through various affiliates including Kahala Franchising, Famous Dave's, Village Inn, Wetzel's Pretzels, Cold Stone Creamery, etc.

The company operates take-and-bake pizza stores — guests create fresh pizza in-store, take it home, bake it. No delivery, no sit-down dining. Average store ~1,400 sq ft. No pizza ovens, venting hoods, delivery drivers, or wait staff.

At end of FY2024 (Dec 2, 2024): 1,079 total stores (1,001 franchised US, 43 company-owned US, 35 international in Canada and UAE). International operations through MTY Canada and third-party master franchise agreements.

Key affiliates: Murphy's Marketing Services, Inc. (FL corp) — marketing and gift card services. MM1 Regional LLC and MM2 Regional LLC — regional cooperatives.

Franchise Relations Pact adopted in 2017.

## Pass D: Territory, Channels, Encroachment (Items 12–16)

### Item 12 — Territory
- **No exclusive territory.** Franchisee may face competition from other franchisees, company-owned stores, or other channels.
- Franchisor reserves right to: establish competing stores anywhere; sell products through any channel including ECS/internet, wholesale, mail order, government/military; purchase or merge with competitors; offer franchises on different terms.
- **ECS reserved exclusively to franchisor.** Franchisee may NOT independently market on internet or social media.
- Must get written approval before advertising in any media.

## Pass E: Contract Burden, Legal Mechanics (Item 17)

### Term and Renewal
- **Initial term:** 10 years
- **Renewal:** One 5-year successive term, subject to: 6 months written notice; faithful performance; refurbish/remodel; sign general release (Exhibit N); sign then-current FA (may have different terms, higher royalty/marketing fees); pay successive franchise fee; acceptable location and lease; attend required training.
- **"Materially different" risk:** Renewal FA may have "different terms and conditions (e.g., higher royalty and/or marketing fees)."

### Transfer
- All transfers require franchisor approval.
- Conditions: 30 days advance written request; purchase price ≤ 120% of fair market value; transferee qualifies; transferee completes training; Transfer Fee paid; transferee signs new FA on then-current terms; store refurbished; seller signs release; franchisor consent of lenders/landlords.
- **Right of first refusal:** Franchisor has 21 days to match any offer (must notify within 7 days of receiving offer).
- **Option to purchase on termination:** Equipment/furnishings at depreciated book value (5-year straight-line); inventory at invoice cost. No payment for goodwill. May set off amounts owed.
- **Option to purchase on expiration:** Fair market value. No goodwill. May set off.

### Termination
- **Terminable with cause only.** No termination without cause.
- **Curable defaults:** 30 days for operational breaches; 10 days for payment defaults; 24 hours for health/safety.
- **Incurable defaults:** Abandonment, repeated default, misrepresentation, failure to complete training, criminal conviction, endangerment of public health/safety, insolvency/bankruptcy, underreporting/fraud, noncompliance with law, lease termination, unauthorized transfer, termination of any other FA, acts injurious to brand.
- **Cross-default:** Termination of any other FA with franchisor is incurable default.

### Death/Disability
180 days for heirs/beneficiaries to apply to continue or transfer.

### Noncompete
- **During term:** No interest in any business engaged in wholesale/retail sale of pizza or products served by the Franchised Store.
- **Post-term:** Same restriction, 25-mile radius from Franchised Store, 2 years after termination/expiration.

### Dispute Resolution
- **Mediation first:** Non-binding mediation via US Arbitration and Mediation Service, Inc., at office nearest franchisor's Support Center (or mutually agreed location).
- **Litigation:** Federal or state court in judicial district of franchisor's principal place of business (currently Clark County, WA).
- **Governing law:** Washington (subject to state addenda modifications).
- **Assignment by franchisor:** No restrictions on franchisor's right to transfer.

### Early Termination Damages
Formula: (average monthly Royalty + BMF for any consecutive 12 months in preceding 48 months) × months remaining ÷ 2. If no 6-month written notice, damages not divided by 2.
