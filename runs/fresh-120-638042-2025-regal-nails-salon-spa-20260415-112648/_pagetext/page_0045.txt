ITEM 10 
FINANCING 
We do not offer direct financing. We do not guarantee your note or obligation.  If we 
are a party tenant to the lease, we provide indirect financing through our lease of the salon 
site, which we sublet to you and for which we remain liable to the landlord.  This indirect 
financing applies exclusively to rents owed under the lease, and does not extend to 
equipment, fixtures, inventory, supplies or any other items.  In most cases, the rent due under 
the Franchise Agreement and the lease is based on the salon premises sq. ft. and its market 
value. The terms under which we will sublease a premises to you is included in the Sublease 
Schedule attached to the Franchise Agreement.
