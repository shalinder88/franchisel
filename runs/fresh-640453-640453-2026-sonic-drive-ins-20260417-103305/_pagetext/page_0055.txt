General Manager Leadership Class

At least one full-time manager working in your Restaurant must complete the General Manager
Leadership Class training to our satisfaction no later than 60 days before your Restaurant’s opening
date and, if the General Manager is hired after your Restaurant’s opening, no later than 6 months after
assuming the management position. You must pay us $250 per attendee upon registration in the
General Manager Leadership Class. You also must pay the salaries, travel, and living expenses for
all persons who attend training.
