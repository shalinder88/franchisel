                      Special Risks to Consider About This Franchise

Certain states require that the following risk(s) be highlighted:

       1.      Out-of-State Dispute Resolution. The franchise agreement and development
               agreement require you to resolve disputes with the franchisor by litigation only
               in the then-current county where the franchisor’s corporate headquarters are
               located, which is currently Atlanta, Georgia. Out-of-state litigation may force
               you to accept a less favorable settlement for disputes. It may also cost you more
               to litigate with the franchisor in its home state than in your own state.

Certain states may require other risks to be highlighted. Check the State Specific Addenda
(if any) to see whether your state requires other risks to be highlighted.
