## Pass B — Front Matter, Formats, Fees, Investment (Items 1–7)

Sonic Franchising LLC is a Delaware LLC formed March 23, 2011, as part of a securitization restructuring. The principal business address is Three Glenlake Parkway NE, Atlanta, Georgia 30328. The Sonic brand began in the early 1950s, franchising since 1974 (through predecessors).

## Pass D — Territory, Channels, Encroachment (Items 12–16)

Territory is NOT exclusive. The Protected Area has significant carve-outs:
1. Previously Protected Radius of existing Sonic Restaurants
2. Previously Protected Development Areas
3. Non-Drive-In Locations (C-Stores, gas stations, travel plazas)
4. Non-Traditional Locations (airports, military bases, stadiums, etc.)

Franchisor reserves rights to: (1) operate any brand in the Protected Area under different marks; (2) sell similar products through internet/catalog/telemarketing in the Protected Area; (3) offer dissimilar products under Sonic marks in Protected Area; (4) operate Sonic Restaurants anywhere outside Protected Area regardless of proximity; (5) be acquired by a competitor.

No restrictions on franchisee soliciting/accepting orders outside Protected Area, but franchisee cannot use internet/catalog/telemarketing. Protected Area may shrink based on census updates.

## Pass E — Contract Burden, Legal Mechanics (Item 17)

**Term:** 10 years (Traditional/Non-Drive-In); lesser of 10 years or facility contract (Non-Traditional). DA term: until last FA signed or last restaurant opens on schedule.

**Renewal:** 2 additional 10-year terms. Requirements: 6–12 months notice; pay $15,000 renewal fee; sign then-current FA (**which may contain materially different terms, including different fees and Protected Area**); remodel; sign general release; re-training.

**Termination by franchisee:** None — no right to terminate.

**Termination by franchisor:**
- **Non-curable (immediate):** Insolvency; failure to secure Site; unauthorized transfer; terrorism; conviction of felony/certain crimes; trademark/trade secret misuse; health/safety endangerment; false books; 3+ defaults in 12 months; termination of any other Sonic agreement.
- **Curable:** 10 or 30 days to cure.

**Transfer:** All transfers require Sonic approval. Conditions: good standing, amounts paid, transferee qualifies, training, transfer fee ($1,000–$3,000), restaurant upgrade, general release, new FA. ROFR on same terms as third-party offer. Option to purchase assets within 30 days post-expiration/termination.

**Death/disability:** Assignment within greater of 120 days or completion of probate.

**Noncompete (during term):** No involvement in any competing business.
**Noncompete (post-term):** 18 months, within 3 miles of former restaurant OR any other Sonic restaurant. Also cannot be landlord of competing business.

**Dispute resolution:** No arbitration. Litigation in courts of Atlanta, Georgia (county of headquarters). Georgia law governs. Subject to state-specific addenda overrides.
