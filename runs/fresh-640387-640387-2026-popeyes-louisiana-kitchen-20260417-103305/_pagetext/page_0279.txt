4.     Franchisee Advertising Contribution. Effective as of the Commencement Date, the
introductory paragraph of Section 3.02 of the Franchise Agreement, being the text before Sections
3.02.A through 3.02.H thereof (which Sections 3.02.A through 3.02.H remain unchanged by this
Amendment), is hereby deleted in its entirety and replaced with the following:

       “3.02 In addition to the payments provided for in Section 3.01 hereof, Franchisee,
       recognizing the value of advertising and the importance of the standardization of
       advertising and promotion to the goodwill and public image of the System, agrees
       to pay to the Popeyes Advertising Fund (“Advertising Fund”) a recurring, non-
       refundable advertising fund contribution (“Advertising Contribution””), payable
       weekly on Gross Sales for the preceding week (or on such other basis as may be set
       forth in the Manual or otherwise agreed to in writing by Franchisor). The
       Advertising Contribution shall be determined as set forth below in this Section 3.02
       and shall be expended by Franchisor in accordance with the conditions and
       limitations set forth in Sections 3.02.A through 3.02.H.

       (a)    Franchisee shall pay an Advertising Contribution amount of 5.0% of weekly
       Gross Sales commencing on the Commencement Date and continuing thereafter for
       the remainder of the Term, subject to modification as set forth in Sections
       3.02(a)(i), and (iii).

              (i)     If Franchisor determines that the Measurement Period 1 EBITDA
                      Condition was satisfied, Franchisee shall pay an Advertising
                      Contribution amount of 5.25% of weekly Gross Sales commencing
                      on the first day of the month following the month during which the
                      applicable Determination Date occurs and continuing thereafter for
                      the remainder of the Term, subject to further increase as set forth in
                      Section 3.02(b).

              (ii)    Intentionally Deleted.

              (iii)   If Franchisor determines that the Measurement Period 1 EBITDA
                      Condition was not satisfied, then Franchisee shall pay an
                      Advertising Contribution amount of 4.6% of weekly Gross Sales
                      commencing on the first day of the month following the month
                      during which the applicable Determination Date occurs and
                      continuing thereafter for the remainder of the Term, subject to
                      increase if approved by a majority vote of the Combined
                      Restaurants, as set forth in Section 3.02(d).
       (b)    If the Measurement Period 1 EBITDA Condition was satisfied, then this
       Section 3.02(b) shall apply.
