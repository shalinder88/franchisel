         Food court Popeyes Restaurants are located within the confines of food court locations, such as
free-standing food court buildings, malls, airports, travel plazas, amusement parks, military bases and
other retail areas where common seating with other food concepts is generally used. In many of the food
court Popeyes Restaurants, the Restaurant has no control over the days or hours the host location is open.

before closing.
