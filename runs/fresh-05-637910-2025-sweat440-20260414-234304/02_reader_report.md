## Pass A: Executive Snapshot

1. **Franchisor**: Sweat440 Franchise Systems, LLC, a Florida LLC formed November 2018; wholly-owned subsidiary of Brickhouse Athletics, LLC (parent). Principal office: 1919 Purdy Avenue, Miami Beach, FL 33139.
2. **Concept**: Membership-based fitness studios offering 40-minute group workouts with four 10-minute stations; clients can join every 10 minutes. Proprietary "Flex-Time Process."
3. **System size (Dec 31, 2024)**: 22 total studios — 17 franchised + 5 company-owned/officer-owned. 32 franchise agreements signed but not yet open (concentrated heavily in NJ — 27 from one operator).
4. **Leadership**: Cody Patrick (CEO, co-founder, since Nov 2018) and Matthew Miller (CBO, co-founder). Small team; short operating history flagged as special risk.
5. **Investment range**: $284,900 – $667,900 (single unit); $553,400 – $1,320,800 (2-pack MUD). Studio size: 2,500–3,500 sq ft.
6. **Fee burden**: Initial franchise fee $60,000; royalty 7% of Gross Sales or $350/wk minimum; brand fund $420/mo; software $695/mo; local advertising $1,200/mo min; marketing management $1,200/mo; call center $1,800/mo. Mandatory minimum payments flagged as special risk.
7. **Item 19**: 17 studios reported 2024 gross revenue ranging from $137,328 (Madison) to $1,452,077 (Miami-Brickell). Parent studios show EBITDA of $617K (South Beach) and $683K (Brickell) before imputed royalty; adjusted EBITDA $526K and $581K.
8. **Financial health**: Franchisor reports member's deficit of ($608,338) at Dec 31, 2024; net loss of ($59,056) for 14-month period. Total revenues grew to $1.36M from $1.03M. Cash position weak at $51,869. Financial condition flagged as special risk. Member took $415K distributions despite losses.
9. **Growth trajectory**: Net +4 studios in 2024 (down from +10 in 2023). 32 signed-not-open agreements suggest pipeline, but 28 belong to a single NJ operator (concentration risk). 1 termination in Alabama in 2024.
10. **Territory**: Protected territory = lesser of 3-mile radius or 50,000 people. Franchisor retains broad reserved rights including internet sales, dissimilar channels, captive market locations.
11. **Renewal**: 10-year initial term, 2 additional 5-year renewal terms. Renewal requires signing then-current agreement (may contain "materially different" terms), remodeling, general release, $10,000 successor fee.
12. **Disputes**: Litigation venue in Florida; Florida law governs. No arbitration/mediation clause. Waiver of jury trial and punitive damages. State addenda override for CA, IL, MD, MN, NY, VA, WA.
13. **Non-compete**: During term — broad prohibition on any competitive fitness business. Post-term — 2 years, 15-mile radius. CA addendum notes may not be enforceable.
14. **Special risks (state-required)**: (1) Out-of-state dispute resolution, (2) Short operating history, (3) Financial condition concerns, (4) Mandatory minimum payments, (5) Unopened franchises with significant backlog.

## Pass D: Territory, Channels, Encroachment (Items 12-16)

### Item 12 — Territory
**Franchise Agreement**:
- **Protected Territory**: Lesser of 3-mile radius or 50,000 people around Authorized Location. Exclusive within that area (no other SWEAT440 studios).
- **Designated Search Area**: Non-exclusive search zone if no location at signing; must find approved site within 180 days or face termination.
- **Franchisor reserved rights** (7 categories): (1) Studios outside Protected Territory; (2) Promote/sell memberships, products, services; (3) Modify product/service offerings; (4) Ancillary products via dissimilar channels (direct mail, wholesale, electronic); (5) Internet/social media promotion; (6) Develop other business systems using Marks or other names; (7) Substitute trademarks.
- **Internet restriction on franchisee**: Cannot conduct business or advertise on Internet without written authorization.
- **No options, rights of first refusal, or similar rights to acquire additional franchises**.
- **Relocation**: Written consent required; $5,000+ fee.

**Multi-Unit Development Agreement**:
- **Designated Area**: Protected development rights based on population density and intended studios.
- **Protection expires**: On earlier of final studio lease signing or last day of Development Schedule.
- **Captive Market Locations**: Franchisor can operate in malls, airports, hospitals, universities, etc. within Designated Area.
- **No exclusive territory statement**: "You will not receive an exclusive territory."

### Item 13 — Trademarks
- **Principal marks**: SWEAT440 (two federal registrations on Principal Register — Reg. 5567218 and 5690024)
- **Owned by**: Brickhouse Athletics, LLC (parent); licensed to franchisor via perpetual Trademark License Agreement (Jun 3, 2019)
- **Termination of license**: If franchisor breaches; existing sublicenses (franchises) survive
- **No material determinations, pending proceedings, or infringing uses**

### Item 14 — Patents, Copyrights, Proprietary Information

### Item 15 — Obligation to Participate
- **Studio Manager**: Required for each studio; can be franchisee or approved designee; must complete training
- **Operating Principal**: If franchisee is entity, must remain active in oversight; has signing authority
- **Regional Manager**: Required for MUD; full-time; may oversee up to 5 studios
- **Replacement deadline**: 30 days if Studio Manager dies/disabled/terminates; if not replaced, franchisor appoints manager at $2,000/wk or 20% of Gross Sales
- **Principal Owner**: 5%+ owners must sign Guaranty and Assumption of Obligations
- **Entities**: May not engage in any business other than SWEAT440 studio ownership/operation (under MUD)

### Item 16 — Restrictions on What Franchisee May Sell

## Pass E: Contract Burden, Legal Mechanics (Item 17)

### Franchise Agreement
- **Term**: 10 years
- **Renewal**: 2 additional 5-year terms if: still offering franchises; 9-6 months notice; in compliance; meet new franchisee requirements; complete refresher training; sign then-current agreement (**may contain materially different terms**); remodel; maintain premises; licenses current; pay $10,000 successor fee; sign general release
- **Termination by franchisee**: Not applicable (no contractual right)
- **Termination by franchisor without cause**: Not applicable
- **Curable defaults (various cure periods)**: 30 days for most operational defaults; 10 days for cross-default acceleration ($200K+); 5 days for fee payment after notice; 72 hours for health/safety
- **Non-curable defaults**: 3+ violations in 12 months; material misrepresentation; felony; government action; insolvency; abandonment; threat to public health; lease expiration; audit understatement >5%; fail sales minimums 2+ times in 12 months; 3 audits showing >2% understatement in 36 months; termination of MUD or other agreement
- **Post-termination obligations**: Cease operations and mark use; pay all amounts due; return manuals; assign phone numbers; de-identify; cease using confidential information; sell back products; no customer diversion for 2 years
- **Assignment by franchisor**: Freely assignable; assignee assumes obligations
- **Transfer by franchisee**: Requires franchisor approval (not unreasonably withheld); extensive conditions including training, fees, compliance, general release, no retained security interest
- **Right of first refusal**: Franchisor can match any offer; 30 days to exercise; 60 days to close
- **Purchase option on termination/expiration**: Assets at book value
- **Death/disability**: Estate must assign to approved buyer within 6 months
- **Non-compete during term**: Broad — no involvement in any competitive fitness business
- **Non-compete post-term**: 2 years; 15-mile radius of studio or any existing SWEAT440 studio; includes e-commerce within 15-mile radius
- **No arbitration/mediation**
- **Venue**: Florida (where corporate HQ is located) — subject to state addenda
- **Governing law**: Florida (without conflict of laws) — subject to state addenda
- **Jury trial waiver**: Yes
- **Punitive damages waiver**: Yes

### Multi-Unit Development Agreement
- **Term**: Through last day of Development Schedule
- **No renewal**
- **Termination by franchisor**: Curable: 10 days for fees, 30 days for other breaches. Non-curable: similar to FA but includes failure to have Regional Manager for 30+ days
- **Cross-default**: Any FA or other agreement termination triggers MUD termination
- **Transfer**: Similar conditions to FA; transfer fee $5,000/unmet development obligation
- **Non-compete**: Identical to FA (2 years, 15 miles)
- **Venue/law**: Florida, same as FA
