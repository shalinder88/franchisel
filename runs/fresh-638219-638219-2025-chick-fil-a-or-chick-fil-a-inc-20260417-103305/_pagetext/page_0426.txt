      Plan Assets
      Plan assets are invested using a total rate of return approach to preserve asset values, diversify
      risk and achieve the expected long-term rate of return. The Company’s expected long-term rate of
      return on plan assets assumption of 7.0% for each of the fiscal years presented in these financial
      statements was determined using a weighted average approach considering the mix of plan assets
      at the determination date and long-term forward-looking expected returns for each asset category.
      Plan assets allocated by asset category approximate target allocations, and consist of equity
      securities (51.30%), fixed income securities (26.32%), cash and cash equivalents (17.65%),
      alternative investments and derivative instruments (4.72%) and private equity (.01%) as of
      December 31, 2024.
