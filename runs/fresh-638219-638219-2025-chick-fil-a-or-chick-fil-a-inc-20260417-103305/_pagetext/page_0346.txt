                                              BACKGROUND

        A.       On or about _________, Chick-fil-A and the Operator-Owner entered into a Franchise
Agreement with its attached Lease relating to the licensing and operation of the Chick-fil-A at __________,
(address) (the “Franchise Agreement”);

        B.      On or about _________, 20___ the Operator-Owner, after obtaining all required approvals
and consents under the Franchise Agreement transferred and assigned his or her interest in the Franchise
Agreement and the Business(es) for their remaining respective Agreed Terms to the Operator, an entity
formed by the Operator-Owner for his or her convenience of ownership, pursuant to the terms and
conditions of an Assignment and Consent to Assignment (the “Assignment”).

         C.       Chick-fil-A has developed a delivery-focused location program (the “DK Program”) for, among
other things, the fulfillment of authorized catering, customer delivery and other orders under a Delivery Kitchen
Agreement (“Orders”) through centralized delivery focused locations established or leased by Chick-fil-A and
leased or subleased to franchised Operators that feature a purely or overwhelmingly digital ordering experience
and minimal to no direct customer service at the restaurant (the “DK”) to, among other things, fulfill directly
orders received by (i) the DK and (ii) the Operators’ respective designated business(es).

         D.       Operator and Operator-Owner desire to have Operator be authorized, and Chick-fil-A is willing
to authorize the Operator, to operate a DK as an Additional Business under the Franchise Agreement, subject to
special terms and conditions applicable to the DK.

        E.       Pursuant to the terms of the Franchise Agreement, Chick-fil-A, the Operator and the
Operator-Owner desire to amend and otherwise reaffirm the terms of the Franchise Agreement (including
any amendments and exhibits to the Franchise Agreement) and the Assignment pursuant to the terms and
conditions of this Additional Business Amendment (and the Delivery Kitchen Agreement attached hereto),
including the designation of an Additional Business described in this Amendment to be operated under the
Franchise Agreement, and to otherwise continue their respective performance under the Franchise
Agreement and the Assignment.
