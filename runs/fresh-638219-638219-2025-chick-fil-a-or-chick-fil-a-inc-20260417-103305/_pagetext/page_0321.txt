                EXHIBIT “A” TO ADDITIONAL BUSINESS AMENDMENT FOR

                                 DELIVERY KITCHEN AGREEMENT

        THIS DELIVERY KITCHEN AGREEMENT (this “Agreement”) for the Chick-fil-A Restaurant
Site described in this Agreement is made by and between CHICK-FIL-A, INC., a Georgia corporation, as
franchisor having its principal business address at 5200 Buffington Road, Atlanta, Georgia 30349-2998
(“Chick-fil-A”), and ______________________, franchisee (the “Operator”), and will be effective as of
the ___ day of __________, 20___.

                                                BACKGROUND

        A.       Chick-fil-A is currently a party to a certain delivery kitchen license agreement attached as
Exhibit C as it may be amended from time to time together with all its exhibits and schedules (the “DK
Lease”) with the third-party prime lessor, ground lessor or licensor of the Site identified in the DK Lease
(“Licensor”). Under the DK Lease, Licensor granted Chick-fil-A the right to conduct a restaurant business
and/or a license to operate one or more Chick-fil-A® delivery kitchens at the Site, consisting of the delivery
kitchen location(s) identified in the DK Summary data sheet (the “DK Summary”) attached as Exhibit A as
it may be amended from time to time (the “Site”). The Site is sometimes also referred to in this Agreement
as the “DK Location(s).”

         B.     The Operator is a franchised Chick-fil-A® Restaurant business operator holding a valid
Franchise Agreement (together with its attached Lease(s) and in the case of any Captive Venue Unit(s), its
attached Concession Sublicense Agreement(s) and their attached Concession Agreement(s), any other
existing Additional Business Amendment(s), and all other exhibits, schedules and amendments, collectively
the “Franchise Agreement”), and is entering into this Agreement the same date that the Additional Business
Amendment for Delivery Kitchen to which this Agreement is attached (the “Additional Business
Amendment”) to operate the Delivery Kitchen Unit (as defined below) as an Additional Business (the
Franchise Agreement, this attached Agreement and all other exhibits and amendments, collectively the
“Franchise Agreement”).

          C.      Chick-fil-A desires to, as applicable, lease, sublease or sublicense its rights and privileges under
the DK Lease to the Operator, and the Operator desires to accept the applicable lease, sublease or sublicense
and faithfully perform Chick-fil-A’s obligations under the DK Lease as part of a set of rights granted by Chick-
fil-A to the Operator under the Franchise Agreement to operate a certain quick-service food business at the DK
Location(s) as a Business that is a Delivery Kitchen Unit under the trade name “Chick-fil-A®” pursuant to the
Franchise Agreement. The Business that is operated pursuant to the Franchise Agreement, the Additional
Business Amendment, and this Agreement (the “Business”), is sometimes also referred to in this Agreement as
the “DK” or “Delivery Kitchen Unit.”

         D.       This Agreement, its attached DK Lease and the Additional Business Amendment to which they
are attached are a part of and are incorporated by reference into the Franchise Agreement. As the Delivery
Kitchen Unit is an Additional Business under the Franchise Agreement, this Agreement and its attached DK
Lease is the applicable Exhibit “A” to the Additional Business Amendment that is used for Delivery Kitchen
Units in place of any Lease for the DK.
