Additional Notes:

Prime Lease and Calculation of Base Rent and Percentage Rent

This Site is subject to an underlying Prime Lease (attached as Exhibit “B” hereto) under which the prime lessee is
Chick-fil-A or a wholly-owned subsidiary of Chick-fil-A. Base Rent and Percentage Rent (if any) are payable to
Chick-fil-A for the Site per the terms of the Franchise Agreement and this Lease which incorporates the Prime
Lease. The Base Rent and Percentage Rent (if any) could increase when and if, among other things, the rent under
the Prime Lease increases pursuant to the terms of such Prime Lease. Percentage Rent is calculated based on the
terms of the Prime Lease. Notwithstanding the definition of Gross Receipts in the Franchise Agreement and Section
3.2 of this Lease, Percentage Rent payable to Chick-fil-A shall be calculated based on the definition of “gross sales”
as defined in the Prime Lease.

Prime Lease

The underlying Prime Lease for this Site is subject to amendment, renewal and/or renegotiation with the landlord for
the Site during the term of your Franchise Agreement and this Lease. As a result, your monthly Lease payments to
Chick-fil-A could change, including without limitation, the calculation of Monthly Base Rent, Percentage Rent, and
Additional Charges and Expenses (as such term is defined in Section 1 of this Lease) (which include, without
limitation, taxes, common area maintenance (CAM) charges and other additional or extra charges and rents).


Additional Charges and Expenses

In addition to Rent, your monthly Lease payments to Chick-fil-A for this Site are subject to and will include any
Additional Charges and Expenses due under the Lease (as such term is defined in Section 1 and as more specifically
described in Section 3.3 of this Lease). Additional Charges and Expenses due under the Lease include, without
limitation, taxes, common area maintenance (CAM) charges, and other additional or extra charges and rents.
