## Key Observations
1. **Unique ownership model:** CFA owns/leases all premises and equipment; operator invests only $10,000 initial franchise fee.
2. **Fee structure is profit-sharing:** 15% of GR as base operating service fee + 50% of net profit as additional operating service fee.
3. **No protected territory.** CFA retains full discretion on all location development.
4. **Termination without cause:** 30 days notice by either party. Auto-termination on death/disability.
5. **0 transfers in 3 years** — no secondary market for franchises.
6. **Gag clause present:** Former operators signed provisions restricting ability to speak openly.
7. **Massive system:** 2,629 franchised restaurants + 55 company-owned + 425 licensed units at end of 2024.
8. **Strong unit economics:** Non-mall average $9.32M in annual sales.
