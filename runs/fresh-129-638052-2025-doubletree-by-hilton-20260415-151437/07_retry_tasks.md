**Rationale:** Coverage audit identified this as the only material gap. Item 17 summary table did not explicitly address jury waiver.

**Result:** FOUND. FA Section 17.1 (PDF page 179, Exhibit D) contains a mutual jury trial waiver. FA Section 17.2 contains a mutual punitive damages waiver.

**State overrides:**
- **Minnesota:** Section 17.1 deleted in its entirety (Minn. Rule 2860-4400J prohibits jury waiver).
- **New York (North Dakota addendum):** Section 17.1 requiring jury waiver deleted.
- **Illinois:** Jury waiver "shall not constitute a waiver of any right conferred upon Franchisee by the Illinois Franchise Disclosure Act."
- **Wisconsin:** Provisions restricting or limiting the right to a jury trial "may not be enforceable."
- **Minnesota FDD addendum:** Notes that requiring waiver of jury trial or requiring consent to liquidated damages may not be enforceable.
