## Omissions and Gaps

### Material Gaps
1. **No cost build-up or EBITDA in Item 19**: Revenue-only FPR. This is the franchisor's disclosure choice, not an extraction gap. The FDD explicitly does not disclose expenses.
2. **Guarantor Monetary Threshold specific amounts**: Referenced as being in Exhibit B-1 of FA. Not disclosed in FDD body. This is by design — amounts are negotiated per deal.

### Minor Gaps
3. **Insurance requirements**: Detailed in FA Article 9 and System Standards, not in FDD body. Standard for hotel FDDs.
4. **FF&E reserve fund audit data**: 5% monthly deposit required but no fund balance or audit data in FDD. Standard gap.

## Unresolveds
1. **Item 19 ADR statistics anomaly**: The Owned/Managed "#/% Exceeding Average" for ADR shows 13/40.6% — this denominator (32) appears to reference all hotels rather than the 12 owned/managed hotels. Severity: low. This may be a formatting artifact in the original FDD.
2. **AOP Term variability**: The AOP Term is "typically" 1-5 years but is negotiated per deal. A prospective franchisee should clarify the specific AOP Term they would receive before signing. Severity: medium (business risk, not extraction gap).

- None identified between FDD sections or between text and tables.
