## D. Contradictions and Unresolveds Preserved Correctly

1. **Carrols reacquisition accounting** — Item 20 shows 1,061 reacquired by franchisor in Table 3 but only 1,023 explicitly mentioned as Carrols. The difference (~38) could be other reacquisitions. Noted but not fully reconciled.

2. **Ceased operations count** — Exhibit O3 lists 1,433 franchisees, but Table 3 shows only 118 (49 non-renewals + 69 ceased ops other) for 2024. The 1,061 Carrols reacquisitions plus carryover from definition (includes those who did not communicate within 10 weeks) likely explains the gap. Preserved as noted.

3. **Item 18 status** — Cannot confirm whether Item 18 says "No public figures" or discloses figures. Preserved as unresolved.

4. **Supply chain economics** — BKC acknowledges revenue from supply chain but does not quantify. This is standard FDD practice but is an unresolved for diligence purposes.
