# Reader Report: Rytech Franchising, Inc. FDD (637915-2025)

## Pass A: Executive Snapshot

1. **Franchisor**: Rytech Franchising, Inc., Georgia corporation (incorporated November 25, 1996), wholly-owned subsidiary of RyBrands, Inc. (FL corp). CEO: William D. Ryan (since 1996). Headquarters: Kennesaw, GA.
2. **Business**: Water damage restoration, applied structural drying, mold remediation, fire/smoke damage, contents cleaning — insurance restoration services franchise. Competes with ServiceMaster, SERVPRO, and similar restoration companies.
3. **System Size**: 92 franchised outlets + 2 company-owned at end of 2024. Net growth: +4 franchised in 2024 (10 opened, 6 terminated, 1 ceased). Present in 26 states + DC.
4. **Investment Range**: $166,500–$258,100 total. Initial franchise fee $60,000 (base, up to $85,000 for larger territories). Home-based start permitted.
5. **Fee Burden**: Royalty 8% of gross revenues ($2,000/month minimum after 12 months); Administrative Fee 11% of gross revenues (to affiliate Rytech Admin Services); National Ad Fund 1% (max 3%); Local advertising $1,000/month or 4%, whichever greater. Total ongoing fee load: 20%+ of gross revenues before local ad spend.
6. **Item 19**: FPR provided — 5-year gross revenue data (2020-2024). 2024: 80 qualifying franchisees, average $731,214, median $467,724. Only 22 (28%) met or exceeded average. Range: $74,010–$4,975,620. Revenue-only; no costs/expenses disclosed.
7. **Financial Health**: Total revenue $4.48M (2024), up from $3.63M (2023). Net income $1.21M (2024). However, distributions of $1.265M exceeded net income, creating accumulated deficit of ($36,361). Cash position $792K, down from $1.02M.
8. **Territory**: Protected zip-code blocks of ~400K population. Not exclusive — national accounts, catastrophic events, and non-participation override territory protections.
9. **Contract Term**: 10 years initial, 5-year renewals. Renewal fee: 10% of then-current initial franchise fee. Must sign then-current agreement with potentially materially different terms.
10. **Noncompete**: During term + 2 years post-term within territory, 10 miles of border, and within/near any Rytech territory. Broad scope.
11. **Dispute Resolution**: Negotiation → mediation → arbitration, in Cobb County, Georgia. Georgia law governs. State addenda override for CA, IL, IN, MD, MI, MN, NY, VA, WA.
12. **Litigation/Bankruptcy**: None disclosed.
13. **Special Risks**: (1) Out-of-state dispute resolution (Georgia), (2) Mandatory minimum royalty/ad payments regardless of revenue.
14. **Affiliated Revenue**: Rytech Admin Services (11% admin fee) — CEO William D. Ryan is majority shareholder of parent RyBrands, which wholly owns both the franchisor and Admin Services. Admin Services revenue from franchisees: $5.72M in 2024.

- **NOT dependent on sales volumes** or market penetration
- **Encroachment risks**:

## Pass E: Contract Burden, Legal Mechanics (Item 17)

### Term and Renewal
- **Initial term**: 10 years
- **Renewal**: Available if approved; 180-day advance notice required
- **Renewal term**: 5 years
- **Renewal conditions**: (a) substantial compliance, (b) current renewal requirements met within 60 days, (c) sign then-current franchise agreement + guaranty + confidentiality agreement, (d) sign general release, (e) pay renewal fee, (f) sign general release
- **Materially different terms risk**: You may be asked to sign a contract with materially different terms; however territory boundaries remain the same and renewal royalty will not exceed that imposed on similarly situated renewing franchisees

### Termination
- **By franchisee**: (a) mutual written agreement, or (b) franchisor fails to cure breach within 60 days of notice
- **By franchisor without cause**: Not applicable
- **By franchisor with cause — curable**: Failure to provide reports, obtain non-competes, complete training, operate per Manual, offer core services, attend 2+ franchise meetings/year, perform background checks, make payments, maintain insurance, maintain license, meet minimum sales ($500K by month 24)
- **By franchisor with cause — non-curable (automatic)**: Bankruptcy, insolvency, receivership, dissolution, levy
- **By franchisor with cause — non-curable (on notice)**: Misuse marks/system, abandon business, illegal operation, material misrepresentation, unauthorized assignment, confidential info disclosure, repeated Manual non-compliance, repeated breach, felony conviction, unauthorized territory intrusion

### Transfer
- No transfer without approval (except to wholly-owned entity)
- Transfer conditions: transferee applies and meets standards, not a competitor, signs current agreements, general release, completes training, all defaults cured and amounts paid
- **Right of first refusal**: Franchisor can match any offer
- **Death/disability**: 90 days for estate to assign to approved buyer or heir to demonstrate qualification

### Post-Termination Obligations
- Pay all amounts owed
- Return all confidential information, Manual, trademarked items
- Cease using marks, confidential info, trade secrets
- Cancel assumed names, transfer phone numbers
- De-identify premises
- Cease operating Rytech office
- Assist in transition of services for carriers and customers

### Non-Competition
- **During term**: No competing business; no diversion of carriers, customers, employees; no hiring of carrier reps or employees of franchisor/affiliates/other franchisees
- **Post-term** (2 years): No competing business within (a) your territory, (b) 10 miles of your territory border, (c) any Rytech franchised or company territory, (d) 10 miles of border of any Rytech territory. Same diversion/hiring restrictions.
- Subject to state law — several state addenda limit or void noncompete

### Dispute Resolution
- Direct negotiations first
- Non-binding mediation (concluded within 60 days)
- Binding arbitration (if unresolved) — except for known monetary amounts and injunctive relief
- **Venue**: Cobb County, Georgia
- **Governing law**: Georgia
- **Subject to state law** — multiple state addenda override

### Other Contract Provisions
- **Modification**: Written by both sides, but franchisor may change Operations Manual unilaterally
- **Integration clause**: Only terms of Franchise Agreement binding; outside representations may not be enforceable (subject to state law)
- **Franchisor assignment**: No restriction on franchisor's right to transfer
