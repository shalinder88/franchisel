and all other income of any kind whatsoever derived directly or indirectly in connection with Your 
operation of Your Franchised Business, the Marks, Your operation under the Marks or any aspect of the 
System, but excluding taxes actually paid to a governmental authority, and refunds actually made to 
Customers.
