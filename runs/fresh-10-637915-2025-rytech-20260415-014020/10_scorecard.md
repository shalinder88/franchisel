## Risk Flags

1. **Very high ongoing fee burden**: 20%+ of gross revenues (8% royalty + 11% admin + 1% ad fund) before any local advertising. This is among the highest total fee loads in franchise systems.
2. **Admin fee to affiliated entity**: The 11% admin fee flows to Rytech Admin Services, controlled by the same CEO/owner.
3. **Median revenue declining**: 2024 median ($467,724) is the lowest in the 5-year series, despite average increasing.
4. **Distributions exceeding net income**: Company moved to accumulated deficit in 2024 ($36K) after distributing $1.265M on $1.208M net income.
5. **Florida termination spike**: 4 of 6 terminations in 2024 were in Florida, the largest state.
6. **Materially different terms on renewal**: Franchisee may face materially different terms at renewal including increased royalty.
7. **Broad noncompete**: 2-year post-term restriction covering own territory + 10 miles + all Rytech territories + 10 miles.
