1.
Services. As part of Hyatt’s Global Property Guest Services Program, Hyatt or its designee shall 
provide the following services (check all that apply): 
☐Foreign Independent Travelers (FIT): Hyatt will manually add reservations on an as needed 
basis. 
☐HySAT Reviews: Hyatt or its subcontractors will engage post-stay online surveys on the 
following sites – Expedia, hotels.com, booking.com, google, and trip advisor, or other sites as Hyatt 
may indicate from time to time. 
☐PBX Services: Hyatt will accept and respond to guest calls that are routed to the Property front 
desk & handle basic requests in HotSOS, in accordance with regularly scheduled service hours as 
reasonably determined by Hyatt. Property agrees to reasonably cooperate with Hyatt so that Hyatt 
may give guests high quality service in accordance with brand guidelines and Hyatt industry standard.  
☐GSB/Rooming List Support: Contractor will build group blocks and/or upload rooming lists on 
an as needed basis. This includes support and changes for the life of the group along with EOI 
Property support.  
☐Post Stay Review Support: Contractor will engage post-stay Medallia surveys on behalf of 
Property.
