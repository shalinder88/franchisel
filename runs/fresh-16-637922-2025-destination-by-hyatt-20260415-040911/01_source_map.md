## Risk Flags (Early)
1. **Massive investment**: $45.8M–$253.8M (luxury resort hotels, 150–500 rooms)
2. **No FPR**: Item 19 does not provide financial performance representations
3. **Out-of-state dispute resolution**: Arbitration/litigation in Illinois
4. **Territory expires**: Area of Protection limited to AOP Term (1–5 years post-opening)
5. **Royalty on channel bookings only**: 7% of Gross Rooms Revenue from Channel Bookings (excludes OTA bookings)
6. **Heavy system services burden**: 3.5% commercial services + 4.5% WoH loyalty + 1.35% digital acquisition
7. **Liquidated damages**: Up to 60 months × (3.5% + 7%) of Average Monthly Revenue; 150% if Consequential Termination
8. **Very small franchise system**: Only 4 franchised Brand Hotels as of Dec 31, 2024
9. **Renewal may be materially different**: Then-current form of agreement
10. **No post-term noncompete**: But cannot be Competing Brand Owner during term
