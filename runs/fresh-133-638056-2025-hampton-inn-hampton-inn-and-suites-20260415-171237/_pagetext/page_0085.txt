RevPAR Index - The RevPAR Index measures a hotel’s RevPAR (revenue per available room) relative 
to an aggregated grouping of hotels (competitive set, market, tract, etc.). An index of 100 represents a 
fair share compared to the aggregated group of hotels. An index greater than 100 represents more than 
fair share of the aggregated group’s performance.  
 
The RevPAR Index is calculated as follows: 
  
(Hotel RevPAR / Comp Set RevPAR) x 100 = RevPAR Index.
