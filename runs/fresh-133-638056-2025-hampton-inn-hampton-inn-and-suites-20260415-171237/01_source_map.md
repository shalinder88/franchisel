## Early Risk Flags
1. **Out-of-state dispute resolution** — mediation/arbitration/litigation in Virginia; if court rejects, New York. Franchisor may sue where hotel is located. (p. 4)
2. **Michigan franchise investment law** — specific prohibitions on unfair provisions highlighted (pp. 5–6)
3. **No renewal right** — franchise is non-renewable; re-licensing at franchisor's sole discretion with potentially materially different terms (Item 17)
4. **Encroachment** — standard franchise agreement grants NO exclusive territory; franchisor retains broad rights to compete (Item 12)
5. **Liquidated damages on early termination** — franchisee cannot terminate before term expiration without paying liquidated damages (Item 17)
