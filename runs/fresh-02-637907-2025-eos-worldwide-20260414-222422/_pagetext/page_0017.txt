Monthly Membership 
Fee(2)  
As of April 2025, Monthly Membership 
Fees are as follows: 
$1,395 for all franchisees designated in 
the “Professional”, “Legacy” or 
“Emeritus” tier. 
$1,495 for all franchisees designated in 
the “Certified” tier. 
$1,695 for all franchisees designated in 
the “Expert” tier. 
We may increase the Monthly 
Membership Fees from time to time, 
but will not increase these fees more 
than once in any calendar year and any 
incremental fee increase will not exceed 
$200 per month ($2,400 annually) in 
any calendar year during the initial term 
of your Franchise Agreement.

Brand Development 
Fund (3) 
Currently we do not have a brand 
marketing fund (“Brand Development 
Fund”). 
We may however establish a Brand 
Development Fund in the future, but we 
will limit the initial fee to no more than 
$100 per month, and will not increase 
the fee more than once in a calendar 
year and any incremental fee increase 
will not exceed $100 per month ($1,200 
annually) in any calendar year during 
the initial term of your Franchise 
Agreement. 
Determined if and 
when the Brand 
Development Fund is 
established, likely 
would be payable in the 
same manner and at the 
same time as the 
Monthly Membership 
Fee. 
We reserve the right to establish 
the Brand Development Fund and 
increase its fee, in each case upon 
sixty (60) days’ notice to you.
