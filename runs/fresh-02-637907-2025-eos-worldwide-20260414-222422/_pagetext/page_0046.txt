We do not require minimum availability of any Implementer, or track availability of any Implementer. 
Except to maintain tier status, Implementers may set their own schedules and fees and perform sessions in 
their discretion without any requirement to allocate a minimum number of hours or days to their EOS 
Franchised Business. 
All EOS Implementers must: 
● Graduate Boot Camp  
● Follow the guidelines provided in Base Camp and the Operations Manual 
● Share EOS Worldwide’s Vision (Core Values, Core Focus, Core Target, Marketing Strategy and 
3-Year Picture) 
● GWC the EOS Implementer “seat” and exhibit continual growth towards EOS mastery 
Criteria to Maintain Professional EOS Implementer Designation: 
 
● At the end of your first five quarters, including your Boot Camp quarter, you must have 
implemented at least four pure Focus Days, with a minimum session fee of $2,500* 
● Positively participated in QCEs during two of the previous four quarters. One QCE must be in-
person.** 
● At the beginning of your eighth full quarter and every quarter thereafter, you must have 
implemented at least 20 pure EOS session days in your prior four full quarters with a minimum 
session fee of $3,000*, and achieved an average session rating of 8.75+ in at least two of your prior 
four full quarters. 
If you fail to meet the Professional Designation criteria as of any measurement date above, you will move 
into the Cure. When in the Cure, you have two full quarters with the help of available support to meet the 
Professional Designation criteria on a trailing four quarter basis. 
Additional Criteria to Become a Certified EOS Implementer: 
To earn and use the designation “Certified EOS Implementer,” members of our Professional EOS 
Implementer Community must also: 
● Have implemented 10+ pure Focus Days & at least one of each of the other four pure EOS sessions 
(Vision Building Day 1, Vision Building Day 2, Quarterly & Annual) 
● Have implemented 80+ lifetime EOS pure session days 
● Have achieved an average session rating of 8.75+ in three of your prior four full quarters 
● Positively participated in QCEs during three of the previous four quarters with at least two QCEs 
in-person.** 
Criteria to Maintain Certified Designation: 
● Have implemented 30+ EOS pure session days in your prior four full quarters 
● Have achieved an average session rating of 8.75+ in three of your prior four full quarters 
● Positively participated in QCEs during three of the previous four quarters. Two QCEs must be in-
person.** 
Certified EOSIs will downgrade to Professional if at any measurement period you do not meet designation 
requirements. 
Additional Criteria to Become an Expert EOS Implementer:  
Expert designation will be given to EOS Implementers who meet the following criteria: 
● Met the designation criteria for Certified EOS Implementer 
● Implemented 500+ lifetime EOS pure session days
