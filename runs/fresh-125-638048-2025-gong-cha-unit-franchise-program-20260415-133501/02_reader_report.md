# Reader Report — Gong cha USA Franchising, LLC (638048-2025)

## Pass A: Executive Snapshot

1. **Brand:** Gong cha — Taiwanese-style bubble tea franchise founded 2006 in Kaohsiung, Taiwan; 2,162 stores in 27 global markets as of Dec 31, 2024.
2. **Franchisor entity:** Gong cha USA Franchising, LLC, Delaware LLC formed Jan 10, 2023. Very young franchisor (< 3 years old). Began offering unit/multi-unit franchises Oct 2023.
3. **Ownership chain:** Gong cha USA Franchising, LLC → Gong cha Americas, Inc. (GCA) → Gong Cha Global Limited (GCG, UK) → GC Group Midco → GC Group Holdco → Gong Cha Limited.
4. **US system size:** 235 total US stores (end 2024): 4 direct unit franchisees, 198 third-party subfranchisees, 31 master-franchisee/affiliate-owned, 2 company-owned. System grew from 187 to 229 (master+franchisor) in 2024, net +5. Growth decelerating from +40 (2022) to +37 (2023) to +5 (2024).
5. **Investment range:** $184,750 to $627,060 total. Initial franchise fee $34,500. Leasehold improvements are the biggest variable ($51,500–$270,500).
6. **Fee burden:** 6% royalty + 1% brand marketing (can increase to 2%) + up to 2% regional marketing + up to 2% local advertising = potential 12% of Net Sales in aggregate fees and spending.
7. **Item 19 (FPR):** 203 stores reporting full 2024 — average Net Sales $439,522; median $401,211; range $72,037–$1,119,281. 42.36% at or above average. No cost data disclosed.
8. **Bottom-quartile risk:** Bottom 52 stores averaged $227,558; low of $72,037 — a store at $227K paying 6% royalty + 1% brand marketing = ~$15.9K in royalties/marketing alone, before COGS and rent.
9. **Financial health:** Franchisor total revenue $4.1M (2024), net income $361,302. Total assets $2.17M, member's equity $1.015M. Very small, young entity. $3.6M GCCA acquisition as subsequent event.
10. **Growth trajectory:** The franchisor is actively buying out master franchisees (GCCA in CA, DRSAM in 13 states) and converting subfranchises to direct franchises, signaling a consolidation phase.
11. **Territory:** Up to 2-mile radius, not exclusive. Significant franchisor reservation of rights (Non-Traditional Sites, internet, alternative brands, acquisitions).
12. **Renewal:** 1x10-year renewal. Must sign new FA with "materially different" terms, remodel, pay 50% of then-current franchise fee, execute general release.
13. **Dispute resolution:** Mediation then arbitration in Massachusetts. Massachusetts law governs. Subject to state-specific overrides.
14. **Special risks (state-flagged):** (a) Out-of-state dispute resolution (MA), (b) spousal liability via guaranty, (c) short operating history.
15. **Litigation:** Three resolved matters — CA registration violation by GCI (consent order, $7,500 penalties); GCCA renewal dispute (settled, no damages); MD/VA registration violations ($30K + $3K penalties). All stem from predecessor/affiliate activities.

## Pass D: Territory, Channels, Encroachment (Items 12–16)

### Item 12 — Territory
- **Protected territory:** Up to 2-mile radius around Store (typical).
- **Protection:** No other Gong cha store within Territory during FA term if in compliance.
- **Not exclusive.** Significant carve-outs:
  - Non-Traditional Sites (airports, stadiums, malls, hospitals, etc.) excluded from Territory
  - Franchisor can operate or license stores outside Territory without limit
  - Franchisor can sell products via grocery, internet, mail order within Territory

## Pass E: Contract Burden, Legal Mechanics (Item 17)

### Term and Renewal
- **Initial term:** 10 years (FA); until end of Development Schedule (ADA)
- **Renewal:** 1x10-year renewal (FA only). Conditions:
  - Written notice
  - Good standing, no default
  - Sign new FA (may have "materially different" terms)
  - Comply with then-current qualification/training requirements
  - Remodel Store to then-current standards
  - Evidence of property control
  - Execute general release
  - Pay Renewal Fee (50% of then-current standard IFF)
- **"Materially different" risk:** Renewal FA may have different terms — a standard but significant risk.

### Termination
- **By franchisee:** Not applicable (no termination right).
- **By franchisor without cause:** Not applicable.
- **By franchisor with cause:** Numerous triggers.
  - **Curable (30-day cure, 7 days for monetary/inspection/operating hours):** Most defaults.
  - **Non-curable (immediate):** Failure to open on time, fraud/conviction, public health threat, loss of premises/license, unauthorized transfer, false books, confidentiality breach, non-compete breach, mark misuse, repeated defaults (3+ in 12 months), abandonment, insolvency, IP License termination.

### Transfer
- All transfers require franchisor approval.
- **Transferor conditions:** Current on obligations, not in default, pay Transfer Fee (50% of then-current IFF), sign release.
- **Transferee conditions:** Meet franchisor's financial/operational requirements, no conflicts, sign new FA (may be materially different), upgrade Store, complete training.
- Transfer fee: 50% of then-current standard IFF; ADA adds $5,000.
- **ROFR:** Franchisor can match any offer for Store or ownership interest.

### Death or Disability
- Death: assign within 12 months to franchisor-approved party.
- Permanent disability: assign within 6 months after notice.

### Noncompete
- **During term:** No involvement in any business primarily offering tea products or similar to Gong cha, anywhere. No diverting customers.
- **Post-term:** 2 years, no involvement in business primarily offering bubble tea/tea/smoothies/juices within 5-mile radius of your Store or any other Gong cha store.

### Dispute Resolution
- **Process:** Mediation first, then arbitration.
- **Forum:** Massachusetts (subject to state overrides).
- **Governing law:** Massachusetts (subject to state overrides).
- **Integration/merger clause:** Only written agreement terms binding; does not disclaim FDD representations.

### Other Provisions
- **Assignment by franchisor:** No restriction.
- **Modification:** No changes without mutual consent (but Manuals can change).
- **Option to purchase:** Franchisor can purchase inventory, supplies, equipment, signs, fixtures on termination/non-renewal.
