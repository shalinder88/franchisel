Note 1. 
This table is an estimate of your initial investment to start a single Store from a 
leased space that is 500 to 1,500 square feet under a unit Franchise Agreement.  We 
assume your Store will be an “in-line” store, typically in a strip mall or other retail

if they are in areas of high traffic in urban and suburban areas, and preferably near 
large residential communities, office buildings, and other commercial areas.  None 
of these payments are refundable (except as provided below).
