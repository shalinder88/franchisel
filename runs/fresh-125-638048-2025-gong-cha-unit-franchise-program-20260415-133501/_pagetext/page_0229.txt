(c)
In connection with the Privacy Policy, Franchisee, shall, within one year of the Effective Date, 
amend the Privacy Policy to include Master Franchisor and its Affiliates as persons with whom

Data based on legitimate interests as defined under applicable Data Protection Laws and shall 
co-operate and take all necessary actions to that end.
