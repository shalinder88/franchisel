NOTE 4.
CONCENTRATION OF CREDIT RISK
The Company places its cash, which may, at times, be in excess of Federal Deposit
Insurance Corporation with a major financial institution limits. Management believes
that this policy will limit the Company's exposure to credit risk. 
As of December 31, 2024, two franchisees accounted for approximately 84% of the
Company's accounts receivable. For the year ended, December 31, 2024, three
franchisees accounted for approximately 45% of the Company's total revenues.
As of December 31, 2023, two franchisees accounted for approximately 95% of the
Company's accounts receivable. For the period from January 10, 2023 (inception)
through December 31, 2023, two franchisees accounted for approximately 40% of the
Company's total revenues.
