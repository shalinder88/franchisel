NOTE 5.
RELATED-PARTY TRANSACTIONS (CONTINUED)
Common control transaction (continued)
A loan receivable was recorded at the transaction date for the net liabilities assumed by
the Company. The Licensor repaid the balance during the period ended December 31,
2023. The deferred revenues transferred represent the value of the remaining
performance obligations under the Agreements and will be amortized over the
remaining life of the Agreements, in accordance to the revenue recognition policy
described in Note 2.
Due from (to) parent
In the ordinary course of business, the Company receives funds from and advances
funds to the Parent. The amount due from (to) the Parent at December 31, 2024 and
2023, was $731,749 and $(655,437), respectively, and is included in ''Due from related
parties, net'' in the accompanying balance sheets. The amount due at December 31,
2024 was related to the service fee overpayment discussed above. No interest is charged
on these advances. Advances from the Parent are unsecured and have no specific
repayment terms. Management does not expect such balances to be settled within the
next year.
Due from related parties, net
The Company receives royalties and brand marketing funds fees from stores owned by
entities affiliated through common ownership. Receivables related to royalties and
brand marketing fund fees owed from these affiliates amounted to $52,741 and $45,501
as of December 31, 2024 and the period from January 10, 2023 (Inception) through
December 31, 2023, respectively.
