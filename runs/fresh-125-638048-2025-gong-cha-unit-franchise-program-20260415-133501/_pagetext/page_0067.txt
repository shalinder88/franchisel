Summary 
i.  Your obligations 
on termination/non-
renewal 
FA: Section 17 
FA: Stop operating the Store, stop using our 
names and the Marks, return information to us, 
assign to us or cancel certain registrations, 
listings, telephone numbers, websites, and 
domain names, pay all amounts you owe us, 
assign to us your lease or sublease for the 
Premises (at our option), and provide us an 
option to purchase your inventory, equipment, 
supplies, signs, and branded items. 
ADA: Sections 9 and 
10 
ADA: No further development rights, cease 
using Confidential Information, comply with 
non-compete covenants. 
j.  Assignment of 
contract by us 
FA: Section 15.1 
FA: No restriction on our right to assign. 
ADA: Section 6.3 
ADA: No restriction on our right to assign. 
k. “Transfer” by you 
– defined 
FA: Section 15.2 
FA: Includes transfer of any rights or 
obligations of the Franchise Agreement or any 
material asset of the Franchisee or the Store, or 
transfer of any ownership interest or control of 
the Franchisee. 
ADA: Section 1.16 
ADA: Includes transfer of any rights or 
obligations of the ADA, or any franchise 
agreement, of any material asset of you or your 
Store, or transfer of any ownership interest or 
control of you. 
l.  Our approval of 
transfer by 
franchisee 
FA: Section 15.2 
FA: We must approve all transfers, but will not 
withhold our consent if all of the requirements 
for the transfer are met. 
ADA: Section 6.1 
ADA: We must approve all transfers, but will 
not withhold our consent if all of the 
requirements for the transfer are met.
