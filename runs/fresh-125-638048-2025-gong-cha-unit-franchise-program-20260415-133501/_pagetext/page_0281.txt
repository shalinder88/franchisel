To the extent the Wisconsin Franchise Investment Law, Wis. Stat. §§553.01 – 553.78 or Wisconsin Fair

Notwithstanding anything to the contrary contained in the Area Development Agreement, to the 
extent that the Area Development Agreement contains provisions that are inconsistent with the

unmodified and in full force and effect.

any conflict between this addendum and the Area Development Agreement, the terms and conditions of 
this addendum shall apply.
