## Key Observations

- **Text layer:** Available on all 310 pages
- **Item map status:** Incomplete from bootstrap; manually reconstructed from TOC
- **FDD page offset:** FDD page 1 = PDF page 9 (offset of +8)
- **No company-owned hotels:** 0 in all years 2022-2024; 100% franchised system
- **Total system size:** 518 hotels at end of 2024
- **Item 19 format:** Room Rate, Occupancy, Occupancy Index, RevPAR Index tables (no EBITDA, no cost build-up)
- **Item 6 is unusually dense:** 13 pages of fees, multiple fee categories
- **Franchise term:** Non-renewable; New Construction 22 years, Conversion 10-20 years
- **Dispute resolution:** Virginia venue; New York law; no arbitration (except Puerto Rico)
- **Special risks:** (1) Out-of-state dispute resolution, (2) "Homewood" mark lacks federal registration
