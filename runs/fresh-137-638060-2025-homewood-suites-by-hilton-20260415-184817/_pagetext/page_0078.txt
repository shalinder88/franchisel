l. Our approval of 
transfer by you 
FA §12.2 
A Transfer of any interest in you, the Hotel, the Hotel Site, or 
the Franchise Agreement (or any rights or obligations under it) 
are prohibited unless expressly allowed in the Franchise 
Agreement. The Franchise Agreement allows 2 types of 
Transfers if certain conditions are satisfied: (a) Permitted 
Transfers; and (b) Change of Ownership Transfers.  In any 
Transfer, the proposed Transferee may not be a Sanctioned 
Person or a Competitor.
