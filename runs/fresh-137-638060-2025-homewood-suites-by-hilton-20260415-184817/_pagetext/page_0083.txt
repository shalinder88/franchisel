As of December 31, 2024, there were a total of 518 Homewood Suites branded hotels operating 
in the US. Of these, 437 were classified as Comparable Hotels. Of the Comparable Hotels, 7 were 
Company-Managed and 430 were Franchisee-Managed. The financial performance results 
detailed in this section for 2023 and 2024 are for the 2024 Comparable Hotels, defined above to 
provide a year-over-year comparison.
