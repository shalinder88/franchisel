## Omissions and Gaps

1. **Item 19 — No EBITDA or cost build-up:** This is by design — the FDD only provides room rate, occupancy, and indices. NOT an extraction gap. The FDD does not contain this data.
2. **Item 19 — No quartile tables:** The FDD provides averages, medians, and percentage above average/median, but not formal quartile breakdowns. NOT an extraction gap.
3. **Financial statement notes:** Notes to financial statements (pp. 133-137) not fully extracted. Key observations from balance sheet and income statement are captured. SEVERITY: LOW.
4. **Connected Room maintenance fee ambiguity:** "$564 to $860 per guest room per month" — unclear if this is per-hotel or per-room. If per-room, the cost for a 131-suite hotel would be $73,884–$112,660/month, which seems implausibly high for a TV streaming system. SEVERITY: MEDIUM — needs retry.
