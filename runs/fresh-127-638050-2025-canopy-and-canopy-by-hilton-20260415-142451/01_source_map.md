## Risk Flags (Early)
1. **No FPR** — Item 19 contains no financial performance representation
2. **No renewal right** — Franchise agreement is non-renewable; re-licensing at franchisor's sole discretion with materially different terms
3. **Principal mark "Canopy" registration concerns** — Special risks page notes the mark lacks the same legal protections as a federally registered mark (though registration 7552571 was obtained Oct 2024)
4. **Gag clause** — Some current and former franchisees signed provisions restricting ability to speak about experience
5. **Massive initial investment** — $64.1M–$141.7M for a 200-room hotel excluding real property
6. **Heavy technology burden** — OnQ, HPMS, GRO, Delphi, Connected Room, Guest Internet, Digital Floor Plan, Digital Key — all mandatory
7. **Multiple required consultants** — Designer, Interior Stylist, F&B, Lighting, Audio consultants must all be franchisor-approved
8. **Antitrust class actions** — Multiple pending hotel pricing algorithm antitrust suits naming Hilton Worldwide
9. **Non-exclusive territory** — No protected territory by default; optional Restricted Area shorter than term
10. **Debt guarantee** — HFH LLC unconditionally guarantees certain Hilton parent debt (maturing 2025–2033); all assets and franchise contracts pledged as collateral
