## Overall Risk Assessment: HIGH

This is an early-stage hotel conversion franchise system with only 11 units, a $31K balance sheet, no financial performance representations, no exclusive territory, and a franchisor whose financial condition has been flagged as a special risk by the FDD itself and by multiple state regulators requiring fee deferral. The parent company retains the ability to terminate the trademark license on 6 months' notice without cause. The 8% continuing fee plus distribution channel fees represent a significant revenue burden. Zero projected openings for 2025 and a decelerating growth rate (from +5 in 2023 to +2 in 2024) raise sustainability questions.
