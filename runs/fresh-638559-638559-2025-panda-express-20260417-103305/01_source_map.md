## Key Observations

- **Captive venue model**: This franchise operates primarily in airports, military bases, universities, hospitals, casinos, travel plazas — not traditional stand-alone locations (though a pilot program exists for drive-thru)
- **Parent structure**: Citadel Panda Express → Panda Express, Inc. (PEI) → Panda Restaurant Group, Inc. (PRG). PRG owns the marks.
- **S Corporation**: The franchisor is taxed as an S Corp — no federal income tax at entity level.
- **Subsidiary**: Citadel Aviation Investments, LLC (wholly-owned) — airplanes on balance sheet ($61.2M)
- **59 of 156 reported restaurants are JV with PRG affiliate as minority owner**
- **Gag clause present**: Item 20 states current/former franchisees may have signed provisions restricting their ability to speak openly
- **No advertising fund** — franchisees benefit from system-wide advertising conducted by affiliates
- **No exclusive territory** — no protected territory rights whatsoever
