NOTE 3   PROPERTY AND EQUIPMENT (CONTINUED)

         During the thirteen periods ended December 28, 2024 and December 30, 2023, the
         airplanes were primarily used by PEI and PRG, who paid for the operating costs. The
         Company has assumed all depreciation expenses related to buildings and improvements,
         the use of which is shared by the Companies, PEI and PRG, during the thirteen periods
         ended December 28, 2024.

         In February 2023, PRG made the final payment of $6,610,400 for the purchase of new
         airplane N100GA on behalf of the Company. The new airplane was delivered to the
         Company and put into use on February 10, 2023.

         In August 2023, PRG sold an airplane N882PX on behalf of the Company for purchase price
         of $13,489,754. PRG received proceeds of $13,489,754 from the sale on behalf of the
         Company.
