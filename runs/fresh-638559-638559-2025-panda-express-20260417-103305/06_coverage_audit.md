## Material Gaps

1. **Exhibit A — License Agreement** (HIGH): 96-page agreement not clause-walked. Item 17 provides summary tables, but operative clause detail (cure periods, notice requirements, specific triggers, exact noncompete scope) requires direct reading.

2. **Exhibit J — State Addenda** (MEDIUM): 14 state addenda identified. California, Maryland, Virginia sampled. Full state-by-state overrides not extracted for all states.

3. **Post-term noncompete scope** (MEDIUM): Item 17 says noncompete "continues in full force" but does not specify duration or geographic scope. Likely defined in Exhibit A.

4. **Death/disability provisions** (MEDIUM): Marked "Not applicable" in Item 17 — unusual. May be addressed elsewhere in the License Agreement.
