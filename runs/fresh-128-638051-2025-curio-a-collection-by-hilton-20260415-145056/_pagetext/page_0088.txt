Spa Amendment §§9 
and 10 
If your right to operate an eforea spa terminates or expires, 
your post-term obligations include termination of use of the 
eforea Brand and any other Brands, marks, systems or 
other rights licensed to you for the spa, delivery of all items 
containing any portion of our trademarks or service marks to 
us for use by us as we may see fit, make the changes we 
request to your Hotel, hotel site and spa to de-identify your 
spa as an eforea spa, return all copies of the eforea spa 
Manual, cancel all assumed name or equivalent 
registrations and transfer any domain name listings and 
registrations that contain any reference to the eforea Brand 
to us, and cease representing yourself or the hotel as then 
or formerly operating an eforea spa.
