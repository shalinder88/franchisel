In this Item 19, the term "Company-Managed" refers to hotels owned and/or managed by Hilton 
Worldwide or its affiliates, including franchised hotels. “Franchisee-Managed” refers to hotels that 
are franchised and are managed by the franchisee or a non-Hilton Worldwide management 
company retained by the franchisee. “Comparable Hotels” means all Curio Brand hotels that were 
open and operating in the US during calendar year 2024 (including those that were only open and 
operating for part of the year). 
 
As of December 31, 2024, there were a total of 88 Curio branded hotels operating in the US. Of 
these 88 hotels, all 88 were classified as Comparable Hotels. Of the 88 Comparable Hotels, 7 
were Company-Managed and 81 were Franchisee-Managed. The financial performance results 
detailed in this section for 2023 and 2024 are for the 2024 Comparable Hotels, defined above to 
provide a year-over-year comparison.
