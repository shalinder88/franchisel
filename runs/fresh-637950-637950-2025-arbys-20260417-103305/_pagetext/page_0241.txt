REVENUE RECOGNITION
As of December 29, 2024, December 31, 2023 and January 1, 2023, contract liabilities (deferred revenue included in "Other
current liabilities" and "Other liabilities") were $16.1 million, $17.1 million, and $17.7 million, respectively. Deferred
revenue primarily represents the Company’s remaining performance obligations under its franchise and license agreements
for which consideration has been received or is receivable, and is recognized on a straight-line basis over the remaining term
of the related agreement. The Company recognized $2.0 million, $2.0 million and $1.9 million of revenues associated with
prior year deferred franchise fees for the years ended December 29, 2024, December 31, 2023 and January 1, 2023,
respectively, offset by cash payments received or due in advance of satisfying the Company's performance obligations.
