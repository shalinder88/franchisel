COMMITMENTS AND CONTINGENCIES
Securitization Notes
The Issuer issued fixed rate senior secured notes and variable funding notes, collectively referred to as the “Arby's
Securitization Notes”. The Arby's Securitization Notes were issued in a securitization transaction pursuant to which certain
franchise-related agreements, intellectual property, in each case relating to the U.S., Canada, Turkey and Qatar, and real
property were contributed by various subsidiaries of ARG to the Issuer, the Company and one other limited-purpose,
bankruptcy remote, wholly-owned subsidiary of the Issuer, each of which acts as a guarantor through the maturity date of
the Arby's Securitization Notes and has pledged substantially all of its assets to secure the obligations of the Issuer.
