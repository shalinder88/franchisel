Section 14:2 of FA

If you meet our approval criteria (including a
full remodel and a General Release, see
Exhibit F), you may receive a Successor FA
(which will be the standard form FA,
including the Guaranty and Assumption of
Obligations, then current as of 12 months
prior to the expiration date of the FA and
may have materially different terms and
conditions than your previous FA). The
Successor term is stated in the FA but is
generally up to 20 years for a Traditional
Franchise and up to 10 years or
coterminous with the lease not to exceed
10 years for a Non-Traditional Franchise.
We will not grant you a Successor FA for a
term that exceeds the period of time that
you are entitled to maintain possession of
the Franchised Premises.
