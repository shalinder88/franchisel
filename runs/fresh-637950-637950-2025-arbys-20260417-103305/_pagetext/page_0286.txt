DEFAULT AND TERMINATION

BY ARBY’S

terminate automatically, without any notice or action required by Arby’s: upon the

assigns or transfers or attempts to assign or transfer any interest in the Franchised
Business or this Franchise Agreement without prior written approval from Arby’s or if the
requirements of Article 16 otherwise are violated.
