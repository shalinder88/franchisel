The Arby's Securitization Notes are subject to a series of covenants and restrictions customary for transactions of this type.

repayment events and events of default. Although the Company does not anticipate an event of default or any other event of
noncompliance with the provisions of the debt, if such event occurred, the unpaid amounts outstanding could become
immediately due and payable. As of December 29, 2024, the Company was in compliance with all debt covenant
requirements.
