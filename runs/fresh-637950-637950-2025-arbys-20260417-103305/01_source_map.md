## Key Findings from Source Map

- **362 total pages** — large FDD with extensive affiliate disclosure (Inspire Brands portfolio)
- Two sets of audited financial statements: (1) Arby's Franchisor LLC (the SPV franchisor), (2) ARG (the operating parent)
- State addenda present for: California, Hawaii, Maryland, Minnesota, North Dakota, Washington
- Michigan notice is a pre-body state disclosure, not in the state addenda exhibit
- Item 19 provides AUV data only (no cost/expense/profitability data)
- Item 18: No public figures used
- Gag clause language present on p.83 (PDF): "current and former franchisees sign provisions restricting their ability to speak openly"
