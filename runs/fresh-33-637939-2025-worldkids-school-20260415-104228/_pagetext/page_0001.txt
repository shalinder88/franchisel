Small (1,000 – 3,000 sq. feet): The total investment necessary to begin operation of a franchised business 
ranges from $195,000 to $291,500. This includes $80,000 which must be paid to the franchisor or affiliate.  
The total investment necessary to begin operation of an Area Development Business for 3-5 outlets is 
$275,000 to $451,500. This includes $160,000 - $240,000 that must be paid to the franchisor or affiliate.  
 
Medium (3,001 – 6,000 sq. feet): The total investment necessary to begin operation of a franchised 
business ranges from $270,000 to $436,500. This includes $80,000 which must be paid to the franchisor or 
affiliate.  The total investment necessary to begin operation of an Area Development Business for 3-5 
outlets is $350,000 to $596,500. This includes $160,000 - $240,000 that must be paid to the franchisor or 
affiliate.  
 
Large (6,001+ sq. feet): The total investment necessary to begin operation of a franchised business ranges 
from $435,000 to $591,500. This includes $80,000 which must be paid to the franchisor or affiliate.  The 
total investment necessary to begin operation of an Area Development Business for 3-5 outlets is $515,000 
to $751,500. This includes $160,000 - $240,000 that must be paid to the franchisor or affiliate.
