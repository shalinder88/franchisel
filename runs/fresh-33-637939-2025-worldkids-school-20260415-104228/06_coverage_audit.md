2. **U-002 (MEDIUM):** No franchisee operating history — zero franchised outlets in system history. Cannot verify franchisor support capability.
3. **U-003 (LOW):** Auditor report date typo — "March 14, 2024" should be "March 14, 2025" (p.145).
4. **U-004 (LOW):** Subsequent events date typo — same error (p.153).
5. **U-005 (LOW):** Item 20 Table 4 year label error — last row shows "2023" should be "2024" (p.52).
6. **U-006 (MEDIUM):** FPR scope limitation — only gross revenue minus royalties/advertising disclosed. No operating costs, no profitability metrics. All data from affiliate-owned outlets only.
7. **U-007 (LOW):** Ad fund not audited — unaudited statement available on request. Moot since no fees collected in FY2024.
8. **U-008 (LOW):** Franchise brokers (Franchise Creator, LLC) listed on Receipt but not in Item 2 business experience.
