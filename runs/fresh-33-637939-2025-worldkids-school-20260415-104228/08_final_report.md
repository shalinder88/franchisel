# Final Diligence Report — WorldKids School (637939-2025)

**Filing ID:** 637939 | **Brand:** WorldKids School | **Franchisor:** WKAF LLC | **Issuance Date:** March 17, 2025

---

## A. Executive Snapshot

1. **System size:** 8 company-owned (affiliate) outlets in Washington State as of December 31, 2024. Zero franchised outlets have ever existed. The franchisor (WKAF LLC) was formed July 18, 2024 and began offering franchises in October 2024.
2. **Entity structure:** WKAF LLC is a Washington State LLC formed in 2024. Its sole affiliate is WorldKids Academy, LLC (formed 2015), which owns and operates the 8 learning centers and owns the trademarks. WKAF LLC is the franchise-selling entity; WorldKids Academy is the operating entity.
3. **Ownership:** Single officer — Nicole Chaudry, CEO of both WKAF LLC and WorldKids Academy, LLC. 10 years of industry experience. Franchise Creator, LLC (Miami, FL) acts as franchise broker/seller.
4. **Investment range:** Small school (1K-3K sqft): $195,000-$291,500; Medium (3K-6K sqft): $270,000-$436,500; Large (6K+ sqft): $435,000-$591,500. No franchisor financing.
5. **Fee burden:** 6% royalty + 2% national ad fund (after 12 months) + local advertising (greater of $2,000/mo or 1.5%) = approximately 9.5% of gross revenue in steady state, plus $2,000/mo floor on local advertising.
6. **Item 19 highlights:** 8 affiliate outlets reported for 2024, gross revenue ranging from $72,501 (Everett, opened Sep 2024) to $3,983,011 (Georgetown/SODO, 12,765 sqft). Revenue only — no expenses, costs, or profitability disclosed. All locations in Seattle/WA metro area.
7. **Bottom-quartile risk:** The smallest full-year outlets (Edmonds at $396,737 and Delridge at $602,449) are small-format (1,095 and 1,345 sqft) locations. After adjusting for royalties and local advertising, these generate adjusted gross revenue of $348,933 and $542,302. Given that no operating costs are disclosed, it is unclear whether these smaller outlets are profitable.
8. **Financial health:** CRITICAL CONCERN — WKAF LLC has total assets of $97 as of December 31, 2024. Zero revenue, zero expenses. The entity is essentially a shell. The FDD itself flags this in Special Risk #4: "The Franchisor's financial condition as reflected in its financial statements calls into question the Franchisor's financial ability to provide services and support to you."
9. **Growth trajectory:** System grew from 5 to 8 company-owned outlets over 2022-2024 (+3 outlets in 3 years). One projected new franchised outlet in Washington for 2025.
10. **Territory:** Exclusive, typically 5-mile radius, no performance contingency. Franchisor reserves internet and direct marketing channels.
11. **Renewal:** 10-year terms, renewable for successive 10-year terms. Renewal requires signing then-current FA with potentially materially different terms, general release, $5,000 fee.
12. **Disputes:** Mediation/arbitration in Washington State. Washington law governs. Jury waiver. Class action waiver. Extensive state addenda override provisions.
13. **Special risks:** (a) Out-of-state dispute resolution, (b) Short operating history, (c) Mandatory minimum payments, (d) Questionable financial condition of franchisor.

## B. Fee Stack, Entry Structure, Initial Investment

### Front Matter and Entity Structure
WKAF LLC is a Washington State LLC formed July 18, 2024, created specifically to offer franchises for the WorldKids School concept. The actual operating business is run through its affiliate, WorldKids Academy, LLC (formed January 2015), which operates 8 learning centers in the Seattle/WA area and owns the trademarks. WKAF LLC has no parent and no predecessor. The separation of the franchise-selling entity from the operating entity is notable — the entity selling franchises has essentially no assets or operating history.

## D. Territory, Competition, Channels, Encroachment

### Protected Territory
- Exclusive territory defined by radius (typically 5 miles), zip codes, or political boundaries.
- Franchisor will not establish company-owned or franchised outlets in territory.
- Territory size varies by population density — no minimum specified.
- Territorial rights NOT contingent on sales volume or market penetration.
- Territory cannot be altered even with population increase.

### Channel Restrictions and Franchisor Rights
- Franchisor reserves Internet, catalog, telemarketing, and direct marketing channels in territory.
- Franchisor would "normally" direct service inquiries from territory to franchisee's outlet.
- Franchisor not obligated to compensate franchisee for orders from inside territory.
- Franchisee may not solicit outside territory but may accept orders.
- Franchisor reserves right to operate/franchise under different trademarks with similar services.

### ADA Development Areas
- Same criteria as single-outlet territories.
- Exclusive within Development Area.
- Failure to meet Development Schedule = loss of future territory rights (existing FAs unaffected).

## E. Contract Burden and Legal Mechanics

### Term and Renewal
- Initial term: 10 years from Effective Date.
- Successive 10-year renewal terms available.
- **Materially different terms risk:** Renewal requires signing then-current FA, which "may contain materially different terms and conditions than your original franchise agreement."
- Renewal conditions: Full compliance throughout term, capital expenditures for system uniformity, all monetary obligations satisfied, not in default, timely notice, meet current qualifications, complete training, sign general release, pay $5,000 renewal fee.

### Transfer Provisions
- Franchisor may assign without restriction.
- Franchisee transfer requires prior written consent with 13 conditions.
- $10,000 transfer fee (no fee for entity-to-entity where franchisee retains control).
- Right of first refusal.
- General release required from both franchisee and transferee.

### Termination and Death/Disability

- Curable defaults: 30-day general cure; 10-day insurance cure; 5-day payment cure.
- Death/disability: Representative must transfer within 180 days or franchisor may terminate.

### Noncompete Restrictions
- During term: No ownership or employment in competitive business.
- Post-term: 2 years, 25-mile radius from any franchised or company-owned outlet. No solicitation.

### Critical Caveats
1. **No expense data:** The FPR reports only gross revenue minus royalties and local advertising. No COGS, payroll, rent, utilities, insurance, or other operating costs are disclosed. A franchisee cannot determine profitability from this data alone.
2. **All affiliate outlets:** All data comes from WorldKids Academy, LLC outlets, not franchised outlets. Operational differences exist between company-owned and franchisee-operated outlets.
3. **Single market:** All outlets are in the Seattle/WA metro area. Revenue may not be representative of other markets.
4. **Size correlation:** Revenue correlates strongly with square footage and student count. The two largest outlets (12,765 and 13,156 sqft) generate ~$3.5-4M; the two smallest (1,095 and 1,345 sqft) generate ~$400-600K.
5. **Partial-year outlets:** Fremont and Everett operated partial years in 2024. Everett's $72,501 on 4 months is not representative of annual performance.
6. **National Ad Fund not included in adjustment:** The 2% NAF deduction is not reflected in the adjustment (it starts after 12 months). A new franchisee's adjusted revenue would be lower by an additional 2% once NAF kicks in.

### Key Financial Observations
1. **Near-zero capitalization:** WKAF LLC has $97 in total assets. This raises fundamental questions about the franchisor's ability to provide pre-opening support, training, advertising fund administration, ongoing guidance, and any other obligations under the franchise agreement.
2. **No revenue:** Zero franchise sales and zero royalty income as of December 31, 2024, despite beginning to offer franchises in October 2024.
3. **Cash flow structure:** The entity received $104,157 in member contributions and immediately distributed $104,060 back. This suggests the entity is being operated as a pass-through with minimal retained capital.
4. **LLC pass-through taxation:** No income tax liability — all income/loss passes to members.
5. **No deferred franchise fees:** No franchise agreements were executed as of the balance sheet date.
6. **Inception comparison:** At September 19, 2024, the entity had $100,223 in cash (member contributions). By December 31, 2024, nearly all capital had been distributed, leaving $97.
7. **Surety bond requirement:** Washington State requires a $100,000 surety bond, which provides some but limited protection.

### Final Assessment
This FDD describes an extremely early-stage franchise system with no operating track record as a franchisor. The selling entity (WKAF LLC) has essentially no assets and no revenue. The underlying business concept (bilingual immersion learning centers) shows positive revenue performance at the affiliate level, with top-line revenue up to $4M for large locations. However, the complete absence of expense disclosure, the shell nature of the franchisor entity, and the lack of any franchisee experience make this a high-risk investment requiring substantial independent due diligence on operating costs, market conditions outside the Seattle metro, and the franchisor's ability to deliver on its obligations.
