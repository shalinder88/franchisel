Confidentiality Agreements
During the last 3 fiscal years, we have signed confidentiality agreements with some current and
former franchisees. Each confidentiality agreement was entered into as part of a settlement of a
dispute between us and the current or former franchisee. In some instances, current and former
franchisees sign provisions restricting their ability to speak openly about their experience with us.
You may wish to speak with current and former franchisees but be aware that not all such franchisees
will be able to communicate with you.
