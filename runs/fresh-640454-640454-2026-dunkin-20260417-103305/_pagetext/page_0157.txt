The legal final maturity dates of the Dunkin' Securitization notes issued in 2017, 2019, 2021, and 2025 are November 2047, May 2049, November
2051, and August 2055, respectively. If the Dunkin' Master Issuer has not repaid or refinanced the notes prior to the anticipated repayment date,
additional interest will accrue pursuant to the terms of the underlying securitization agreement.

(b)

The 2019 Class A-1 Senior Variable Funding Notes ("2019 Variable Funding Notes") have an anticipated repayment date of August 2029 with two
one-year extension options available.

(c)

The 2019 Variable Funding Notes bear interest at per annum rates equal to funding cost or index plus 1.50%. The 2019 Variable Funding Notes are
subject to certain commitment fees of 0.50% based on utilization.
The 2023 Class A-1 Senior Variable Funding Notes ("2023 Variable Funding Notes") bear interest at per annum rates equal to a funding cost or index
plus 1.75%. The 2023 Variable Funding Notes are subject to certain commitment fees of 0.50% based on utilization.

The Securitization Notes are secured by substantially all of the assets of and guaranteed by the Master Issuer’s direct parent
and subsidiaries (collectively with the Master Issuer, “Securitization Entities”), and were issued in a securitization
transaction pursuant to which certain assets such as franchise agreements, intellectual property and real property were
contributed to such Securitization Entities.
Interest on the Dunkin' Securitization Notes is payable on a quarterly basis.
In November 2025, the Company issued $550.0 million 2025-1 Class A-2-I Senior Secured Fixed Rate Notes and $350.0
million 2025-1 Class A-2-II Senior Secured Fixed Rate Notes (collectively, the "2025 Fixed Rate Notes"). Proceeds from
the 2025 Fixed Rate Notes were used to repay the remaining $376.0 million outstanding on the 2019-1 Class A-2-II Senior
Secured Fixed Rate Notes due May 2026, repay $481.6 million in drawn 2023 Variable Funding Notes balances, and pay
related transaction fees and expenses. The remaining net proceeds were distributed to the Company. No amendments or
terminations were made to the 2023 Variable Funding Notes in connection with the transaction.
The 2019 Variable Funding Notes and 2023 Variable Funding Notes allow for the issuance of up to $150.0 million and
$850.0 million, respectively, of variable funding notes and certain other credit instruments, each including letters of credit of
$100.0 million in support of various Dunkin' subsidiary obligations. As of December 28, 2025, the Company had
outstanding borrowings of $88.8 million under the 2019 Variable Funding Notes leaving $61.2 million of remaining
capacity for future borrowings. As of December 28, 2025, the Company had no outstanding borrowings exclusive of $56.4
million of outstanding letters of credit under the 2023 Variable Funding Notes leaving $793.6 million of remaining capacity
for future borrowings.
