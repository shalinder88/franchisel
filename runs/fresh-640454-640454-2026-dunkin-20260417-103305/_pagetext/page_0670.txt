5) Exclusivity
During the term of this Agreement, other than your participation in the Program, you will not, either internally
or through a third party, offer or participate in any other mobile or online ordering, purchasing or payment
program or any other proprietary, closed network, online stored value card program at or through any Dunkin’
or Baskin-Robbins restaurant. You may participate in “Hospitality Programs.” Hospitality Programs are card
programs that: (i) your restaurant is required to participate in by the host of such location (where the host is an
entity within whose broader rules of operation the restaurant must operate, such as but not limited to, a school,
university, hospital, or military PX); and (ii) do not involve the use of Dunkin’ Brands, Inc.’s brands on the
cards.
6) Transfer of Restaurant; Termination
a) Termination. Participation in the Loyalty Program and this Agreement shall terminate automatically with
respect to the applicable Restaurant on the termination or expiration of your Franchise Agreement; provided,
however that if the Franchise Agreement is terminated for convenience in connection with a renewal of term
and the simultaneous entry by the Franchisee into a new Franchise Agreement for the same Restaurant, this
Agreement and your participation in the Loyalty Program shall continue.
b) Transfer. Effective as of the transfer of the Restaurant, this Agreement is terminated and you will not be
eligible to receive reimbursements for any redemptions claimed after the date of transfer or refunds of any LCP
paid prior to the date of transfer, or any other rights under this Agreement; provided, however, that you will
remain liable for any required payments due and other obligations pursuant to this Agreement arising through
the date of transfer.
c) Effect of Termination. Upon termination of the Franchise Agreement and this Agreement, unpaid LCP for
Loyalty Sales made prior to such termination shall become immediately due and payable. You will not be
eligible to receive reimbursements for any redemptions made after the date of termination; provided, however,
that you will remain liable for any required payments due and other obligations pursuant to this Agreement
arising through the date of transfer/termination.
