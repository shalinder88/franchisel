Notes to the Tables Above Regarding Combo Restaurants:
If one brand of a Combo Restaurant is impacted by a closure or other change that is disclosed in the
tables above (e.g., ceased operations), then the entire Combo Restaurant is disclosed as such (e.g.,
ceased operations) even when the other brand was not impacted (e.g., it remains in operation).
Notes to All Item 20 Tables:
1.

The tables above provide information during the periods labeled as 2023, 2024 and 2025. The
fiscal year 2023 numbers represent the 12-month period between January 1, 2023, and
December 31, 2023. The fiscal year 2024 numbers represent the period between January 1,
2024, and December 29, 2024. The fiscal year 2025 numbers represent the period between
December 30, 2024, and December 28, 2025. If multiple events occurred affecting a
restaurant, the table shows the event that occurred last in time.

2.

The total number of standalone Dunkin’ Restaurants may be impacted by the addition of a
Baskin-Robbins to an existing Dunkin’ Restaurant. These former standalone Dunkin’
Restaurants are then counted as Combo Restaurants.

3.

The total number of standalone Dunkin’ Restaurants may be impacted by the closing of the
Baskin-Robbins/Other Brand portion of a Multi-Brand Restaurant or Combo Restaurant.
These former Multi-Brand Restaurants or Combo Restaurants are then counted as standalone
Dunkin’ Restaurants.

4.

The tables do not include restaurants located on U.S. military bases outside of the United
States.
