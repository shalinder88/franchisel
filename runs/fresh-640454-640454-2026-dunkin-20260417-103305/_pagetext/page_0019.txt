brands in the United States: Merry Maids SPE LLC (“Merry Maids”), ServiceMaster Clean/Restore
SPE LLC (“ServiceMaster”) and Two Men and a Truck SPE LLC (“Two Men and a Truck”). Merry
Maids and ServiceMaster became Affiliated Programs through an acquisition in December 2020.

three franchisors have a principal place of business at One Glenlake Parkway, Suite 1400, Atlanta,
Georgia 30328 and have never offered franchises in any other line of business.

maintenance services under the ServiceMaster Clean® mark. ServiceMaster’s predecessor
