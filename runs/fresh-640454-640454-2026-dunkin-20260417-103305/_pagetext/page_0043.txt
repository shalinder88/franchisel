Under the Pacific Northwest Incentive program, with respect to each Restaurant, if you comply with
the Incentive Eligibility Requirements, then for the first and second traditional Dunkin’ Restaurants
that you or your affiliate develop in the Pacific Northwest Territory under the Pacific Northwest
Incentive program in compliance with the Incentive Eligibility Requirements, we will reimburse you
up to $20,000 for the first Restaurant and up to $10,000 for the second Restaurant for amounts that
you spend on promotional activities related to the opening of the first and second traditional Dunkin’
Restaurants, so long as you undertake the promotional activities in the manner and to the extent that
we prescribe in accordance with our Standards. You may also be eligible for the CFF credits, which
we describe in Item 6.

Incentive Eligibility Requirements. “Incentive Eligibility Requirements” are as
follows: (i) you and your affiliates are in substantial compliance with the Franchise Agreement and
each other agreement between us and you or your affiliates, including (if applicable) a Development
Agreement, (ii) you open and begin operating the Restaurant in compliance with the Franchise
Agreement on or before the required opening date, (iii) you provide to us a report, in the format and
containing the information that we reasonably specify, identifying the amounts that you spend in
various categories relating to the development and opening of the Restaurant within 120 days after
the Restaurant first opens for business, and (iv) you build the Restaurant in the design, to the
specifications, and at the location approved by us.
