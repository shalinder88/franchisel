- Item 1 is unusually long (13 pages) due to extensive Inspire Brands affiliate disclosures (Arby's, Buffalo Wild Wings, Jimmy John's, Sonic, Baskin-Robbins, GoTo Foods brands, Driven Holdings brands, ServiceMaster brands, Subway, Dave's Hot Chicken, etc.)
- Item 2 lists 25+ officers/directors with detailed business experience
- Item 3 discloses 3 pending actions (Minda's Donuts wrongful termination, Shree Krishna breach/counterclaim, Phoenix Food Pakistan trilogy) plus 3 actions commenced by franchisor, plus 5 concluded actions
- Item 4: No bankruptcies
- Item 5: IFF tiered by DMA ($40K–$90K for standard), with extensive DMA-to-county mapping
- Item 6: CFF 5.9%, CAF 5.0% (2.5% for SDOs), Loyalty Program Contribution 1.4% (declining to 1.2% in 2027), multiple incentive programs (Standard, Strategic, Pacific NW, Early Opening, VetFran)
- Item 7: Four investment tables (Freestanding $532K–$1.83M, Shopping Center $443K–$1.33M, Gas/C-Store $216K–$1.07M, SDO $142K–$863K)
- Item 10: No financing offered
- Item 12: No exclusive territory under FA; limited territorial protection under DA
- Item 17: 20-year term, 20-year renewal; noncompete 24 months / 5 miles; Georgia law; Atlanta venue
- Item 18: No public figures
- Item 19: FPR provided — AUVs for 7,010 franchised restaurants (avg $1,372,069); 4 tables (overall quartiles, by site type, by drive-thru status, count above thresholds)
- Item 20: Full 5-table set for both Dunkin' standalone and Combo restaurants; system grew from 8,118 to 8,780 total outlets (2023–2025); gag clause present
- Item 21: Audited by KPMG, unqualified opinion, no going concern; total revenue $1.398B; net income $732.9M; total assets $1.572B; distributions to parent $813.3M
- Exhibit B: Two sets of financial statements — DD Franchisor LLC and Dunkin' Brands, Inc. consolidated
- Exhibit E: State-Specific Appendices (30 pages, ~pp.323–352)
- Exhibits G-1 through H-4: Extensive franchisee/developer lists and former franchisee lists spanning ~240+ pages
