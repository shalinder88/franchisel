# Reader Report — Dunkin' 2026 FDD (Filing 640454)

## Pass A: Executive Snapshot

1. **Franchisor entity:** Dunkin' Donuts Franchising LLC, a Delaware LLC formed March 2006; indirect subsidiary of Inspire Brands, Inc. (Roark Capital portfolio). Principal office: Three Glenlake Parkway, Atlanta, GA 30328.
2. **Brand origin:** Dunkin' brand began 1954; franchising since 1955. Franchisor has never operated a Dunkin' Restaurant.
3. **System size (as of Dec 28, 2025):** 9,999 Dunkin' restaurants in the U.S. (9,963 franchised, 36 company-owned); 8,744 single-branded Dunkin', 36 Multi-Brand Locations, 1,219 Combo (Dunkin'+Baskin-Robbins). Additionally, 4,242 international Dunkin' restaurants.
4. **Investment range:** Freestanding $532,400–$1,832,500; Shopping Center $443,000–$1,333,500; Gas/C-Store $216,400–$1,065,500; SDO $142,000–$862,500.
5. **Fee burden:** CFF 5.9% of Gross Sales; CAF 5.0% (2.5% for SDOs); Loyalty Program Contribution 1.4% (declining to 1.2% in 2027); total ongoing royalty+ad burden approximately 12.3% of Gross Sales for standard locations.
6. **Item 19 highlights:** Average AUV for 7,010 franchised restaurants was $1,372,069 (median $1,297,694). Top quartile average $2,154,341; bottom quartile average $718,088. 78% of traditional restaurants exceeded $1M AUV.
7. **Item 20 trajectory:** Strong growth — net +281 Dunkin' outlets in 2025 (8,499→8,780 total); 314 new franchised openings vs. 81 closures (24 non-renewals, 2 terminations, 55 ceased ops). 406 projected new franchised outlets in next fiscal year.
8. **Item 21 financial health:** KPMG-audited, unqualified opinion, no going concern. Total revenue $1.398B; net income $732.9M; total assets $1.572B; member's equity $1.331B. Distributions to parent $813.3M (exceeding net income).
9. **Territory:** No exclusive territory under Franchise Agreement; limited territorial protection only during Development Agreement term.
10. **Renewal:** 20-year initial term; 20-year renewal available subject to conditions including signing then-current FA, cure of defaults, payment of renewal fee, and obtaining premises lease.
11. **Disputes:** Georgia law governs; venue in Atlanta, Georgia (subject to state law). No arbitration or mediation requirement.
12. **Noncompete:** During term — no interest in business deriving >20% revenue from coffee and/or baked goods. Post-term — 24 months, 5-mile radius from Premises or any Dunkin' Restaurant.
13. **Special risks:** Out-of-state dispute resolution only.
14. **Gag clause:** Present — confidentiality agreements signed with some current/former franchisees as part of dispute settlements; some restricting ability to speak openly.

## Pass E: Contract Burden, Legal Mechanics (Item 17)

### Renewal
- **Term:** 20 years
- **Renewal term:** 20 years
- **Conditions:** Written notice; maintained standards during term; not more than 3 default notices in prior 10 years from notice to renew; no default notice after issuing renewal notice; no uncured defaults; all amounts paid; obtain lease or substitute premises; termination and mutual general release; sign then-current FA (which may be materially different); pay renewal fee
- **SDO locations:** No renewal rights
- **Development Agreement:** Mid-term extension available if development obligations met; renewal subject to similar conditions plus new development schedule

### Termination
- **By franchisee:** No right
- **By franchisor without cause:** No right
- **By franchisor with cause:** Only upon default or failure to comply

**Curable defaults (FA):**
- 24 hours: violation of law/Standard relating to health, sanitation, safety; ceasing operations without consent
- 7 days: failure to pay
- 30 days: failure to comply with any other provision

**Non-curable defaults (FA):** Loss of Premises/lease termination; unauthorized transfer; unauthorized use of Confidential Information; felony conviction/crime of moral turpitude; fraud; intentional underreporting of Gross Sales; unauthorized use of Premises; another contract terminated; 3+ notices of same/similar defaults in 12 months; bankruptcy.

**Cross-default provisions** between FA and DA.

### Transfer
- Broad definition: voluntary/involuntary, direct/indirect, including ownership interest changes, mergers, divorce, foreclosure
- **Controlling Transfer** requires: transferee meets criteria/executes guaranty; all obligations satisfied; Restaurant in compliance; sale price not excessive; general release; repair/remodel if required; transferee bound by FA terms or sign then-current form
- **Non-Controlling Transfer:** transferee of good character, meets standards, executes guarantee; Fixed Documentation Fee ($2,000)
- **ROFR:** 60 days to match terms for any interest sale; if terms change, new 60-day option
- **Death/disability:** Transfer within 12–18 months, subject to franchisor consent
- **Post-termination option to purchase:** Within 30 days, franchisor may purchase Operating Assets at fair market value less indebtedness

### Noncompete
- **During term:** No interest in Competitive Business (>20% revenue from coffee and/or baked goods); no customer diversion; no opposing government approval of new Dunkin'; no acts injurious to goodwill
- **Post-term:** 24 months after expiration/termination/transfer — no interest in Competitive Business at Premises, within 5 miles, or at any other Dunkin' Restaurant

### Dispute Resolution
- **No arbitration or mediation** required
- **Forum:** Federal, state, or local courts within Atlanta, Georgia (subject to state law)
- **Governing law:** Georgia law (subject to state law)
- **Integration/merger clause:** FA and DA constitute entire agreement
- **Modifications:** Only in writing, except franchisor's right to modify System, Manuals, Standards, Marks
