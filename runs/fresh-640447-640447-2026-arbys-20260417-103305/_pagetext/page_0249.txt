ARTICLE 9: RIGHT OF INSPECTION

9:1     GENERAL

        shall have the right to test all Licensed Products offered for sale and supplies used by Franchisee for
        the purpose of determining the quality and specifications of such products and supplies. Arby’s shall
        have access to the Franchised Premises at all reasonable times for these purposes.

        Franchisee’s actual cost.
