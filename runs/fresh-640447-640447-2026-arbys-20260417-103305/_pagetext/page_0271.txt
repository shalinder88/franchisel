and (c) Arby’s would not enter into the Agreement unless each of the undersigned agrees to sign and comply
with the terms of this Guaranty.

amend this Guaranty, which will be continuing and irrevocable during the term of the Agreement (including

owners or guarantors; and (e) this Guaranty will continue in full force and effect for (and as to) any extension
