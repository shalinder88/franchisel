## Pass B: Front Matter, Formats, Fees, Investment (Items 1–7)

### Item 1 — The Franchisor
Arby's Franchisor, LLC is a single-member Delaware LLC formed July 2, 2015 as part of a securitization transaction. The franchisor has never operated an Arby's restaurant. ARG is the predecessor and current manager under a management agreement. Inspire Brands is the ultimate parent, with affiliated brands including Buffalo Wild Wings, Dunkin', Baskin-Robbins, Jimmy John's, Sonic, and numerous others through Roark Capital portfolio (GoTo Foods brands, CKE/Carl's Jr./Hardee's, Driven Holdings/Meineke/CARSTAR/Take 5, ServiceMaster/Merry Maids/Two Men and a Truck, Nothing Bundt Cakes, Mathnasium, Youth Enrichment Brands/i9 Sports/School of Rock, Subway, Dave's Hot Chicken, Primrose Schools, Massage Envy).

Two restaurant types: Traditional (full-menu, drive-thru) and Non-Traditional (limited menu, airports/military/etc.).

Multi-Brand Locations possible with Buffalo Wild Wings, Dunkin', Baskin-Robbins, Jimmy John's, Sonic.

### Item 5 — Initial Fees
- **Development Fee**: $12,500/restaurant (Standard Traditional), $0 (Non-Traditional), $12,500 (Standard Incentive)
- **Franchise Fee**: $37,500 (Standard Traditional), $18,750 (Non-Traditional), $0 (Standard Incentive — waived)
- **Training Fee**: $2,100/attendee (expected to increase to $4,000 mid-year); waived for 2 managers in first restaurant
- **Design Fees**: $1,200 PSL, $750–$1,200 PKL

Standard Incentive waives franchise fee and provides $7,500 royalty credit. Relocation Incentive waives all initial fees and provides $50,000 royalty credit if opened by Dec 31, 2028. VetFran provides $10,000 royalty credit per restaurant (up to $100,000). Pioneer Incentive provides $50,000 royalty credit for first 2 restaurants in CT, DC, HI, MA, NH, RI, VT.

### Item 6 — Other Fees
- **Royalty Fee**: 4% of Gross Sales (Traditional); 6.2% (Non-Traditional). Standard Incentive: 0.5% (Year 1), 1.0% (Years 2-4), 2.0% (Year 5), 4.0% (Year 6+). Additional reduction possible if Gross Sales below $1.3M after Year 4. Relocation Incentive: 1% (Year 1), 2% (Year 2), 3% (Year 3), 4% (Year 4+).
- **Advertising & Marketing Service Fee**: 3.22%–5.0% of Gross Sales (tiered by DMA); minimum aggregate 5.2% including local and co-op. Standard Incentive: 75% discount Years 1-3, 50% Year 4, standard Year 5+. Minimum 0.85%.
- **Renewal Fee**: 10% of then-applicable franchise fee (Traditional); applicable Non-Traditional franchise fee
- **Transfer Fee**: $17,500 for first restaurant; $2,500 if already operate ≥1 Arby's
- **Training Program**: $2,100–$4,000/attendee
- **Learning Management System**: $62.99/year per restaurant
- **Arby's Order Ahead Platform**: $50/month + 4% per transaction (35¢ min, $1.25 max); 3% on third-party delivery
- **Interest**: up to 18% per year on overdue amounts
- **Audits**: audit expenses plus interest if >3% deficiency

## Pass E: Contract Burden, Legal Mechanics (Item 17)

### Term and Renewal
- **Traditional**: Up to 20-year term (may be shorter if lease is shorter or purchasing existing restaurant)
- **Non-Traditional**: Shorter of 10 years or lease term
- **Successor FA**: Standard form then-current 12 months before expiration — **may have materially different terms**
- **Renewal conditions**: Substantial compliance, no default in prior 12 months, remodel to current image, training compliance, General Release (Exhibit F), apply 6–9 months before expiration, pay 10% of current franchise fee

### Termination
- **Franchisee termination**: None — no right to terminate
- **Franchisor without cause**: None
- **Curable defaults**: 30 days for non-compliance (or longer per state law); 10 days for non-payment; 180 days for casualty restoration
- **Non-curable defaults (immediate)**: Insolvency, bankruptcy, assignment for benefit of creditors, unauthorized transfer, loss of premises, criminal conviction, false information, system duplication, noncompete/NDA violation, denial of inspection, 2+ monetary defaults in 12 months, imminent health/safety danger, abandonment
- **DA termination**: Immediate on death of individual developer, bankruptcy, failure to comply with Development Schedule, unauthorized transfer

### Transfer
- Requires prior written approval (not unreasonably withheld if conditions met)
- Transfer fee: $17,500 (first restaurant), $2,500 (existing franchisee)
- Conditions: good character/reputation, financial qualification, sign current agreements, complete training, correct deficiencies in 90 days, General Release
- **Right of first refusal**: Applies except for "pre-qualified buyer" (≥5 Arby's restaurants, no defaults, no litigation with Arby's, no competing roast beef business)
- Death/disability: Can transfer minority control (<50%) to family member/trust without consent; DA terminates immediately on death of individual developer

### Noncompete
- **During term**: Cannot own/operate/be employed by roast beef sandwich business within Protected Area. Applies to franchisee, guarantors, partners, 15%+ equity holders.
- **Post-term**: Same restriction continues 12 months after termination. Narrow scope (roast beef sandwiches only, Protected Area only).

### Dispute Resolution
- No arbitration or mediation
- **Forum**: Federal/state court closest to franchisor's principal office (currently Atlanta, GA). Franchisor can also file where franchisee resides/does business.
- **Governing law**: Georgia law (without conflict-of-laws rules); Georgia franchise law applies only if jurisdictional requirements independently met. Interest governed by state of Restaurant or Georgia, whichever higher.

### Post-Termination
Must immediately cease use of trademarks, advertising, Manual, exterior design. Return Manual. De-identify premises.
