# Reader Report — 637941-2025 Fish & Chips Gordon Ramsay

## Pass A: Executive Snapshot

1. **Brand**: Fish & Chips Gordon Ramsay — fast-casual British fish and chips restaurants featuring classic English street food, milkshakes, beer and wine service.
2. **Franchisor**: GRNA FC Group LLC, a Delaware LLC formed June 20, 2024. Principal office: 6191 North State Highway 161, Suite 400, Irving, TX 75038-2251. Began offering franchises as of FDD issuance date (February 26, 2025).
3. **Parent chain**: GRNA Franchise Group LLC → GRNA LLC → GRNA MidCo LLC → GRNA Holdings Inc. All share the same principal address.
4. **Celebrity endorsement**: Gordon Ramsay is 49% indirect owner of GRNA Holdings. Name and image used in marketing. No direct investment in the franchisor entity. Not involved in day-to-day management.
5. **System size**: Extremely small. End of FY2024: 2 "franchised" outlets (both are licensees under affiliate IPH2, in Nevada hotel/casino locations) and 3 company-owned outlets (NY, Orlando FL, Washington DC). Total: 5 outlets. No actual franchise agreements signed as of issuance. No projected openings.
6. **Investment range**: $1,503,000 – $2,410,000 (single unit under Franchise Agreement).
7. **Fee burden**: Initial franchise fee $50,000; royalty 5% of Restaurant Revenue (can be increased to 8% for menu pricing violations); Brand Fund 0.5%; local marketing 1%; combined cap 4%.
8. **Item 19**: No financial performance representation made.
9. **Financial health**: Balance sheet dated January 31, 2025 shows $100,000 cash = total assets = total members' equity. Entity funded with $100,000 capital contribution on January 30, 2025. Company has not commenced operations. Entity is less than 1 year old at issuance date.
10. **Territory**: Designated Area determined by franchisor. Not an exclusive territory per se — broad reserved rights for non-traditional venues, collateral products, delivery from outside territory, other branded restaurants within territory.
11. **Renewal**: Four additional 5-year renewal terms after initial 10-year term. Must sign then-current franchise agreement (may be "materially different"). General release required.
12. **Noncompete**: During term — no involvement with any casual dining/food service that is substantially similar OR identified by/promoted in connection with a celebrity chef ("Competitive Business"). Post-term: 2 years, within Designated Area or 3 miles of any Restaurant.
13. **Dispute resolution**: Mediation then binding arbitration (AAA, Dallas TX). Litigation in Dallas TX courts. Texas law governs.
14. **Special risks flagged**: (1) Out-of-state dispute resolution (Texas); (2) Short operating history — franchisor at early stage of development.
15. **Litigation/bankruptcy**: None disclosed (Items 3 and 4).

## Pass D: Territory, Channels, Encroachment (Items 12–16)

### Item 12 — Territory
**Franchise Agreement — Designated Area**:
- Franchisor determines Designated Area based on DMA, population, density, affluence, traffic, topography.
- **Not an exclusive territory** — may face competition from other franchisees, company outlets, other channels/brands.
- Franchisor will not operate or grant another Fish & Chips restaurant in Designated Area during FA term (subject to compliance).
- **Broad reserved rights**:
  1. Advertise within Designated Area
  2. Operate Fish & Chips or other brands outside Designated Area (including adjacent)
  3. Sell collateral products anywhere through any channel (grocery, catalogs, Internet, kiosks, food trucks)
  4. Operate any non-Fish & Chips restaurant/business inside Designated Area under Marks or other marks
  5. Acquire/merge businesses operating within Designated Area
  6. Operate Fish & Chips in Reserved Areas (airports, universities, military bases, hospitals, hotels, casinos, stadiums) inside Designated Area
  7. Deliver/cater from outside restaurants into Designated Area
- No alternative channels (mobile, kiosks, Internet) without approval.
- Territorial rights dependent on full compliance. May modify/reduce territory upon default.
- No options, rights of first refusal for additional franchises.
- May modify Designated Area upon renewal.

**Area Development Agreement — Development Territory**:
- Same broad reserved rights as FA.
- Must develop min 3 restaurants per Development Schedule.
- Failure to meet schedule: may terminate ADA, modify exclusivity, reduce territory, reduce number of remaining restaurants.
- Can purchase one 180-day extension for $12,500.

### Franchise Agreement
- **Initial term**: 10 years

- **Termination by franchisee**: Not applicable.
- **Termination by franchisor without cause**: Not applicable.
- **Termination by franchisor with cause**:
  - *Non-curable defaults* (immediate termination upon notice): material misrepresentation; failure to open by deadline; abandonment; felony conviction; unauthorized transfer; unauthorized use of Marks/Confidential Info; 3+ defaults in 12 months or 10+ during term (regardless if corrected); receipt of ADA or other FA termination notice; failure to pay taxes.
  - *Automatic termination*: bankruptcy, assignment for benefit of creditors, receiver/trustee appointment, attachment/seizure of Restaurant.
  - *Curable defaults* (30-day cure period): failure to pay; inaccurate revenue reporting; failure to comply with law; failing Brand Audit score; inspection deficiencies; lease non-compliance; other FA non-compliance.
  - **Cross-default** between FA and ADA.
- **Post-termination obligations**: Cease operations; pay all amounts owed; pay liquidated damages; discontinue Marks/System/Confidential Information; return Manuals; de-identify restaurant; assign/cancel phone, domain, email, social media; comply with noncompete/confidentiality; comply with franchisor purchase option.

- **Right of first refusal**: Franchisor can match any offer.
- **Franchisor purchase option**: Upon expiration/termination, right to purchase assets and/or assume lease.
- **Death/disability**: 6 months to transfer to approved third party.
- **Noncompete during term**: No involvement with any "Competitive Business" — defined as (1) same/substantially similar to Fish & Chips, including any restaurant where British-style fish and chips ≥25% of menu or gross sales, OR (2) any restaurant identified by/promoted in connection with a celebrity chef.
- **Noncompete post-term**: 2 years within Designated Area OR within 3 miles of any Restaurant.
- **Modification**: Written and signed by both parties only.
- **Integration clause**: Only FA terms binding (subject to state law). Does not disclaim FDD representations.
- **Dispute resolution**: Mediation then binding arbitration (AAA, Dallas TX). Subject to applicable state law.
- **Choice of forum**: Dallas TX (state or federal courts).
- **Choice of law**: Texas law (subject to state law).
