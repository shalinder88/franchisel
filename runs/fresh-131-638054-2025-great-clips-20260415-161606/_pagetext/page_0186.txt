Note 10. 
Employee Benefit Plan 
The Company has an employee benefit plan that provides employees the ability to contribute up to 100% 
of their earnings, up to the maximum allowed by the Internal Revenue Code. The Company provides a 
safe harbor match equal to 100% of the first 2% and 50% of the next 4% of earnings deferred by the 
employee. Employer matching contributions and administrative related fees were approximately 
$1,040,000, $1,048,000 and $890,000 for the years ended December 31, 2024, 2023 and 2022, 
respectively.
