18. 
CONSEQUENCES OF TERMINATION   
 
Upon the termination, expiration or non-renewal of this Agreement: 
 
(a) 
Franchisee’s interest in this Agreement and all rights licensed to Franchisee 
automatically revert to Great Clips.  All goodwill associated with the licensed 
Marks will at all times remain the exclusive property of Great Clips; 
(b) 
Franchisee immediately must cease all use of the Marks, any materials containing 
the Marks and any other confusingly similar name or marks; 
(c) 
Franchisee immediately must assign to Great Clips the telephone number used in 
the operation of the Salon, and Great Clips may direct that an intercept be placed 
on the Salon’s telephone number; 
(d) 
Franchisee immediately must pay all sums it owes to Great Clips or to Great Clips’ 
affiliates, including any liability for outstanding GREAT CLIPS® Gift Cards, and 
all sums it owes to third parties for which Great Clips may also be liable. In the 
event Great Clips owes any money to the Franchisee upon expiration or termination 
of this Agreement or any other agreement between Great Clips and Franchisee, or 
Great Clips and one or more of Franchisee’s owner(s) or affiliate(s), Great Clips is 
entitled to offset that amount with amounts owed to Great Clips or third parties for 
which Great Clips may also be liable; 
(e) 
Franchisee immediately must return to Great Clips any tangible copies of the 
Operations Manual and other manuals, confidential data, all Salon Data, all 
database information, disks or other hardware or storage devices with information 
(if any), software, operational data and customer information, videos, electronic 
media, reports, bulletins or other documents furnished by Great Clips; and 
immediately cease any use of GREAT CLIPS®  University, INsite+ and the other 
Websites, distinctive, proprietary or confidential operational, administrative or 
advertising techniques, systems or know-how, or trade secrets, disclosed to 
Franchisee by Great Clips;
