17.
TERMINATION
17.1   Immediate Termination.  Great Clips will have the absolute right and privilege, 
unless prohibited by applicable law, to immediately terminate this Agreement without any 
opportunity to cure if:  
(a)
any act or practice by Franchisee impairs or imminently threatens to impair the
goodwill associated with the Marks or System, including, without limitation, a
serious health or safety violation;
(b)
Franchisee is determined to be insolvent within the meaning of any state or federal
law or becomes a party to any bankruptcy proceedings;
(c)
Franchisee admits that it is unable to pay debts when they are due, makes an
assignment for the benefit of creditors or enters into an arrangement for the
disposition of its assets for the benefit of creditors;
(d)
Franchisee abandons the GREAT CLIPS® business or closes and fails to operate
the Salon for three (3) consecutive days (not including designated holidays) without
Great Clips’ prior written approval;
(e)
Franchisee is convicted of or pleads guilty or no contest to a charge or violation of
law, or any felony;
(f)
Franchisee assigns an interest in the Franchise Agreement without the consent of
Great Clips;
(g)
Franchisee fails to renew this Agreement in accordance with Section 3.3;
(h)
Franchisee makes a material misrepresentation or omission in its franchise
application;
(i)
Franchisee fails to timely cure a default under the lease;
(j)
Franchisee makes or permits any unauthorized use of Confidential Information;
(k)
Franchisee makes, directly or indirectly through its agents and employees, a
material misuse of customer data and information;
(l)
Franchisee, directly or indirectly through its owner(s) or any affiliates, breaches
any of its confidentiality or non-compete obligations under Sections 20.1 or 20.2.
