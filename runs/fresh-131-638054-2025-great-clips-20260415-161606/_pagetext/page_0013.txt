Senior Vice President of Marketing and Recruiting Resources:  Sarah J. Yatchak 
Ms. Yatchak joined Great Clips in December 2016 and has held various position since that time, 
including serving as Assistant Secretary, Senior Director of Legal and Franchise Administration 
(December 2016 to February 2018), Associate General Counsel and Assistant Secretary (March 
2018 to December 2019), and Secretary and Vice President, Legal/Franchise Administration & 
General Counsel (January 2020 to June 2022). Ms. Yatchak became the Vice President 
of Operations in June 2022 until December 2025 and as of January 2026 she is the Senior 
Vice President of Marketing and Recruiting Resources. All located in Minneapolis, Minnesota.  
Vice President of Learning and Development:  Michelle E. Sack 
Ms. Sack joined Great Clips in July 1993 and has held various positions since that time, including 
serving  as Regional Vice President of Business Services (November 2005 to August 2010), 
Education Vice President (September 2010 to December 2012), Vice President of Learning and 
Development (January 2013 to December 2015), Vice President of Franchise Services and Human 
Resources (January 2016 to September 2018), and has served as Vice President of Learning and 
Development since September 2018, all located in Minneapolis, Minnesota.  
Vice President of Expansion:  Adam J. Husemann 
Mr. Husemann joined Great Clips in December 2014 and has held various positions since that 
time, including serving as Senior Director of Facilities and Purchasing and General Office 
(December 2014 to December 2016), Vice President of Franchise Development and Facilities and 
Purchasing (January 2017 until July 2018), and has served as Vice President of Expansion since 
August 2018, all located in Minneapolis, Minnesota.  
Vice President of Marketing and Communications: Traci L. Elder 
Ms. Elder joined Great Clips in November 2025 as the Vice President of Marketing and 
Communications located in Minneapolis, Minnesota. Prior to joining Great Clips, Ms. Elder was 
employed by Purpose Brands, located in Woodbury, Minnesota, where she served as Vice 
President of Brand Strategy and Creative Expression from January 2023 to October 2025 and prior 
to that role she was the Vice President of Brand Marketing for Waxing the City, the Bar Method 
and Basecamp Fitness from October 2020 to December 2022. Prior to joining Purpose Brands, 
Ms. Elder was the Vice President of New Business and Marketing for Little & Company from 
December 2012 to July 2020, located in Minneapolis, Minnesota.
