Notes to estimated initial investment for a single franchise under a Franchise Agreement: 
 
(1) 
The Initial Franchise Fee is $20,000 if you are applying for a single Franchise Agreement 
with no participation in an incentive program.  See Item 5 for the conditions under which 
the Initial Franchise Fee is refundable.   
 
If you are participating in the Three Star Program, you will sign the Three Star Program 
Agreement, along with three separate Franchise Agreements.  Instead of paying the 
$20,000 Initial Franchise Fee under each Franchise Agreement, you will pay us a non-
refundable Program Fee of $35,000.  The estimates provided in the estimated initial 
investment table above, are applicable to your first franchise under the Three Star Program 
Agreement.  Your estimated initial investment may be less for the second and third 
franchises you develop under the Three Star Program Agreement, provided you have fully 
executed leases for each of the Three Star Franchises within the required 24-month time 
frame, as more fully described in Item 5.
