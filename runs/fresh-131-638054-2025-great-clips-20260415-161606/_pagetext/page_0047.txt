QuickConnect Training Program for Franchisees.  This training covers the basic aspects of 
establishing and operating a GREAT CLIPS® salon.  It is a blend of online training delivered 
through the GREAT CLIPS® University online learning system, virtual learning, and in-person 
training, and includes participation in Great Clips Academy, LEADS, in-salon training, and two 
days of Building Your Legacy classroom training delivered in Minneapolis, Minnesota.  You will 
be granted access to GREAT CLIPS® University upon becoming a GREAT CLIPS® Franchisee 
and will be required to complete a number of mandatory online courses prior to attending each in-
person training session.  Building Your Legacy training presenters’ experience varies, but 
generally these individuals will have five to 25 years of experience in the GREAT CLIPS® 
System.
