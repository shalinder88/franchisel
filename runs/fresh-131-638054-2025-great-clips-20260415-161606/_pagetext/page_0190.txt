GREAT CLIPS® 
FRANCHISE AGREEMENT 
 
 
This Franchise Agreement (this “Agreement”) is made by and between Great Clips, Inc., a 
Minnesota corporation (“Great Clips”), and ____________________________________ (“Franchisee”); 
 
 
WHEREAS, Great Clips has expended considerable time, effort, skill and financial 
resources in developing an image, distinctive brand and business system for the development and 
operation of distinctive retail salons offering haircare and personal grooming products and services 
(the “System”), which products and services are identified and distinguished by the GREAT 
CLIPS® service mark and other trademarks, service marks and trade dress as defined by Great 
Clips from time to time and as licensed in this Agreement (the “Marks”). 
 
 
WHEREAS, Franchisee has had a full and adequate opportunity to be thoroughly advised 
of the terms and conditions of this Agreement by legal counsel or another adviser, and has had 
sufficient time to evaluate and investigate the GREAT CLIPS® System, the financial requirements, 
and the business risks associated with owning and operating a GREAT CLIPS® business and 
Franchisee desires to obtain the right to use the Marks and System in the operation of a GREAT 
CLIPS® salon under the terms and conditions of this Agreement, and 
 
 
WHEREAS, a material component of Franchisee’s decision to acquire the franchised 
business offered herein is to own and operate a salon business.  Franchisee specifically 
acknowledges and understands that the franchised business is separate and distinct from the 
business conducted by Great Clips as a franchisor with ultimate responsibility for the Marks, brand 
and System, and that Franchisee will be solely and exclusively responsible for the exercise of all 
day-to-day operation, controls, management, and performance of the franchised business, 
including with respect to all employment-related matters and general working conditions.  
 
 
NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the covenants and agreements described below, 
Great Clips and Franchisee agree as follows: 
 
1. 
GRANT OF LICENSE   
 
1.1  
Grant.  Great Clips grants to Franchisee, subject to the terms and conditions of this 
Agreement, the right and license to:  (a) establish and operate one GREAT CLIPS®-branded retail 
haircare salon at a location for which Great Clips has provided its consent (the “Salon”); (b) use 
the Marks under the terms of this Agreement to identify and promote the Salon and the business 
conducted and the services and products that Great Clips authorizes to be offered by the Salon; 
and (c) use Great Clips’ proprietary business methods, know-how, standards, requirements, and 
recommendations for the System (which may be changed, improved and further developed from 
time to time) as set forth in the GREAT CLIPS® Operations Manual, other manuals, training 
programs, the Online Resources (as defined in Section 10.6), or other communications to 
Franchisee.  Great Clips has no obligation to enter into additional or new Franchise Agreements
