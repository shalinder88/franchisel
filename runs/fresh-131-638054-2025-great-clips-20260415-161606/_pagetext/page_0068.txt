Newly opened GREAT CLIPS® salons tend to have average sales and cash flows significantly 
below the average for the salons included in Tables 1, 2 and 3 above.  This is especially true of 
new GREAT CLIPS® salons opened by new franchisees in markets that have few existing salons.  
Markets with many GREAT CLIPS® salons and correspondingly larger cooperative advertising 
budgets tend to have salons with higher sales and cash flows than markets with few existing salons.
