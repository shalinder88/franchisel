15.4 
Conditions to Approval of Assignment.  Great Clips will not unreasonably 
withhold consent to the assignment provided that Franchisee and the assignee comply with the 
following conditions and all of the terms, conditions and requirements set forth at this time or from 
time-to-time in the future in Great Clips’ then-current development or other policies:  
(a)
Franchisee and each of Franchisee’s owners in this Agreement must sign a General
Release in favor of Great Clips;
(b)
all current liabilities of Franchisee must be paid in full, including any liability for
outstanding GREAT CLIPS® Gift Cards;
(c)
the Assignment Fee, if required, set forth in Section 15.6 is paid in full;
(d)
Designated Operators must be Franchisees in accordance with the terms of this
Agreement;
(e)
Franchisee will complete, at Franchisee's expense, reasonable refurbishing and
repair of the Salon and its fixtures, furnishings, premises and signage as Great Clips
may prescribe to conform to the then-current System standards;
(f)
if Great Clips has any ongoing liability under Franchisee’s lease for the Salon,
Great Clips will require the assignee to personally and corporately guarantee Great
Clips and may further condition its consent upon receiving a fully-executed copy
of an assignment of lease from Franchisee to the assignee; and
(g)
the assignee must:  (i) be of legal age and capacity to enter into a Great Clips
Franchise Agreement; (ii) meet Great Clips’ standards and qualifications for new
franchisees and assignees, as outlined in Great Clips’ then-current applicable
policies; (iii) agree to operate the Salon as a GREAT CLIPS® salon; (iv)
demonstrate that it is financially qualified to operate the Salon;  (v) meet the
residency requirements set forth in Great Clips’ expansion policies;  (vi) have no
conflicting interests with Great Clips as determined by Great Clips in its sole
determination; (vii) must be eligible for expansion under Great Clips’ applicable
policies); (viii) agree to devote its best efforts to the management of the GREAT
CLIPS® business; (ix) be able to communicate, in English, with Great Clips and
with Franchisee's customers, suppliers and employees; (x) successfully complete
Great Clips’ training program within the time frame established by Great Clips (if
the assignee has experience operating GREAT CLIPS® salons, Great Clips will
consider that experience and performance as a factor in the approval process); and
(xi) assignee and each of assignee’s shareholders in a corporate assignee must sign
a General Release in favor of Great Clips.
