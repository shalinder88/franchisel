In assessing the realization of deferred tax assets, management considers whether it is more likely than 
not that some portion or all of the deferred tax assets will not be realized. The ultimate realization of 
deferred tax assets is dependent upon the generation of future taxable income during the periods in 
which those temporary differences become deductible. Based on the level of projected future taxable 
income over the periods in which the deferred tax assets are deductible, management believes that it is 
more likely than not that the Company will realize all the benefits of these deductible differences on the 
United States and Canadian income tax returns.  
 
The Company recognized no liability for unrecognized tax benefits, interest and penalties related to 
federal and state taxes at December 31, 2024, 2023 and 2022. 
 
The Company files tax returns in the United States federal jurisdiction and various state, local and foreign 
jurisdictions.
