ESH Strategies Franchise LLC is offering franchises for EXTENDED STAY AMERICA PREMIER SUITES® 
hotels that provide extended stay and transient guest lodging services for self-sufficient, value conscious guests.  
The total investment necessary to begin operation of a newly constructed 125-room EXTENDED STAY AMERICA 
PREMIER SUITES hotel, excluding real property, is $10,058,979 to $15,123,386. This includes $66,350 to $87,850 
that must be paid to the franchisor or affiliate. The total investment necessary to begin operation of a 100-room 
conversion EXTENDED STAY AMERICA PREMIER SUITES hotel, excluding real property, is $322,659 to 
$5,590,465. This includes $60,350 to $83,850 that must be paid to the franchisor or affiliate.   We may also offer 
you the right to develop multiple EXTENDED STAY AMERICA PREMIER SUITES hotels under a Development 
Agreement if you pay a Development Fee based upon the number of hotels you agree to open, which must be at 
least two hotels.  The Development Fee will be an amount determined by multiplying $50,000 by the number of 
hotels you agree to open under the Development Agreement, which must be at least two.
