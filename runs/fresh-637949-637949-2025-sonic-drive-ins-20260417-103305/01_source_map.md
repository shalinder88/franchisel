## Key Observations
- Very large franchise system (3,461 total, 3,144 franchised) — mature, established brand
- Net shrinkage: system shrank by 60 units in 2024 (80 closures vs 30 openings franchised)
- Inspire Brands parent — multi-brand conglomerate (Arby's, BWW, Dunkin', Baskin-Robbins, Jimmy John's, Subway)
- Securitization: ~$1,417.3M outstanding under Sonic Securitization Notes
- Three restaurant formats: Traditional Drive-In, C-Store, Drive-Thru Only
- Gag clause present: "some franchisees signed provisions restricting their ability to speak openly"
- Materially different renewal terms warning explicitly stated
