## 1. System Overview

Rocky Rococo is a regional quick-service pizza and pasta franchise system based in Oconomowoc, Wisconsin. The system operates 31 total restaurants (24 franchised, 7 affiliate-owned) across Wisconsin (30) and Minnesota (1) as of year-end 2024. RFC has offered franchises since 1995, with its predecessor Rocky Rococo Corporation having offered franchises from 1982–1987.

The system is controlled by the Hester family through multiple related entities. RFC itself does not operate restaurants; its affiliates (Pizza Rococo Inc., TRH Restaurants Inc./II/III, Airport Pizza Roc Inc.) own and operate the 7 company-side units. The brand specializes in pan-style pizza and pasta with dine-in, carry-out, and delivery service.

## 4. Key Strengths

- **Low supplier control**: Only soft drink supplier is designated. No RFC revenue from franchisee purchases.
- **Healthy franchisor financials**: $669K cash, no debt, growing revenue and net income.
- **Simple fee structure**: Straightforward royalty with a meaningful safety valve (3.5% reduction under $425K).
- **No FPR complexity**: Clean no-FPR election.
- **Long term**: 15+15 years provides operational stability.
- **No renewal fee**: Reduces long-term franchisee cost burden.
- **Growing system**: 6 net new franchised units over 2 years.

## 5. Key Risks and Concerns

- **Regional concentration**: 30 of 31 units in Wisconsin. Heavy dependence on Wisconsin economy.
- **Family control**: All management and affiliates are Hester family-controlled. $168K in related-party management fees. Facility provided at no charge by related party. Succession risk if family leadership changes.
- **No FPR**: No financial performance data disclosed, limiting prospective franchisee due diligence.
- **Territory carveouts**: The 1-mile exclusive territory has significant exceptions (malls, CBD). The document simultaneously grants territory protection and disclaims exclusivity.
- **Noncompete discrepancy**: FA post-term noncompete is 5 miles; FDA noncompete is 50 miles. The 10x difference creates uncertainty.
- **No state addenda**: Despite registration in 5 states, no state-specific addenda are included. State law protections are referenced only through "subject to State Law" qualifiers.
- **Renewal at then-current terms**: Renewal requires signing then-current FA which "may contain materially different terms and conditions." Franchisees face uncertainty about renewal economics.
- **Computer system upgrade liability**: RFC may require system upgrades with unknown frequency and cost, and the system may not be amortizable over the remaining term.

## 10. Regression Recovery Findings (Post-Regression Pass)

- **RR-01 (FA Deep Read)**: 10 operative FA provisions recovered and promoted to canonical:
  - **Security interest / UCC lien**: RFC holds first lien on all franchisee property (equipment, inventory, fixtures, leasehold improvements, proceeds). Subordination available for bank financing.
  - **Insurance**: 5 coverage types required. RFC as additional insured. Waiver of subrogation. 30-day cancellation notice. Minimums in Operations Manual.
  - **EFT / Direct debit**: RFC may require EFT for all fees. Franchisee must authorize direct debits. RFC may debit based on computer system data if sales not reported.
  - **Data ownership**: RFC has right at all times to retrieve all information from franchisee computer system. All data is exclusive property of RFC.
  - **System modification**: RFC may change the system; franchisee must accept and fund changes.
  - **Remodel**: At least once during term; not required during last 4 years. 10-day cure for deficiencies.
  - **Computer upgrade cap**: $10,000 cap if franchisee spent $10K+ in previous 5 years.
  - **Gift cards**: Must purchase through RFC; may not produce own.
  - **Payment application**: RFC has sole discretion to apply payments to past due amounts.
  - **Credit card acceptance**: Must accept all forms RFC designates.

- **RR-02 (FS Notes Recovery)**: 4 families recovered:
  - **Advertising expense actuals**: $270,344 (2024), $229,359 (2023), $152,565 (2022). RFC spent slightly more than collected ad fees each year.
  - **VIE election**: RFC elected not to consolidate Hester family entities per ASU 2018-17. Financial statements reflect only the franchisor.
  - **Note 4 tax split**: Current/deferred breakdown for all 3 years.
  - **Accounting policies structured**: Depreciation, receivables, impairment, revenue recognition, subsequent events.

## 11. Conflict Adjudications

- **C1 (Noncompete radius)**: FA 5-mile governs operating franchisees; FDA 50-mile governs pre-franchise applicants. Different agreements, different phases. Adjudicated: FA controls for post-termination.
- **C2 (Computer access)**: FA Sect. IX.D clearly grants RFC full data access rights, overriding the contradictory Item 11 p.32 statement. Adjudicated: RFC has full access.
- **C3 (Royalty reduction mechanism)**: Item 6 says "refund"; FS Note 1 says "credit to future balance." Different contexts (contractual vs. accounting). Both values preserved with reconciliation.
