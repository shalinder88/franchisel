PROPOSED TERMS 
Property 
Improvement Plan 
(Conversion only) 
You will pay $10,000 for a property improvement plan (“PIP”) to establish 
the renovation requirements for the Hotel.  
  
If applicable: We will apply the PIP fee as a credit against the Application 
Fee, if you submit a complete Application for the Hotel within six months 
from the date of this letter. 
 
Term 
The Franchise Agreement will be effective on the date the Committee 
approves your Application (the “Effective Date”) and expire at midnight on 
the last day of the month  
 
NEW BUILD – twenty-three (23) years after the Effective Date. 
 
ALTERNATIVE – twenty (20) years after the authorized opening of the 
Hotel under the Brand (the “Opening Date”). 
 
CONVERSION – ____ years after the authorized opening of the Hotel 
under the Brand (the “Opening Date”). 
 
Monthly Royalty Fee 
The current standard Monthly Royalty Fee is 5% of the Hotel’s Gross 
Rooms Revenue (“GRR”) for the preceding calendar month for the term of 
the Franchise Agreement.  
 
OPTIONAL:  The Monthly Royalty Fee for the Hotel will be modified as 
follows:   
 
Year 1*:   ___ % of GRR 
Year 2:     ___ % of GRR 
Year 3:     ___ % of GRR through the remaining Term 
 
* Through the first twelve full calendar months after the Opening Date. 
 
Monthly Program 
Fee 
 
4% of monthly GRR, subject to change.  This rate will not be increased more 
than 1% of GRR during the Term. 
 
Hilton Honors Fee 
Currently, 4.3% of total eligible guest folio. This fee is waived for stays in 
which the guest is enrolled on-property in Hilton Honors.
