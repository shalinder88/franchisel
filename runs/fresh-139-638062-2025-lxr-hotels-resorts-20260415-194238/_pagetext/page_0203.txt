Guaranty 
If you and your Affiliates are new to the System, do not have an established 
credit history with us, are not current on fees or charges due under an 
agreement with us or our Affiliates, or have been in default under an 
agreement with us or our Affiliates in the past three years, we may require 
you or an upstream corporate entity to sign a guaranty of franchisee’s 
obligations under the Franchise Agreement.  In addition, if you do not own 
fee title to the real property and improvements that comprise the Hotel and 
Hotel Site (or hold a ground lease for the Term of the Franchise Agreement), 
we may require the fee owner or another third-party acceptable to us to 
execute a guaranty of your obligations under the Franchise Agreement.  We 
may also require a guaranty if you obtain a loan that is or will be cross-
defaulted to other loans, or cross-collateralized by other real estate, and 
request a lender comfort letter from us.
