Required Computer 
Systems 
Our required business computer system is called “OnQ.” It provides a suite 
of functionality for hotel operations, reservations, sales, distribution, revenue 
management, business intelligence, and other services.  It is comprised of 
hardware and software components. You may purchase the hardware from 
our affiliate, HSS, or any preferred providers. You must license the software 
from HSS. You will pay for the software, installation, and configuration 
charges about 45 days before your hotel opens. You must also pay for the 
necessary communications vehicles, ongoing maintenance and support, and 
certain third-party software licenses such as virus removal tools. You must 
refresh (replace and upgrade) the hardware and software about every 3 
years. About 90-120 days before your hotel opens, you must sign a separate 
agreement for OnQ and any related agreements we require, which will 
govern your use of this system.
