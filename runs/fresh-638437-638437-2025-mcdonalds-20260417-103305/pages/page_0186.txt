INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR AGREEMENT
THIS INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR AGREEMENT (this “Agreement”) is made as of
________, 20__ (the “Effective Date”), by and between MCDONALD’S USA, LLC, a Delaware limited
liability company with an address of 110 North Carpenter Street Chicago, IL 60607 (“McDonald’s”), and
_________________________, an individual with a mailing address of _____________________,
(“Candidate”). McDonald’s and Candidate are sometimes referred to herein as the “Parties.”
Background
A.
Candidate wishes to be considered by McDonald’s for a McDonald’s franchise to operate
a McDonald’s restaurant.
B.
Before McDonald’s will consider Candidate for any McDonald’s franchise opportunity,
Candidate must first complete, to McDonald’s satisfaction in its sole discretion, McDonald’s Franchise
Candidate Training and Evaluation Program consisting of McDonald’s restaurant and classroom experience
and training (“Training Program”).
C.
McDonald’s and Candidate have separately entered into that certain preliminary agreement
(the “Preliminary Agreement”), dated on or about the date hereof, with respect to the Candidate’s
application to receive a franchise opportunity and enter the Training Program.
D.
Candidate has requested that he or she be permitted to participate in the Training Program,
and McDonald’s is willing to allow Candidate to do so, upon the following terms and conditions of this
Agreement.
Agreement
McDonald’s and Candidate, each intending to be legally bound and for good and valuable
consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which is acknowledged, hereby agrees as follows:
1.
Candidate shall, subject to the terms and conditions of this Agreement and the Preliminary
Agreement, train within the Training Program in one or more McDonald’s restaurants commencing on the
Effective Date.
2.
McDonald’s reserves the right from time to time to establish and modify the guidelines and
standards applicable to the Training Program. Candidate agrees to comply with the guidelines and
standards of the Training Program at all times.
3.
To the fullest extent permitted by law, Candidate shall be an independent contractor
hereunder, and Candidate shall not be deemed an agent, employee, joint employee or servant of
McDonald’s or any subsidiary or affiliates of McDonald’s or McDonald’s Corporation, a Delaware
corporation and the parent of McDonald’s (“McDonald’s Corporation”) (collectively, the “Affiliates”).
