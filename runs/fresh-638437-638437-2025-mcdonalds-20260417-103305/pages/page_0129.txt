SCHEDULE A
Pollution Coverage (rev. 6/25)
The standard commercial general liability insurance purchased by owner/operators excludes pollution
and/or environmental contamination. Since the oil alliance sites have a pollution exposure if
contamination is caused by negligence of McDonald’s or the owner/operator, an Environmental
Impairment Liability Policy (Pollution) has been purchased by McDonald’s for the protection of the
owner/operators and McDonald’s.
This coverage is being purchased on behalf of the owner/operators because, individually, such insurance
is either not available or the cost is prohibitive.
All Oil Alliance sites are covered under this policy.
