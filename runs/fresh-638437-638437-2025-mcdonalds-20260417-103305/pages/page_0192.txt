In cases where McDonald’s, through the New Term Committee, concludes that it will not offer a new term franchise
to the Owner/Operator, the Owner/Operator will be advised of this decision in writing. The decision of the New
Term Committee not to offer a new term franchise to the Owner/Operator is final. In these cases, McDonald’s may
extend an alternative offer, which would give the Owner/Operator the opportunity to sell the restaurant business to
a qualified buyer prior to the start of the new term franchise. Subject to the terms and conditions stated in any
alternative offer, which includes a legal release, McDonald’s will offer a new term franchise to the qualified buyer.
McDonald’s may deviate from the above process when considering a new term franchise for non-traditional venues.
