CANDIDATE AGREEMENTS
PRELIMINARY AGREEMENT
THIS PRELIMINARY AGREEMENT (“Agreement”) is made as of ________, __ (the
“Effective Date”), by and between MCDONALD’S USA, LLC, a Delaware limited liability company with
an address of 110 North Carpenter Street Chicago, IL 60607 (“McDonald’s”), and
_________________________, an individual with a mailing address of _____________________,
(“Candidate”). McDonald’s and Candidate are sometimes referred to herein as the “Parties.”
Background
A.
Candidate wishes to be considered by McDonald’s for a McDonald’s franchise to operate
a McDonald’s restaurant.
B.
Before McDonald’s will consider Candidate for any McDonald’s franchise opportunity,
Candidate must first complete, to McDonald’s satisfaction in its sole discretion, McDonald’s Franchise
Applicant Training and Evaluation Program consisting of McDonald’s restaurant and classroom experience
and training (“Training Program”).
C.
Candidate understands that Candidate’s participation in the Training Program hereunder
may exceed a period of two years, without any commitment or promise of a McDonald’s franchise.
D.
McDonald’s and Candidate have separately entered into that certain independent contractor
agreement (the “Contractor Agreement”), dated on or about the date hereof, with respect to the Candidate’s
participation in the Training Program.
E.
The satisfactory completion of the Training Program, in McDonald’s sole discretion, is no
guarantee that Candidate will be offered a franchise opportunity. Upon satisfactory completion of the
Training Program, the decision to offer Candidate any franchise opportunity rests in McDonald’s sole
discretion.
Agreement
McDonald’s and Candidate, each intending to be legally bound and for good and valuable
consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which is acknowledged, hereby agrees as follows:
1.
McDonald’s authorizes Candidate to participate in the Training Program until further
notice by McDonald’s. McDonald’s reserves the right from time to time to establish and modify the
guidelines and standards applicable to the Training Program. Candidate agrees to comply with the
guidelines and standards of the Training Program at all times.
2.
McDonald’s decision to consider Candidate for any franchise opportunity as well as the
location and type of franchise opportunity to be offered, if any, and the terms of any contracts rest in
McDonald’s sole discretion. McDonald’s is unable, at this date, to determine the exact type of the franchise
opportunity and contracts, if any, which may ultimately be offered to Candidate.
