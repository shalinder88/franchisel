ESH Strategies Franchise LLC is offering franchises for EXTENDED STAY AMERICA SELECT SUITES® hotels 
that provide extended stay guest lodging services for self-sufficient, value conscious guests seeking flexible stay 
terms at affordable rates in a guest-centric environment. 
The total investment necessary to begin operation of a newly constructed 123-room EXTENDED STAY AMERICA 
SELECT SUITES® hotel, excluding real property, is $8,612,237 to $13,285,766. This includes $66,350 to $87,850 
that must be paid to the franchisor or affiliate. The total investment necessary to begin operation of a 100-room 
conversion EXTENDED STAY AMERICA SELECT SUITES hotel, excluding real property, is $195,146 to 
$3,759,926. This includes $60,350 to $83,850 that must be paid to the franchisor or affiliate.

plain English. Read this Disclosure Document and all accompanying agreements carefully. You must receive this

verified the information contained in this document.
