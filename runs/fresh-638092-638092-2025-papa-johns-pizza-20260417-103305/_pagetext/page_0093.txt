         Confidentiality Agreements

          In some instances, current and former franchisees sign provisions restricting their ability to
 speak openly about their experience with Papa Johns. You may wish to speak with current and former
 franchisees, but be aware that not all such franchisees will be able to communicate with you. Although
 some current and former franchisees have signed confidentiality clauses during our last three fiscal
 years, the clauses have been in the context of settlement agreements for disputes, litigation or arbitration
 actions between us and the franchisee (or former franchisee) requiring both parties to keep the terms of
 the settlement confidential and not to disparage the other party or its business.
