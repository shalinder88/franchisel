               (b)     Approved Operator.

                        (i)    Each member of the Franchise Group acknowledges and agrees that
an individual designated by Franchisee and approved by Franchisor shall be required to supervise
the operation of the Covered Restaurants in a designated market area (“DMA”) throughout the
entire term of the related Covered Franchise Agreements (such individuals and any replacements
thereof hired pursuant to this Section 2.(b)(i), an “Approved Principal Operator”). At all times
during the Term of this Agreement, the Franchise Group shall cause such an Approved Principal
Operator to (1) be employed on a full-time basis in connection with the Covered Restaurants in his
or her DMA and (2) have the primary responsibility and authority to control the day-to-day
management and operations of each of the Covered Restaurants.

                        (ii)   If at any time during the Term, an Approved Principal Operator (1)
is no longer employed on a full-time basis in connection with any of the Covered Restaurants in
his or her DMA for any reason or (2) no longer has primary responsibility and authority to control
the day-to-day management and operations of any of the Covered Restaurants (each of clauses (1)
and (2) above, an “Approved Principal Operator Termination”), then the Franchise Group
shall, as soon as reasonably practicable but in no event later than 30 calendar days after such
Approved Principal Operator Termination, replace such Approved Principal Operator with a
similarly-qualified individual with significant managerial experience in quick-service restaurant
operations to serve as a replacement Approved Principal Operator, provided that the hiring of any
such replacement Approved Principal Operator shall be subject to Franchisor’s prior written
consent and approval. Any failure by the Franchise Group to replace an Approved Principal
Operator within 60 days after an Approved Principal Operator Termination shall constitute a
breach of the Covered Franchise Agreements that will entitle Franchisor or its applicable Affiliates
to terminate any or all of such Covered Franchise Agreements.
