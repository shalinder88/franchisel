## Special Risk Flags (from page 4)

1. **Out-of-State Dispute Resolution** — litigation only in New York
2. **Short Operating History** — franchisor at early stage, limited history
3. **Sales Performance Required** — $1,250,000 minimum gross revenues per calendar year; failure removes territorial protections
4. **Financial Condition** — franchisor's financial statements call into question ability to provide services and support

## Key Observations

- Item 19: **No financial performance representation made** — this is a material absence
- Item 20: System is extremely small — 1 company-owned restaurant (NYC), 0 operating franchised units as of 12/31/2024
  - 1 franchised ghost kitchen opened in 2023 (DoorDash), ceased operations 2024
  - 1 franchise agreement signed for Las Vegas food hall (not yet opened)
- Item 21: Franchisor is pre-revenue for franchising; net losses all 3 years ($51,767 in 2022; $81,337 in 2023; $76,856 in 2024)
- Total assets only $33,040 as of 12/31/2024; members' equity only $1,040
- $120,000 capital contribution in Feb 2025 (subsequent event)
- UK master franchise sold September 2023
- No advertising fund established yet
- No arbitration/mediation provision — litigation only
