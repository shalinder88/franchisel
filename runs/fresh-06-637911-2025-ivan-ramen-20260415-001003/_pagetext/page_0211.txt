to you.

4. TERM

Term 
The term ("Term") of this Agreement will be for a period beginning on the date we sign this 
Agreement and extending until the earlier of either the actual or scheduled Date of Execution of the 
last Franchise Agreement executed pursuant to this Agreement and specified in Section 6.01 of this 
Agreement, unless sooner terminated in accordance with the provisions of this Agreement.
