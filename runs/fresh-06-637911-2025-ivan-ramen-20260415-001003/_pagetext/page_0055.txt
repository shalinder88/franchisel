p. Your death 
or disability 
Section 12.04 
On your death or disability (if you are an individual), or the death or 
disability of your last surviving owner (if you are a business entity), that 
person’s rights pass to his or her "Estate". The Estate may continue 
operating the Business if it provides an acceptable Area Business 
Manager. This Area Business Manager must assume full time operation 
of the franchise within 90 days of death or disability.
