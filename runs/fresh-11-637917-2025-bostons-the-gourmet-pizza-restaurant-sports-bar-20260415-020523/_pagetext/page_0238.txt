23. WAIVER OF JURY TRIAL 
THE PARTIES IRREVOCABLY WAIVE TRIAL BY JURY IN ANY ACTION, 
PROCEEDING OR COUNTERCLAIM, WHETHER AT LAW OR IN EQUITY, BROUGHT BY 
EITHER PARTY RELATING TO THE RELATIONSHIP BETWEEN THE PARTIES OR 
ARISING UNDER OR IN ANY WAY CONNECTED WITH THIS AGREEMENT OR ANY RIGHT 
OR REMEDY HEREUNDER. 
24. OTHER AGREEMENTS/CROSS DEFAULT 
If there is any conflict between this Agreement and the Franchise Agreement, the Franchise 
Agreement shall prevail. Franchisee shall be deemed to have committed a “Material Default” of the 
Franchise Agreement if Franchisee: 
(a) fails to comply with any of its respective obligations hereunder for a period of 14 days 
after written notice of such default has been delivered by BPR to Franchisee, or 
(b) fails to comply at any time with its obligations under Section 7 of this Agreement.
