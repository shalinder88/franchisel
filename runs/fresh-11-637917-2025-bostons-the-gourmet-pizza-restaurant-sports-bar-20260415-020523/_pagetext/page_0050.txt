Section 18 
Owners may not have an interest in, own, 
manage, operate, finance, control or participate 
in any competitive business within the 
Development Area or within  radius of ten miles 
of the Development Area for two years 
following termination or expiration.  If you or 
your Owner engages in any activities prohibited 
by the Franchise Agreement during the 
restricted period, then the restricted period 
applicable to you or the non-compliant Owner 
shall be extended by the period of time during 
which you or the non-compliant Owner, as 
applicable, engaged in the prohibited activities. 
(Subject to applicable state law.)
