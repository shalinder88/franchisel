18. SUBSEQUENT EVENTS

   The Company has evaluated events through March 14, 2025, the date these consolidated financial
   statements were available for issuance, for consideration as a subsequent event to be included in the
   accompanying consolidated financial statements.

   On November 8, 2024, the Company and its equity holders entered into an Equity Purchase Agreement
   with Submarine Buyer LLC (the “Buyer”), a Delaware limited liability company controlled by affiliates of
   Blackstone Inc. Pursuant to the Equity Purchase Agreement, Jersey Mike’s Inc. (“Seller”) formed a new
   Delaware limited liability company, Jersey Mike’s HoldCo LLC, to indirectly hold 100% of the equity
   interests of the Company (the “Transaction”). On January 16, 2025, the Company was acquired for an
   enterprise value of $8.0 billion which includes a maximum additional $250 million in cash and is payable
   upon reaching certain earnout conditions or upon a change in control event. Upon completion of the
   transaction, the 2251 Landmark Place, LLC and Farnborough, LLC entities were transferred to the Seller
   and will decrease property and equipment by approximately $79 million and notes payable by
   approximately $56 million. As of the date these financial statements are available to be issued, the
   preliminary purchase price allocation accounting for the Transaction is not yet complete. The Company
   expects to finalize its analysis within the measurement period (up to one year from the acquisition
   date) of the Transaction. As part of the Transaction, the Company issued $100 million Series 2024-1
   Variable Funding Senior Secured Notes which will allow the Company to borrow amounts as needed on
   a revolving basis.
