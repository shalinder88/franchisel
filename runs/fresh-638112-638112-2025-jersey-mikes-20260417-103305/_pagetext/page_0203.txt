14. COMMITMENTS AND CONTINGENCIES

   Lease Guarantees—As of December 31, 2024, the Company has guaranteed the payment of
   approximately $21,500,000 of corporate store and franchisee leases through July 2037. In the event of
   default by a franchisee where the Company is called upon to perform under its guarantee, the
   Company has the right to pursue repayment from the franchisee. The Company does not receive the
   benefits of the lessee in regard to the right to use the asset and, therefore, these guarantees are not
   included on the consolidated balance sheets. As of December 31, 2024 and 2023, the Company has
   historically received no judgments of default for any lease as the guarantor.

   Commitments—For the years ended December 31, 2024, 2023, and 2022, rent expense was
   approximately $2,790,000, $2,350,000, and $1,910,000, respectively.

   The Company entered into multiple legally binding agreements for sponsorships, promotions, and
   naming rights. The terms of these agreements call for payments over the lifetime of the agreements
   remaining approximately $150 million through June 2041.
