          cleaning, restoration, mitigation, and reconstruction services; construction services; mold
          remediation and bioremediation services; and cleaning and disinfection services. As of

          September 2024. Its immediate predecessor, Tropical Smoothie Cafe, LLC, offered

          297 franchised and 24 company-owned 7 BREW® Stores in operation in the United States.
