On November 8, 2024, the Master Issuer and its equity holders entered into an Equity Purchase
Agreement with Submarine Buyer LLC (the “Buyer”), a Delaware limited liability company controlled by
affiliates of Blackstone Inc. Pursuant to the Equity Purchase Agreement, Jersey Mike’s Inc. (“Seller”)
formed a new Delaware limited liability company, Jersey Mike’s HoldCo LLC, to indirectly hold 100% of
the equity interests of the Company (the “Transaction”). On January 16, 2025, the Master Issuer was
acquired for an enterprise value of $8.0 billion which includes a maximum additional $250 million in
cash and is payable upon reaching certain earnout conditions or upon a change in control event. As of
the date these financial statements are available to be issued, the preliminary purchase price allocation
accounting for the Transaction is not yet complete. The Master Issuer expects to finalize its analysis
within the measurement period (up to one year from the acquisition date) of the Transaction. As part
of the Transaction, the Master Issuer issued $100 million Series 2024-1 Variable Funding Senior Secured
Notes which will allow the Master Issuer to borrow amounts as needed on a revolving basis.
