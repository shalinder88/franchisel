When incurred 
Gift card program fees are allocated based 
on a shared cost model between franchisees 
and the national marketing fund (“NMF”). 
Currently, franchisees pay fees equaling 3% 
of total gift card redemptions, which ADQ 
estimates will be about $200 per year per 
location. NMF covers the balance of the gift 
card program’s costs. In the future, the 
percentage allocation of costs between 
franchisees and NMF may change.
