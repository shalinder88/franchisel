Senior Vice President, Operations Center of Excellence: Roger C. Brewin

1, 2026. He served as Vice President of U.S. Franchise Operations, West from May 2021 through

Vice President, Operations East for U.S. and Canada: David Giacone 
David Giacone has served as Vice President, Operations East for U.S. and Canada since January 
1, 2026. He served as Vice President of U.S. Franchise Operations, East from May 2021 through 
December 2025 and as Vice President of Concept Support Services from July 2018 through April 
2021.  Mr. Giacone was employed as Director of Operations for the Texas Region from February 
2017 through June 2018 and Director of Development Operations from 2013 to 2017.  From 2011

of ADQ. From 2000 to 2011, Mr. Giacone held various field operation positions with ADQ. 
 
Vice President, Operations West for U.S. and Canada: Theresa Lear
