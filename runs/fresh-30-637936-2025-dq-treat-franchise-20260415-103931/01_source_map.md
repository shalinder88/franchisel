## Risk Flags — Early
1. **No FPR (Item 19)**: ADQ does not make any financial performance representations. No FPR tables, no revenue data.
2. **Shrinking system**: Direct-licensed outlets: 828 → 789 → 751 → 727 (2021–2024). Subfranchised territory outlets: 289 → 376 → 371 → 358. Net system is declining.
3. **No exclusive territory**: Site-only franchise. No minimum area or territory granted.
4. **Materially different renewal terms**: Renewal requires signing then-current agreement, which "may differ materially, including higher or additional fees."
5. **Retained deficit**: IDQ has retained deficit of $(68,411K) as of Dec 31, 2024, driven by dividends exceeding net income over time ($110M dividends vs $97.9M net income in 2024).
6. **Out-of-state dispute resolution**: Arbitration in Minneapolis, MN (subject to state law overrides).
7. **Gag clause flag**: "In some instances, during the last three fiscal years, current and former franchisees have signed provisions restricting their ability to speak openly about their experience with ADQ." (Page 67)
8. **Maryland financial assurance**: Maryland Securities Commissioner required initial fee deferral until opening (page 73).
9. **TOC/body mismatch**: Texas listed in TOC state addenda but not present; Maryland present but not in TOC.
10. **Termination fee**: 2x last 12 months continuing license fee upon default termination.
