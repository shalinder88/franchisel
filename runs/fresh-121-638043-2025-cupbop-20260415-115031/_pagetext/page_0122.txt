JOINT AND SEVERAL LIABILITY. 
If two or more persons, corporations, limited liability companies, partnerships or other 
entities or any combination thereof, sign this Agreement, the liability of each shall be joint and 
several. All members of a general partnership and all members of any association or other 
unincorporated entity, which is part of Franchisee, are jointly and severally liable for Franchisee’s 
performance hereunder.
