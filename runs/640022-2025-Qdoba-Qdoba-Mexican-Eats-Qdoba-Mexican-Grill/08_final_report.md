## 1. System Overview

Qdoba operates 827 restaurants (175 company, 652 franchised) across 45 U.S. states, DC, Puerto Rico, Japan, South Korea, and Canada as of September 28, 2025. The brand is positioned in the fast-casual Mexican segment, competing directly with Chipotle and other national/regional chains.

The franchisor entity was created as a special-purpose vehicle in the November 2023 securitization transaction. Qdoba Restaurant Corporation (QRC), the predecessor franchisor, now serves as management services provider. The parent chain runs through Qdoba Issuer LLC → Qdoba Funding Holdco LLC → QRC → Qdoba Corporation → New MRC Holdings LLC, ultimately linked to Butterfly Equity LP.

The system experienced a major refranchising wave in FY2023 (110 company-owned restaurants sold to franchisees), shifting from ~39% franchised to ~79% franchised over three fiscal years. The system grew from 733 total outlets (FY2023 start) to 827 (FY2025 end), net +94.

## 2. Entry Economics

**Traditional franchise:** $40,000 initial franchise fee, total investment $548,100–$1,294,000.
**Non-traditional (license):** $20,000 initial fee, total investment $234,500–$898,000.
**Development Agreement:** $10,000/site development fee (credited to franchise fee).

**Recurring fees (traditional):**
- Royalty: 5% of Gross Sales (max 7% with franchisee vote)
- Marketing: 4.5% of Gross Sales (max 6% with system vote; was 4.0% in FY2025)
- Technology: $250/restaurant/period + 0.21% of Gross Sales (~$6,800/yr at median AUV)
- LMS: $24–$40/month

**Cash incentive:** Up to $100,000/restaurant for early openings (limited to first 150 qualifying units).

## 6. Contract Burden

- **Term:** 10 years, 1 renewal for 10 years
- **Non-compete:** During term + 2 years post-term within 10 miles of ANY Qdoba
- **Dispute resolution:** Mediation → binding arbitration, San Diego CA (JAMS)
- **Governing law:** California (modified by state addenda for 11 states)
- **Guaranty:** Personal guarantee required from entity owners and spouses
- **Security interest:** First-priority security interest in franchise assets
- **ACH authorization:** Franchisor has direct bank account access
- **Technology modification:** Uncapped; 90 days notice for fee changes
