# RT-05: Master Technology Agreement Deep Read — Exhibit K

## Agreement: Master Technology Agreement (pages 417-424)

### Parties
- Qdoba Franchisor LLC ("Qdoba")
- Franchisee

### Technology Fee Structure
- **Base fee:** $250 per restaurant per period (13 periods per year = $3,250/year base)
- **Variable fee:** 0.21% of Gross Sales
- **Example at $1.7M revenue:** $3,250 + $3,570 = ~$6,820/year total technology fee
- Fees subject to modification with 90 days written notice

### Scope of Services Provided by Qdoba
- POS system configuration and setup
- Credit card system configuration
- Inventory management system setup
- Kitchen display system
- Online ordering system integration
- Catering platform integration
- Guest loyalty system
- IT helpdesk support
- System monitoring and maintenance

### Franchisee Technology Responsibilities
- Purchase and install approved POS hardware (minimum 2 terminals)
- Purchase and install hardware firewall (stateful packet inspection capable)
- Maintain structured cabling per specifications
- Maintain internet connectivity meeting minimum speed requirements
- Maintain PCI-DSS compliance
- Run approved PA-DSS version of POS software
- Allow automatic software updates
- Maintain UPS (uninterruptible power supply) for POS systems
- Replace end-of-life hardware as directed

### Data Rights
- Qdoba retains access to all POS data, transaction data, and operational data
- Franchisee data transmitted to Qdoba through POS systems
- Qdoba may use aggregated data for system-wide analysis, benchmarking, marketing

### Modification Rights
- Qdoba may modify technology requirements at any time
- 90 days notice for fee changes
- No stated cap on capital expenditures required for technology upgrades
- Franchisee must comply with all system changes within specified timeframes

### Termination
- Coterminous with Franchise Agreement
- Qdoba may suspend technology services for non-payment or non-compliance
- Upon termination, franchisee must discontinue use of all Qdoba technology systems

### Key Risk Factors
1. **Uncapped technology costs:** No ceiling on what Qdoba can require in technology investment
2. **Unilateral modification:** Qdoba can change systems/fees with only 90 days notice
3. **Data ownership:** Qdoba controls all franchise data flowing through its systems
4. **Suspension rights:** Non-compliance can trigger technology suspension, effectively shuttering operations
5. **Mandatory third-party delivery:** Must participate in delivery platforms as directed
