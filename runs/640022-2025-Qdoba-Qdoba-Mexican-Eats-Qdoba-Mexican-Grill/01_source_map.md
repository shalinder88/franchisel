## Notes

1. **Exhibit A (Funding Holdco) CID encoding issue:** Pages 76–96 (Qdoba Funding Holdco LLC financial statements) use CID-encoded fonts that produce garbled text on standard extraction. The balance sheets, income statements, and cash flow statements exist but require CID font decoding. The independent auditor's report (pages 77–78) is readable.

2. **Exhibit A (Qdoba Corporation)** on pages 97–126 is fully readable with standard text extraction. This includes audited consolidated financial statements as of September 28, 2025 and September 29, 2024.

3. **Guaranty of performance pages** appear at pages 125–126 (end of Exhibit A), but use CID encoding and are only partially decodable.

4. **Franchisee list (Exhibit D)** spans 33 pages (131–163) and includes: current franchised locations (pages 132–160), former franchisees and store closures (pages 161–162), and signed-but-not-opened agreements (page 163).

5. **State-specific addenda (Exhibit H)** cover states: CA, HI, IL, MD, MI, MN, NY, ND, RI, VA, WA — spanning pages 333–409.

6. **No separate guaranty exhibit** — guaranties are embedded as exhibits within the Franchise Agreement (Exhibit B to FA) and Development Agreement.

7. **Item 19 contains 3 charts:** Chart 1 (same-store sales growth), Chart 2 (quartile net sales), Chart 3 (historical sales, costs, and EBITDA).

8. **Item 20 contains 5 tables:** Table 1 (systemwide summary), Table 2 (transfers), Table 3 (franchised outlet status), Table 4 (company-owned status), Table 5 (projected openings).
