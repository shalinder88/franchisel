1. **Marketing fee rate contradiction:** FY2025 data in Item 19 Chart 3 uses 4.0% combined advertising fee, but current rate effective 9/29/2025 is 4.5%. This is noted as a contradiction in canonical and accurately reflects the timing difference — historical data is correct for reported period but understates current fee burden. **Preserved correctly.**

2. **CID encoding unresolved:** Funding Holdco financials use CID-encoded fonts that prevent reliable text extraction. This is documented in source map, reader report, and canonical with clear severity assessment. **Preserved correctly.**

3. **Three-year financial statement gap:** Funding Holdco entity formed September 7, 2023 and cannot provide three full years of audited statements. Noted in canonical and reader report with guaranty of performance as mitigation. **Preserved correctly.**

4. **Refranchising trajectory:** 110 company restaurants sold to franchisees in FY2023 dramatically changed system composition. This structural shift is documented but its implications for same-store performance comparisons are not explicitly analyzed. **Preserved with note.**
