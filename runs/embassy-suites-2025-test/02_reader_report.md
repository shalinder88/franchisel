# Reader Report — Embassy Suites by Hilton 2025

## Pass A — Executive snapshot

1. Hilton Franchise Holding LLC (Delaware LLC, formed Sep 2007) franchises Embassy Suites in the US; parent Hilton Domestic Operating Company Inc. → Hilton Worldwide Holdings Inc. (NYSE: HLT).
2. System size 2024-12-31: **237 franchised** US hotels, **0 company-owned**. Net growth +1 each of 2022, 2023, 2024 (flat to very slow).
3. Projected openings for 2025: **3 new franchised** hotels (Alabama 1, Tennessee 2), 0 company-owned. 29 signed but unopened FAs.
4. Investment range (176-suite new build ex real estate): **$50,082,670 – $82,170,820**. Up to $326,179 payable to Hilton or affiliates.
5. Royalty ramp: **3.5% Y1 / 4.5% Y2 / 5.5% Y3+** of Gross Rooms Revenue (new build); **5.5% flat** on COO/Re-licensing.
6. **Program Fee 4% GRR** (monthly, can increase by up to +1% over term). Hilton Honors **4.3%** of eligible guest folio. Total ongoing % burden ≥ 13.8% GRR on a mature hotel excluding add-ons.
7. Franchise Application Fee: $100,000 New Build / $200,000 COO / $100,000 Re-licensing (+$400/room over 250).
8. Term: **23 years** for new construction, **10–20 years** conversion. **No renewal right** — re-licensing at Hilton's sole discretion with potentially materially different terms.
9. **No exclusive territory** granted under standard FA. Optional "Restricted Area Provision" can be negotiated; carve-outs broad (existing hotels, brand-chain acquisitions, other Hilton brands like Hilton Garden Inn, DoubleTree, Homewood).
10. Item 19: 220 "Comparable Hotels" (32 company-managed, 188 franchisee-managed). 2024 Avg Room Rate **$184.12**, Avg Occupancy **74.4%**, RevPAR Index **121.7**. Franchisee-managed hotels trail company-managed materially on room rate and occupancy index. **No profit/EBITDA disclosure.**
11. Item 21: Clean audit opinion by **Cherry Bekaert** (Tysons Corner VA, dated 2025-03-18) on 2024 and 2023 balance sheets.
12. Litigation: Several pending antitrust class actions (extended stay, luxury hotel pricing via IDeaS/STR/Amadeus) naming Hilton Worldwide. Concluded Texas ($2.1M) and Nebraska ($300K) resort-fee disclosure settlements.
13. Governing law **New York**; venue **EDVA Alexandria / Fairfax Cty / NY, NY**; arbitration only in Puerto Rico.
14. No post-term noncompete. In-term noncompete (no competing hotel brand ownership) applies.
15. Restaurant Brands (Exhibit J-2: Poppy's, Bronco Blanco, Haley & Bear, Comeback, Che Vita, Magnolia Smokehouse, Spencer's) not offered under this FDD.

## Pass D — Items 12–16 (territory, channels, operations)

**Item 12.** **Non-exclusive license** at specified location. **No protected territory** in standard FA. Optional "Restricted Area Provision" may be negotiated for New Development/Conversion (typically not COO/Re-licensing), for a Restricted Area of variable shape and a Restrictive Period typically shorter than FA term. Exclusions: existing Brand hotels, hotels under other brands, gaming hotels, vacation ownership, chain acquisitions (4+ hotels), replacement hotels. Excludes other Hilton brands (HGI, DT, Homewood, etc.) — so Embassy Suites protection is brand-only, not Hilton-family-wide. Hilton/affiliates may own, operate, franchise or acquire ANY other business anywhere in Restricted Area. "Historically extended Restrictive Period for full FA term; however, we do not intend to do so in the future" — **material change signal**. Franchisees may compete with Network Hotels; no conflict-resolution mechanism.

**Item 13.** Trademarks — Embassy Suites by Hilton marks, federally registered (standard hotel brand trademark block).

**Item 14.** Proprietary info — OnQ, HPMS/PEP (developed with HotelKey), Manual, confidential training materials.

**Item 15.** Operator/manager must be approved; franchisee obligations to personally participate limited (hotel FDDs typically allow third-party manager).

No timeshare/fractional/condo sales without permission. No shared facilities without Shared Facilities Addendum. No tenant-in-common syndication without permission. Must sell only brand-approved services.

## Pass E — Item 17 (contract mechanics)

- **Term**: NC 23 years; Conversion 10–20 years; COO = remaining FA term or as agreed.
- **Renewal**: None. Re-licensing at Hilton's discretion with "materially different" terms.
- **Termination by franchisee**: Not allowed pre-expiration; breach → Liquidated Damages.
- **Termination by franchisor w/o cause**: Condemnation/casualty (with 18-month rebuild window).
- **Cause (curable)**: Payment default (10-day cure), failure to start/complete/open (10 days), insurance (10 days), other breaches (30 days). Interim remedies include reservation suspension, website delist, divert bookings, disable software, revoke fee discounts.
- **Non-curable defaults (FA §13.2, 16 grounds)**: Repeat breach within 24 months; 3 notices in 12 months; insolvency/bankruptcy; loss of possession of hotel; 5 consecutive closed days; contesting marks; reputational conduct; fraudulent reporting; unapproved transfer; sanctions; adverse gaming regulation; guarantor breach; public health/safety threat; becoming a Competitor; termination of other hotel agreements.
- **Liquidated Damages**: Higher of Hotel's Average Monthly Royalty × remaining months OR System Average Monthly Royalty × approved rooms × remaining months.
- **Transfer**: Permitted Transfers (no-consent): publicly traded, <50% privately held w/ no control change, intra-institutional-fund. Permitted (with 60-day notice + consent + fee): affiliates, family/trust, death, >50% private equity transfers. Change-of-Ownership Transfer: 60-day notice, transferee must meet new-franchisee standards, pay Franchise App Fee, sign then-current FA, possibly PIP. No transferee may be Sanctioned Person or Competitor.
- **Death/disability**: On death, transfer to immediate family/trust without consent IF family meets standards within 1 year.
- **Noncompete during term**: Franchisee and Affiliates cannot own/license competing hotel brand; minority interest in Competitor OK in some circumstances; may be franchisee or manager of Competitor.
- **Post-term noncompete**: None.
- **Dispute resolution**: No arbitration except Puerto Rico (AAA/JAMS, English, Federal Rules). Courts: EDVA Alexandria, Fairfax Cty VA, or New York NY; franchisor may sue where hotel located.
- **Choice of law**: New York.
- **ROFR / Option to purchase**: None.
