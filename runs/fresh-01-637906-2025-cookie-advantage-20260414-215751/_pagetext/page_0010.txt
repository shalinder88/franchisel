ITEM 5 
INITIAL FEES 
You must pay us an initial franchise fee (“Initial Franchise Fee”) of $34,900 in full when you sign 
the Franchise Agreement.  The Initial Franchise Fee is payment for all of our pre-opening assistance that 
we provide to allow you to open your Cookie Advantage Business and also offsets some of our franchisee 
recruitment expenses.  The Initial Franchise Fee is uniform, deemed fully earned by us once paid and 
non-refundable under any circumstances.  During our last fiscal year, ended December 31, 2024, we did 
not collect Initial Franchise Fees.    
You must purchase an initial inventory of product packaging (“Packaging”) for $2,500 prior to 
opening your Cookie Advantage Business from our Affiliate.  The Packaging costs include shipping and 
are uniform and non-refundable under any circumstances. 
Some states have imposed a fee deferral.  Please refer to the State Addendum in Exhibit G to the 
Franchise Disclosure Document.
