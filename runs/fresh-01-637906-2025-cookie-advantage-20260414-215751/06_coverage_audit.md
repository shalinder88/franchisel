## Unresolveds

1. **Missouri Table 3 date anomaly**: Middle year shows "2021" instead of "2023". Likely typographic error in FDD. Data is consistent. Severity: low.
2. **Administrative fee structure**: $488,555 paid to KDKC as "Administrative fee" (89% of revenue). The Notes describe this as a management fee covering office, production facilities, and day-to-day operations. The fee amounts for 2024/2023/2022 were $488,555/$459,151/$420,000 respectively — but the exact formula or basis is not disclosed. Severity: medium.
3. **Virginia equity risk**: Investment range ($92,550-$171,250) exceeds stockholders' equity ($78,724). Virginia addendum explicitly flags this. Severity: medium.
4. **Minimum royalty impact**: $800/month minimum creates effective royalty rate of 19.1% on lowest-grossing outlet ($50,198). FDD does not analyze this effect. Severity: medium.

## Contradictions
1. **Cash beginning 2023**: Cash flow statement should reconcile with prior year end. Cross-checking: 2022 end = $45,968, 2023 beginning = $45,968. Reconciled. No contradiction.
2. **Table 3 Missouri year label**: As noted above, cosmetic issue only. Data reconciles.
