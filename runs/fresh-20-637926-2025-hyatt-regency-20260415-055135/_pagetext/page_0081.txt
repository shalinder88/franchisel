Item 19 
FINANCIAL PERFORMANCE REPRESENTATIONS 
The FTC’s Franchise Rule permits a franchisor to provide information about the actual or 
potential financial performance of its franchised and/or franchisor-owned outlets, if there is a 
reasonable basis for the information, and if the information is included in the disclosure document.  
Financial performance information that differs from that included in Item 19 may be given only 
if: (1) a franchisor provides the actual records of an existing outlet you are considering buying; or 
(2) a franchisor supplements the information provided in this Item 19, for example, by providing 
information about possible performance at a particular location under particular circumstances. 
Characteristics of Covered Hotels  
As of December 31, 2024, there were 103 Brand Hotels operating in North America, which 
we define as the U.S. and Canada.  103 of these Brand Hotels (the “Covered Hotels”) operated 
during all of 2024.  There were no Brand Hotels opened during 2024.  No Brand Hotels were 
excluded from the group of Covered Hotels because they closed during 2024.  All of the financial 
performance representations (“FPRs”) in this Item 19 relate to the results during the period from 
January 1, 2024 through December 31, 2024 for the 103 Covered Hotels or a defined subset of the 
Covered Hotels.  When we use the term “Gross Rooms Revenue” in this Item 19, it has the 
definition listed in Item 6. 
The Covered Hotels are similar in size and services offered to the Brand Hotels that we 
expect franchisees will develop.  The Covered Hotels operate in a mix of urban and suburban sites.  
The Covered Hotels had operated under the “Hyatt Regency” name for an average of 30.2 years 
as of December 31, 2024.  Some of the Covered Hotels converted from another brand and system 
to the “Hyatt Regency” name and therefore had an existing marketing and customer base before 
converting to the “Hyatt Regency” name.  The average number of rooms at the Covered Hotels is 
541, with 2,032 as the highest room count and 182 as the lowest room count.  The Covered Hotels 
operate throughout the United States and Canada and are generally not concentrated in specific 
regions or markets.  During 2024, approximately 78.9% of the reservations at the Covered Hotels 
were for 1 to 4 nights, approximately 18.7% were for 5 to 11 nights, approximately 1.9% were for 
12 to 29 nights, and approximately 0.6% were for 30 or more nights.
