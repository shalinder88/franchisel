Exhibit A 
Definition of 
“Average 
Monthly 
Revenues” 
The definition of “Average Monthly Revenues” is deleted and 
replaced with the following: 
“Average Monthly Revenues” means: 
(a) if the date of termination occurs after the Ramp Period, the 
average monthly Gross Rooms Revenue and Gross F&B 
Revenue of the Hotel during the twelve (12) full calendar 
months preceding the month of termination; or 
(b) if the date of termination occurs after the Opening Date but 
before the end of the Ramp Period, the greater of (i) the average 
monthly Gross Rooms Revenue and Gross F&B Revenue of the 
Hotel from the Opening Date until the effective date of 
termination or (ii) the amount determined under part (c) below; 
or 
(c) if the date of termination occurs before the Opening Date, 
the average monthly Gross Rooms Revenue and Gross F&B 
Revenue per available guest room for all Brand Hotels in the 
United States (including those that Hyatt and its Affiliates own, 
manage, and franchise) during the twelve (12) full calendar 
months preceding the month of termination, multiplied by the 
number of guest rooms approved for the Hotel.

Exhibit A 
Definition of 
“Gross F&B 
Revenue” 
The following definition is added to Exhibit A: 
“Gross F&B Revenue” means “Total F&B Revenue,” or the 
equivalent thereof, as determined in accordance with the 
Uniform System of Accounts.  If Franchisee receives any 
proceeds from any business interruption insurance applicable to 
the F&B Operations, then there shall be added to Gross F&B 
Revenue an amount equal to the imputed gross revenues that the 
insurer used to calculate those proceeds.
