Owner/Operator Orientation 
Your managing owner and senior operations officer must attend and complete to our 
satisfaction the owner/operator orientation program (“Orientation”) within 6 months after signing 
the Franchise Agreement for existing Brand Hotels or within 6 months after the Hotel’s opening 
date for new Brand Hotels.  We conduct Orientation virtually, or in person at a Hyatt hotel location 
we designate or at our company headquarters in Chicago, Illinois approximately once per quarter.  
As of the date of this disclosure document, this 2-day briefing takes place in a classroom or virtual 
setting and covers the following topics:
