## Unresolveds
1. **Franchised vs Owned/Managed RevPAR gap**: Franchised RevPAR ($114.58) is 25.7% below owned/managed ($154.18). The FDD provides no explanation for this disparity. Severity: medium.
2. **AOP Term variability**: Exact AOP Terms are negotiated per deal and not disclosed. A 1-year AOP vs 5-year AOP is materially different protection. Severity: medium.
3. **Goodwill impairment signal**: $110M impairment in the management and franchising reporting unit in 2024 may indicate declining unit economics or brand value changes. Severity: medium.
4. **Company-owned outlet decline**: Net -4 company-owned hotels in 2024 (2 closed, 2 sold to franchisee) may reflect asset-light strategy shift or performance issues. Severity: low.
