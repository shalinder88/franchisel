 
Taco Cabana FDD – 2025  
 
Special Risks to Consider About This Franchise 
Certain states require that the following risk(s) be highlighted: 
1. Out-of-State Dispute Resolution. The franchise agreement requires you 
to resolve disputes with the franchisor by litigation only in Texas. Out-of-
state litigation may force you to accept a less favorable settlement for 
disputes. It may also cost more to litigate with the franchisor in Texas than 
in your own state. 
 
 
Certain states may require other risks to be highlighted. Check the “State Specific 
Addenda” (if any) to see whether your state requires other risks to be highlighted.
