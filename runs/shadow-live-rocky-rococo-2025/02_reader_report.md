# Reader Report — Rocky Rococo Pizza and Pasta (Filing 638785, 2025)

## A. Executive snapshot
- Regional Wisconsin/Minnesota pan-pizza & pasta franchisor; parent entity is Rococo Franchise Corporation (RFC), a Wisconsin corp founded June 13, 1995; affiliated with Rocky Rococo Corporation, the mark owner (brand dates to 1974/1982).
- Very small system: **31 total outlets** at 12/30/2024 (24 franchised, 7 affiliate company-owned, 0 predecessor-franchised). Net growth +3 franchised stores 2023→2024; predecessor-franchised count wound down from 7 (2022) to 0 (2024) as those locations transferred to franchisees.
- Only two states of operation: Wisconsin (23 franchised) and Minnesota (1 franchised).
- Initial investment range **$211,000 – $705,500** (including $25,000 initial franchise fee; $17,500 for a second store).
- Royalty **5% of gross sales**, with retroactive step-down to 3.5% if a store fails to clear $425,000 annual gross sales (or $433,173 in 53-week fiscal years).
- Marketing Fund **up to 0.75%** (currently collected); Local Ad Fund up to 3.5% (NOT currently collected).
- **No Item 19 FPR** — RFC expressly declines to give financial performance representations.
- Clean audit opinion from Vrakas, S.C. (Brookfield, WI), dated March 6, 2025; no going-concern language.
- Healthy franchisor P&L: 2024 revenues $1.38M, net income $70,626; retained earnings $507,177; cash $669,550; total equity $446,700.
- Long-serving management: Thomas R. Hester (Co-CEO since 1995), Sharon R. Hester (Co-CEO), Thomas R. Hester III (President); Earl Rambo (Ops/Training since 2006/2013); Kurt Kimball (Controller since 2000).
- Territory is a **1-mile radius** protected zone, with carveouts for major regional shopping malls and central business districts — materially narrow.
- 15-year term, 15-year renewal, no renewal fee; renewal requires "then-current" Franchise Agreement which may have materially different terms.
- Post-term noncompete: 1 year / 5-mile radius (FA) — note the Item 17 table also lists a 50-mile variant from the FDA provision subject to state law.
- Dispute resolution: binding arbitration (AAA) in RFC's nearest office; Wisconsin governing law; WI Franchise Investment Law and WI Fair Dealership Act are **NOT arbitrable**.
- No litigation, no bankruptcy required to be disclosed.

- Marketing Fund: up to 0.75% of gross sales weekly.
- Local Ad Fund / Local Advertising: up to 3.5% of gross sales (tiered: 1.5%/2.5%/3.5% by media type). Not currently collected; can be reinstated on 30 days' notice.
- Transfer Fee: 50% of then-current initial franchise fee.
- Additional training / on-site assistance: franchisee pays transportation, meals, lodging, and a reasonable charge for RFC rep.
- Amounts advanced by RFC: immediately repayable.
- Interest: max allowed by law or 1.5% per month.
- Indemnification: uncapped (see Note 7, eleven subparts).
- Audit: actual cost plus interest; franchisee pays if deficiency > 2% of reported gross sales.

## C. Supplier, operations, training, tech (Items 8–11)
**Supplier restrictions (Item 8):** Only mandated designation is a **single national soft-drink supplier** (RFC and affiliates take no revenue from this). Otherwise 100% of goods can be sourced from franchisee's choice of supplier. Products must conform to RFC recipes/specifications and Operations Manual standards. No purchasing cooperatives exist.

**Training (Item 11):** 10-week pre-opening formal training in Mayfair Mall, Wauwatosa, WI (5 days/week, 8–10 hrs/day), at RFC's cost. Optional on-the-job training up to 20 weeks at affiliate restaurants during construction. Opening assistance: 5 business days (first 2 days of operation supported by RFC operational staff) — RFC pays wages, franchisee pays lodging/meals after 5 days.

Training-program matrix (on-the-job hours): VCM/Dough 12; Pizza Prep 24; Quality Control/Oven 36; Bussing/DMO 4; Customer Service 36; Delivery 12; Administration 36. Classroom hours 0 across the board.

**Technology:** POS / bookkeeping / inventory / training / marketing computer system required; approximately **$10,000** initial cost. Ongoing updates required; RFC does not provide maintenance. Franchisee must maintain a dedicated line for RFC access; all retrieved data is RFC property.

**Marketing Fund spending (FY 2024):** 66.83% marketing/advertising, 3.63% administration, 29.53% public relations. Fund is **unaudited** (explicitly stated). No advertising council currently; RFC reserves right to create one. RFC is not obligated to conduct any advertising and does not. RFC may borrow from the Fund or reimburse franchisees.

## D. Territory, channels, encroachment (Items 12, 16)
Territory = **1-mile radius around the franchised location**. Carveouts: (a) Rocky Rococo restaurants in major regional shopping malls (500,000+ sq ft, ≥2 anchor tenants) are excluded from the protection — RFC can place another mall store within a franchisee's 1-mile zone; (b) central business district locations get no 1-mile protection. RFC retains right to operate or grant other Rocky Rococos outside protected zone and to sell through other channels/marks. **You will not receive an exclusive territory.** Franchisee may not solicit outside territory restrictions are not imposed; other franchisees are free to solicit into franchisee's territory. No right of first refusal on adjacent territories.

## E. Contract burden, legal mechanics (Item 17)
- **Term:** 15 years. **Renewal:** additional 15 years, no renewal fee, but franchisee must sign then-current FA which may contain "materially different" terms, execute general release, remodel, and re-train.
- **Franchisee termination:** none, except FDA right if no site within 180 days.
- **Franchisor termination without cause:** none (except FDA 180-day clause).
- **Termination with cause:** 60-day cure for most defaults (reporting, insurance, lease issues, tax delinquencies, "any other default"); 10-day cure for non-payment; non-curable defaults include bankruptcy, abandonment, criminal misconduct, unauthorized assignment, unauthorized disclosure, intentional understatement of gross sales, repeat non-compliance.
- **Transfer:** RFC approval required (not to be unreasonably withheld); transferee must qualify, accounts current, 50% transfer fee, training, release signed, noncompete signed, remodel, new FA signed.
- **ROFR:** Yes — RFC may match any offer.
- **Death/disability:** Assign to approved buyer/heir within 6 months.
- **Noncompete during term:** No involvement in any competing business anywhere (subject to state law).
- **Post-term noncompete:** 1 year / 5-mile radius of the designated area and of any other System location (subject to state law). FDA variant listed as 50-mile radius — contradiction between FA and FDA noted.
- **Modification:** No agreement modifications, but the Operations Manual may change unilaterally.
- **Dispute resolution:** AAA binding arbitration in RFC's nearest office. WI Franchise Investment Law and WI Fair Dealership Act explicitly NOT subject to arbitration.
- **Governing law:** Wisconsin.
- No choice of forum restriction.

Litigation (Item 3): none required to disclose. Bankruptcy (Item 4): none required to disclose.
