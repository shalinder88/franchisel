## Key Observations
- **Item 19**: No financial performance representation made. This is a significant omission for prospective franchisees.
- **Securitization**: Taco Bell Franchisor, LLC is a post-securitization SPV; TBC is the operational manager.
- **License (not franchise)**: This FDD uses "license" terminology throughout, not "franchise."
- **Express Unit focus**: This FDD covers Express Units only (smaller format), not Traditional/In-Line/End-Cap units.
- **No territorial protection**: License Agreement provides no exclusivity or territorial protection.
- **System shrinking**: Licensed Express Units declined from 251 (2022) to 224 (2024), net loss of 27 units.
