such right at any time after the termination of the Franchise Agreement by written notice to

may be. The assumption of Tenant's obligations under the Lease by Franchisor or its designated 
affiliate thereof shall in no way relieve Tenant from any obligations, expenses, charges or 
liabilities of Tenant to Franchisor under the terms of the Franchise Agreement or from any 
obligations, expenses, charges or liabilities of Tenant to Landlord under the Lease.
