The federal Clean Air Act and various implementing state laws require certain state and local areas 
to meet national air quality standards that limit emissions of ozone, carbon monoxide and particulate 
matters, including emissions from commercial food preparation.  Some areas have also adopted or are 
considering proposals that would regulate indoor air quality. 
 
We recommend that you check with your state and local agencies to determine which laws apply 
to the operation of a Unit in your area. You should consider these laws and regulations when evaluating 
your purchase of a license.
