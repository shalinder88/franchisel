WHEREAS, each Guarantor owns or holds some interest (directly or indirectly) or

obligations in favor of Franchisor and effect certain modifications to the Franchise Agreements as 
more fully set forth therein; and 
 
WHEREAS, Guarantors agree to execute this Guaranty, containing the terms, conditions, 
rights, and obligations stated below, in favor of Franchisor specifically to induce Franchisor to 
grant the Franchise Agreements to Franchisee and enter into the Relationship Agreement;
