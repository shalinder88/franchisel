Section 11.06

SUMMARY
Subject to state law, Missouri law applies generally, except
for applicable franchise laws of other states.

Some states may have statutes and other states may have court decisions that may supersede the Franchise Agreement and Area
Development Agreement in your relationship with us, including the areas of termination and renewal of your franchise.
Provisions in the Franchise Agreement and the Area Development Agreement that provide for termination upon your bankruptcy
may not be enforceable under federal bankruptcy law (11 U.S.C. Section 101 et seq.).
