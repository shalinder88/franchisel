## FDD 2026 | Filing ID: 640323

## Pass A: Executive Snapshot

1. **Franchise type**: Master franchise / subfranchisor model — buyer acquires the right to sell and service Anago unit franchises within a defined territory; this is NOT a unit-level cleaning franchise
2. **Entity structure**: Anago Franchising, Inc. (AFI) is a wholly-owned subsidiary of Anago Cleaning Systems, Inc. (ACS), a Florida C-corp. ACS owns the trademarks and guarantees AFI's performance
3. **Founded**: AFI incorporated February 15, 1995; predecessor Anago International, Inc. (AII) began franchising in 1991
4. **System size (domestic)**: 44 franchised subfranchisors + 1 company-owned as of December 31, 2025; 3 international (all Canada)
5. **Investment range**: $219,000 to $339,000 total initial investment; $98,000 initial fee (fully earned, non-refundable); VetFran 15% discount available
6. **Fee burden**: 5% royalty on Gross Revenues (escalating monthly minimums starting month 13 at $1,500/mo, increasing $1,500/year); 2% administrative support fee; $550/mo NBDS software; up to 2.2% advertising fund (not currently assessed); $50,000/year mandatory client marketing spend
7. **Item 19**: FPR disclosed — 2025 average annual sales $3,453,102 across 37 qualifying subfranchisors (90% of total); median $3,531,399; range $282,393 to $8,243,733
8. **Financial health**: ACS consolidated — total revenue $9.39M (2025), operating income $868,589, comprehensive income $1.02M, total assets $8.89M, stockholders' equity $6.65M, EBITDA ~$1.02M. Clean audit opinion (Miller CPA, PLLC). No debt.
9. **Growth trajectory**: Net flat — 45 outlets start of 2025, 45 at end (44 franchised + 1 company). Slight shrinkage in franchised count. New Market Developer model launched December 2024 (2 MDs under separate FDD)
10. **Territory**: Defined area with minimum 500,000 population; not exclusive but franchisor won't grant another subfranchisor in same area; significant national accounts reservation
11. **Contract term**: 10 years initial + one 10-year renewal; renewal requires signing then-current agreement (may contain materially different terms); minimum royalty floor at renewal = average of last year's royalty
12. **Disputes**: Binding arbitration in Pompano Beach, FL per AAA Commercial Rules; FL governing law (subject to state overrides)
13. **Litigation**: None pending, none required to be disclosed as prior
14. **Special risks (state-flagged)**: Out-of-state dispute resolution (FL), mandatory minimum payments, sales performance requirements
15. **Gag clause**: YES — "certain Subfranchisors have signed confidentiality provisions restricting their ability to speak openly about their experience"

## Pass D: Territory, Competition, Channels, Encroachment (Items 12-16)

### Item 12 — Territory
- **Area**: Defined counties per Subfranchise Rights Agreement, minimum 500,000 population, per Subfranchise Program purchased
- **Exclusivity**: NOT exclusive BUT franchisor will not grant another subfranchisor in same Area during term (if not in default)
- **Territory reduction**: If franchisee fails to meet minimum Client Bid schedule, franchisor may reduce or redefine Area
- **No expansion rights**: No options, rights of first refusal, or additional territory rights
- **No relocation** permitted
- **Franchisor's reserved rights**: Broad — operate other businesses under different marks; sell similar services via Internet/catalogs/direct mail in or outside Area; grant retail businesses using Anago marks outside Area; bid on and enter National Account contracts
- **National Accounts**: Must refer to franchisor; cannot enter contracts without written consent; defined as client with 10+ locations in Area, or multi-location crossing Area boundaries, or RFP responses
- **Solicitation restriction**: Cannot solicit Unit Franchisees or clients outside Area (including via Internet, catalogs, telemarketing)
- **Market Developer overlap**: Franchisor does not anticipate overlap with Market Developer territories; no specific conflict resolution method established

### Item 13 — Trademarks
- **Owner**: ACS (parent company) owns all Proprietary Marks
- **License**: ACS licensed AFI via June 1, 2012 agreement (10-year initial term, auto-renewing 1-year periods)
- **Key marks**: ANAGO (Classes 35 & 37), Anago Cleaning Systems (35 & 37), CleanCom (37), Anago Protection+ Disinfection (37), Anago CleanSource (42), ELEVATE Your Cleaning Experience (35 & 37)

## Pass E: Contract Burden and Legal Mechanics (Item 17)

### Term and Renewal
- **Initial term**: 10 years from President's countersignature
- **Renewal**: One additional 10-year term
- **Renewal conditions**:
  1. Written notice 9-12 months before expiration
  2. Not in default of any agreement with franchisor/affiliates; all debts satisfied
  3. Sign then-current Successor Subfranchise Rights Agreement 30 days before expiration (MAY CONTAIN MATERIALLY DIFFERENT TERMS) + general release of all claims
  4. Meet then-current qualification and training requirements
  5. Successor agreement minimum monthly royalty ≥ average monthly royalty of last year of current agreement
- **MATERIALLY DIFFERENT TERMS RISK**: Explicit — successor agreement "may contain materially different terms or conditions"

### Termination
- **By franchisee**: Not applicable (no contractual right)
- **By franchisor without cause**: Not applicable
- **By franchisor with cause**:
  - **Curable defaults** (Section 8.3): 30 days to cure after written notice
  - **Non-curable defaults** (Sections 8.1-8.2): 25 enumerated grounds including insolvency, bankruptcy, felony, violation of laws, denial of audit rights, material breach of Unit Franchisee obligations, dishonesty, unauthorized transfer, breach of confidentiality/non-compete, false books, repeated Unit Franchisee complaints, failure to achieve minimum annual performance, misuse of Proprietary Property, 3+ notices for same default in 12 months

### Transfer
- Requires prior written approval
- Transfer defined broadly (direct/indirect, voluntary/involuntary)
- Conditions: not in default, general release, transferee assumes or signs current agreement, $10,000 transfer fee, franchisor must approve transferee
- **ROFR**: 30 days to match bona fide offer; 60 days to close
- **Death/disability**: Transfer within 6 months; replacement manager required

### Non-Competition
- **During term**: No involvement with any Competitive Business; no interference with franchisor or other franchisees
- **Post-term**: 24 months after expiration/termination — no Competitive Business within Area, any other subfranchisor's area, or 20 miles of perimeter; no interference

### Dispute Resolution
- **Pre-arbitration**: 60-day good-faith negotiation period
- **Arbitration**: Binding, per AAA Commercial Rules, 1 arbitrator, within 50 miles of franchisor's principal place of business (Pompano Beach, FL); governed by Federal Arbitration Act
- **Exception**: Franchisor may seek injunctive relief in court for IP-related defaults
- **Choice of forum**: State or federal court closest to Pompano Beach, FL
- **Choice of law**: Florida (subject to state overrides)
- **State overrides available**: CA, IL, MD, MI, MN, NY, VA, WA (Exhibit H)
