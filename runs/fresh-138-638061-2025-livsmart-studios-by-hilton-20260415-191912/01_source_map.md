## Key Observations
- This is a brand-new brand with ZERO open hotels as of December 31, 2024
- 73 franchise agreements signed but not yet opened
- Formerly known as "Project H3 by Hilton" (launched May 2023, rebranded to LivSmart Studios)
- No financial performance representations (Item 19)
- Trademark "LivSmart Studios by Hilton" is NOT yet federally registered (applications pending)
- Parent chain: Hilton Worldwide Holdings Inc. (NYSE: HLT)
- Extended-stay midscale segment
- No renewal right — franchise agreement is non-renewable (re-licensing at franchisor's discretion)
