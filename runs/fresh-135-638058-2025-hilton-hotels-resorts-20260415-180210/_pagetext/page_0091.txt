As of December 31, 2024, there were a total of 242 Hilton or Hilton Suites branded hotels operating in 
the US. Of these 242 hotels, 199 were classified as Comparable Hotels. Of the 199 Comparable Hotels, 
46 were Company-Managed and 153 were Franchisee-Managed. The financial performance results 
detailed in this section for 2023 and 2024 are for the 2024 Comparable Hotels, defined above to provide 
a year-over-year comparison.
