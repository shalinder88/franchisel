Except for certain claims for injunctive relief, all disputes must

18.G. 
All mediation, arbitration and, if applicable, litigation 
proceedings must be conducted in, or closest to, state court of 
general jurisdiction that is within or closest to Alameda 
County, California or, if appropriate, the United States District 
Court nearest to our corporate headquarters at the time such 
action is filed. This provision is subject to applicable state law.
