EXHIBIT A 
TO DIGITAL ANCILLARY SERVICES PROGRAM SCHEDULE  
TO CENTRAL HOTEL SERVICES BY HYATT MASTER AGREEMENT 
We are pleased to inform you that we have opted in to secure additional Digital Ancillary Services through 
the Central Hotel Services by Hyatt Program for our hotel.  Attached is a fully executed Central Hotel 
Services by Hyatt Master Agreement and Digital Ancillary Services Program Schedule for these additional 
Digital Ancillary Services from Hyatt.  
Under this agreement, Hyatt or its designee, Way, will provide additional Digital Ancillary Services 
support to our hotel, including negotiated pricing for annual Way software license, exclusive ancillary 
merchandising placements, and custom reporting capabilities to drive ancillary revenue opportunities at 
our hotel. These enhanced services will be in addition to any base ancillary services provided by Hyatt 
pursuant to your existing agreements.   
Should you wish to rescind the agreement, you have thirty days from the date of this letter to contact 
digitalancillary@hyatt.com and request such action. Otherwise, we look forward to receiving these 
services and will work to maximize the benefits we receive.  
Please let us know of any questions.
