1.6 
Collection 
Brand 
The following is added as a new Section 1.6: 
Collection Brand.  Notwithstanding anything in this 
Agreement to the contrary, Hyatt will exercise the rights 
granted in this Agreement, including the right to modify and 
the manner of enforcement of the Hotel System and System 
Standards, subject to the understanding that the Brand is, and 
shall remain during the Term, a “collection” brand, such that 
(a) each Brand Hotel is intended to be unique in its design, 
concept, appearance and some aspects of its operations; and 
(b) Franchisee has the primary responsibility for developing a 
distinct brand identity for the Hotel with minimal and limited 
involvement by Hyatt, subject to the Hotel remaining 
consistent with Hyatt’s standards for the overall positioning of 
Brand Hotels.
