## 10. Notable Observations

1. **No standalone training program table found in Item 11.** Training is described narratively (pages 66-67) but no formal TRAINING PROGRAM table with Subject/Hours/Classroom columns was detected. Needs deep read to confirm.

2. **Item 19 is unusually rich.** Two full sections (A: Sales Distributions across 6 restaurant types; B: Remodel Uplift with 6 sub-tables + multi-year data). This is significantly more FPR content than typical FDDs.

3. **Carrols LLC appears extensively in Exhibit O3** (ceased operations). Carrols was an affiliate that operated BK restaurants and is undergoing a refranchising program.

4. **Two complete financial statement sets** (RBI Inc. + RBILP) — no separate BKC-level financials observed. This is standard for RBI-owned brands.

5. **Exhibit P covers 7 states** for agreement amendments (CA, HI, IL, MD, MN, ND, WA) and **11 states** for FDD addenda (adds NY, RI, SD, VA).

6. **Z-series exhibits (Crown Your Career Program)** — boundary between Z1/Z2/Z3 is approximate and needs page-level verification.

7. **Item 10 references Crown Your Career financing** (promissory note + security agreement in Exhibits Z3-Z4), indicating BKC offers direct financing under this program.

8. **Document numbering note:** The FDD uses internal page numbering (Page 1 = PDF page 10). All page references in this source map use PDF page numbers unless marked "FDD page."
