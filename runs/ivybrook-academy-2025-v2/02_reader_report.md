## Pass D — Territory, channels, encroachment (Items 12–16)

**Item 12 — Territory.** Lesser of 125,000 population or 3-mile radius. Exclusive only vs. another Ivybrook Academy unit — franchisor retains rights to: use Marks outside the Territory; sell branded merchandise anywhere (online excluded from courses/curriculum); operate non-Ivybrook businesses anywhere including inside the Territory; solicit customers into the Territory via advertising. **Franchisee may not sell via mail order, phone, mobile app, internet, or any off-site channel except approved off-site events.** Territory may be modified at renewal.

**Item 13 — Trademarks.** IVYBROOK (word mark, Reg. 4283406, Jan 29 2013) and Ivybrook Academy design mark (Reg. 4807315, Sep 8 2015). No current litigation disclosed. Franchisor may substitute marks at franchisee expense.

**Item 14 — Patents / Copyrights / Trade Secrets.** 7 curriculum copyrights (all registered Nov 29, 2018, TXu series). Innovations clause: franchisee automatically assigns all rights in inventions / curriculum / software / processes to franchisor. Customer List is franchisor property.

**Item 15 — Participation.** At least one owner must directly participate. Designated CEO (≥10% ownership) must be on-premises ≥20 hrs/week first year. School Administrator (separate or same person) must be on-premises ≥40 hrs/week. Both must sign noncompete/non-disclosure/non-solicit.

**Item 16 — Restrictions on What Franchisee May Sell.** Only franchisor-authorized products/services. Brick-and-mortar only — no telemarketing, internet, mobile app, mail-order, or truck channels. Unlimited franchisor right to require participation in promotions / tests / new product rollouts without credit or offsets.

## Pass E — Contract burden and legal mechanics (Item 17)

- **Term**: 15 years. **Renewal**: two 10-year extensions; renewal fee = greater of $10,000 or 10% of then-current initial franchise fee. Franchisor states successor agreement may have "materially different terms and conditions."
- **Termination by franchisee**: only for uncured material breach by franchisor after 90-day cure window.
- **Termination by franchisor (no-cause)**: none.
- **Termination by franchisor — curable**: failure to maintain insurance; failure to pay; failure to comply with Manual/spec; other.
- **Termination by franchisor — non-curable (illustrative)**: failure to open; failed training; material misrepresentation in application; felony conviction; unauthorized Manual/trade-secret use; abandonment ≥3 days; unauthorized transfer; bankruptcy; mark misuse; ≥2 reporting/fee failures in 12 months; ≥2 health/safety or other law violations; 2 separate under-report events >1%; refusal to sign noncompetes; loss of required licenses; failure to meet minimum customer-evaluation scores; default that cannot be cured.
- **Assignment by franchisor**: unlimited.
- **Transfer by franchisee**: requires written consent; franchisor has ROFR; $10,000 transfer fee + $10,000 transfer training fee; transferee signs then-current FA (which may differ materially); general release required; $25,000 Transfer Damages if unauthorized transfer.
- **Death/disability**: interest must be transferred within 180 days to an approved transferee.
- **Noncompete during term**: all owners + spouses/domestic partners prohibited from diverting business or owning/working for a competitive business (subject to state law).
- **Noncompete post-term**: **2 years**, within the greater of the Territory or **10 miles** of franchise location or any other Ivybrook Academy Franchised Business (subject to state law).
- **Dispute resolution**: mediation + arbitration in **Charlotte, NC** (franchisor's principal place of business); NC governing law. Integration/merger clause preserved.
- **Litigation history**: none required to be disclosed.
