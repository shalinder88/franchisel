Tables 2B and 2C discloses the average, median, and high and low EBITDA of the certain franchisee owned and operated schools for 
the period of January 1, 2024 to December 31, 2024.  
 
Table 2B (labeled “2 Years +”) includes the data from 24 franchisee-owned schools that have been open two or more years and at least 
since January 1, 2023. The number of franchisees open 2 Years + included in this disclosure who achieved or surpassed the (i) average 
Gross Revenues is 11, (ii) median Gross Revenues is 13, (iii) average EBITDA is 11, and (iv) median EBITDA is 12. 
 
Table 2C (labeled “3 Years +”) includes the data from 18 franchisee-owned schools that have been open three or more years and at least 
since January 1, 2022. The number of franchisees open 3 Years + included in this disclosure who achieved or surpassed the (i) average 
Gross Revenues is 8, (ii) median Gross Revenues is 9, (iii) average EBITDA is 7, and (iv) median EBITDA is 9.
