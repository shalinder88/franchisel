17.2 
Right of Franchisor to Acquire Business. When this Agreement expires, non-renews, or 
is terminated, Franchisor will have the right (but not the obligation) to purchase any or all of the 
assets related to the Franchised Business (including all business and customer records, real 
property, leasehold improvements, equipment, supplies, and inventory) at fair market value. To 
exercise this option, Franchisor must notify Franchisee no later than 60 days after this Agreement 
expires or is terminated and shall pay the purchase price within 60 days of the notice or the date 
the fair market value is determined, whichever is later. If the parties cannot agree on fair market 
value within 60 days after the exercise notice, the fair market value will be determined by an 
independent appraiser reasonably acceptable to both parties. The parties will equally share the cost
