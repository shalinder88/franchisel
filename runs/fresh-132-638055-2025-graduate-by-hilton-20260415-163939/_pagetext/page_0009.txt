THE FRANCHISOR, AND ANY PARENTS, PREDECESSORS AND AFFILIATES 
About the Franchisor, Its Parents and Its Predecessor 
To simplify the language in this Disclosure Document, “we” or “us” means Hilton Franchise 
Holding LLC, the Franchisor. "You" means the person(s) who signs the franchise agreement, the 
“Franchisee.” If you are a business entity, "you" means both the business entity and its owners. 
The “Brand” refers to the name or names under which we will license your Hotel. Capitalized 
words not defined in this Disclosure Document have the meaning set forth in the Franchise 
Agreement. Our agent for service of process in the states whose franchise laws require us to 
name an agent for service is shown on Exhibit I.   
We are a Delaware limited liability company, formed in September 2007. For purposes of this 
franchise offering, we do business under the name “Graduate.” Our principal business address is 
7930 Jones Branch Drive, Suite 1100, McLean, Virginia 22102 USA and our telephone number 
is 703-883-1000.   
We became the franchisor of hotels which operate under the Graduate Brand in the 50 states of 
the United States, its Territories and Possessions and the District of Columbia (“US”) on May 28, 
2024. 
 
Our parent company is Hilton Domestic Operating Company Inc., a Delaware corporation formed 
on July 12, 2016 (“Hilton”). Hilton’s parent company is Hilton Worldwide Holdings Inc., a 
Delaware corporation formed on March 18, 2010 (NYSE: HLT) (“Hilton Worldwide”). The 
principal business address of both companies is 7930 Jones Branch Drive, Suite 1100, McLean, 
Virginia 22102 USA.   
 
Hilton became our parent company on January 4, 2017, as the successor to our previous parent 
company, Park Hotels & Resorts, Inc. (“Park”). Together, Hilton and Park have conducted a 
guest lodging business since 1946.  Park was originally called Hilton Hotels Corporation ("HHC") 
from May 29, 1946 to December 19, 2009.  It changed its name to Hilton Worldwide, Inc. (“HWI”) 
on December 20, 2009, and to Park Hotels & Resorts Inc. on June 1, 2016. On January 4, 2017, 
Park became an independent company in a spin-off transaction. As a result of that spin-off, 
nearly all company-owned hotels were divested with Park.  For convenience, all references 
“Hilton” in this Disclosure Document include HHC, HWI, and Park during the relevant time frames 
for each, unless otherwise noted. 
 
On May 24, 2024, Hilton Worldwide acquired the Graduate® hotel brand, including all associated 
trademarks and intellectual property assets, from Graduate IP LLC.  After that acquisition, 36 
existing Project Alpha by Hilton hotel franchises (which had been offered and sold under a 
separate Project Alpha by Hilton franchise disclosure document) were converted to the Graduate 
Brand in accordance with their terms.  Afterward, we became the franchisor of hotels which 
operate under the Graduate Brand in the US on May 28, 2024, and the Project Alpha by Hilton 
brand was retired. 
 
We do not have any predecessor that offered franchises for the Graduate Brand in the US or 
Thailand.
