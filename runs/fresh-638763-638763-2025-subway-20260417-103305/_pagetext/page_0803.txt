1.2.4

In the event the Master Concession Agreement contains extension or renewal options,
Subconcessionaire agrees to provide written notice to Subconcessionor of its desire to
exercise its option at least one hundred eighty days (180) prior to the date on which
Subconcessionor must notify the Master Concessionor of its intention to exercise its
option to extend or renew the Master Concession Agreement. Subconcessionaire agrees
that Subconcessionor may elect not to extend or renew the Master Concession
Agreement if Subconcessionaire fails to give Subconcessionor notice as provided in this
Subsection. Furthermore, Subconcessionor may elect not to extend or renew the Master
Concession Agreement if Subconcessionaire shall be in default in the performance of
any of the terms of the Master Concession Agreement, this Subconcession Agreement or
the Franchise Agreement during the period one hundred eighty (180) days prior to the
date Subconcessionor must give notice to Master Concessionor.

1.2.5

If Master Concessionor fails to perform its duties under the Master Concession
Agreement, Subconcessionaire must give written notice thereof to Subconcessionor
describing Master Concessionor's default in detail. Upon receipt of the notice,
Subconcessionor shall then promptly notify Master Concessionor and demand
performance as required in the Master Concession Agreement. In the event
Subconcessionaire wishes to engage the services of an attorney to settle any disputes
arising out of the Master Concession Agreement, all fees and costs shall be borne by
Subconcessionaire, it being understood that Subconcessionor is under no obligation to
bring or defend any action brought by or against Subconcessionaire, Subconcessionor or
Master Concessionor.

1.2.6

Subconcessionaire shall not make any agreement with Master Concessionor which could
modify, cancel or terminate the Master Concession Agreement.
