Service Agreement
The Company is a party in a Master Services Agreement (“MSA”) with entities affiliated by common
control. Pursuant to the MSA, the Company has agreed to reimburse the affiliate for the affiliate’s
actual costs incurred in providing administrative support services. Expenses are allocated based on
a percent of revenue. For the years ended December 31, 2024, 2023, and 2022, the Company’s
total expense pursuant to the service agreement, amounted to $92,895, $293,884 and $159,176,
respectively, and is recorded within “Operating expenses, net” on the accompanying statements of
income. Effective June 20, 2024, the Company no longer pays this service fee to its affiliate
company.
Other Transactions with Affiliates
During the years ended December 31, 2024, 2023 and 2022, an entity related to the Company by
common control charged the Company approximately $7,305, $15,454 and $8,715, respectively,
for point-of-sale usage and maintenance expenses. The amounts are recorded within “Operating
expenses, net” on the accompanying statements of income.
During the year ended December 31, 2024 the Company received $43,493 of non-cash
contributions from an affiliate and made $16,392 of non-cash distributions to an affiliate.
Amounts due to/from affiliates related by common control are noninterest bearing and have no
specific date for repayment. Such amounts are expected to be satisfied within 12 months of the
balance sheet date in the normal course of operations and, accordingly, have been classified as
current liabilities/assets.

Guarantees
In connection with SPV Holdco’s securitized note offering the Company, together with its affiliates
Subway IP LLC and Subway US IP Holder LLC (“Guarantors”), have irrevocably and
unconditionally guaranteed the obligations of SPV Holdco under the Series 2024-1 and Series
2024-3 fixed rate senior secured notes, which had total outstanding principal of $3,350,000 and
$2,335,000, respectively, as of December 31, 2024. The guarantees are collateralized by a pledge
of the Company’s assets, which include all right, title, interest in substantially all the revenuegenerating assets of the Company, pursuant to a guarantee and collateral agreement made by the
Guarantors in favor of the Trustee for the benefit of the secured debtholders.
Concurrently with the 2024-1 securitized note issuances, Subway Funding LLC, issued 2024-1
Class A-1-LR variable rate term notes to a third-party lender, to be funded on a delayed draw basis
in the maximum principal of $93,000.
