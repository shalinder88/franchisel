## Omissions and Gaps

1. **Lease maturity schedule (financial notes):** The text references a lease maturity table but the actual dollar amounts may be embedded in an image/table not fully captured by text extraction. The key terms are captured (5-year lease, $5K/month, 2% annual increase, expires April 2027). **Severity: Low.**

2. **Deferred revenue amortization schedule (financial notes):** Referenced in notes but the tabular data may not be fully captured by text extraction. Key facts captured (franchise fees recognized over 5-year term). **Severity: Low.**

3. **Accounts receivable table (financial notes):** Referenced in notes but AR breakdown table may be image-based. Key facts captured ($32,144 net at 12/31/2024, allowance for credit losses under CECL). **Severity: Low.**

4. **Franchise Agreement clause-by-clause detail:** Read but not walked clause-by-clause; key provisions extracted via Item 17 summary. Detailed burden analysis deferred to A2 depth pass. **Severity: Expected — handled in A2.**

5. **Guarantee of Franchise Agreement:** Referenced but text not independently extracted. Form is FA Exhibit 3. **Severity: Low — standard guarantee.**

## Unresolveds

1. **Wisconsin addendum references wrong entity name:** PDF page 159 states "Realty ONE Group Affiliates, Inc." instead of "Iron Valley Real Estate, LLC." This is a template error that could create legal ambiguity for Wisconsin franchisees. **Severity: Medium.**

2. **Outlet count discrepancy between Item 20 and Note 7:** Item 20 reports 44 franchised at end of 2024; Note 7 says 46 operating locations. May reflect different counting methods or timing. **Severity: Low.**

3. **Financial condition flagged as special risk:** Cover page explicitly flags financial condition concern. Members' equity was negative through 2023; MD, VA, WA require fee deferral. This is a genuine business-risk flag, not an extraction gap. **Severity: High (business risk).**

4. **Marketing fund not audited:** Franchisor deposits marketing fees into operating account (not separate fund), retains 15% admin fee, and only provides unaudited statement on request. **Severity: Medium (business risk).**

5. **Renewal terms may be materially different:** Explicitly disclosed that renewal FA "may have materially different terms and conditions." **Severity: Medium (business risk).**

## Contradictions

1. **Outlet count mismatch:** Item 20 Table 3 totals: 44 franchised at end of 2024. Financial statement Note 7 (p.113): "46 operating locations franchised by IVREL." Possible causes: Note 7 may include signed-but-not-yet-opened, or may use different counting methodology. Item 20 Table 5 shows 0 signed-not-opened, which makes the discrepancy harder to explain. **Items involved: Item 20, Item 21 Note 7. Resolution: Unresolved.**
