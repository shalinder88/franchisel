for franchisee to renew or extend," and Item 17(m), entitled "Conditions for franchisor approval of 
transfer": 
 
However, to the extent required by applicable law, all rights you enjoy and any causes of 
action arising in your favor from the provisions of Article 33 of the General Business Law of 
the State of New York and the regulations issued thereunder shall remain in force; it being 
the intent of this proviso that the non-waiver provisions of General Business Law Sections 
687.4 and 687.5 be satisfied.

franchisee": 
 
You may terminate the agreement on any grounds available by law.

forum", and Item 17(w), titled "Choice of law": 
 
The foregoing choice of law should not be considered a waiver of any right conferred upon 
the franchisor or upon the franchisee by Article 33 of the General Business Law of the State 
of New York.
