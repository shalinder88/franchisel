# Reader Report — Trend Hotels and Suites by My Place (638045-2025)

## Pass A: Executive Snapshot (8–15 bullets)

1. **Franchisor**: My Place Hotels of America, LLC ("MPHOA"), a South Dakota LLC organized July 22, 2011. No parents, no predecessors. Principal address: 1910 8th Ave NE, Aberdeen, SD 57401.
2. **Brand**: Trend Hotels and Suites by My Place — an upper-midscale/upscale conversion hotel concept. Franchising offered since June 5, 2020. **Zero Trend outlets have opened as of the issuance date** (March 28, 2025).
3. **System size**: MPHOA's My Place Hotels® system has 73 operating franchises (as of 12/31/2024). Trend is a separate brand with zero operating units.
4. **Investment range**: $272,500–$2,947,000 (excludes land/lease cost). Paid to franchisor/affiliates: $61,000–$77,000.
5. **Fee burden**: Royalty 5% of GRR (after ramp-up), Marketing 2.5%, Revenue Intelligence 1%, Loyalty 1% = **9.5% ongoing fee load on Gross Room Revenue** plus technology subscription ($1,204/mo), website fee ($1,000/yr).
6. **Item 19**: No financial performance representations provided. No FPR at all.
7. **Item 20**: All five tables show zero across all years 2022–2024. No outlets, no transfers, no openings, no projected openings. This is a pre-revenue franchise brand.
8. **Financial health**: MPHOA total revenue $9,554,082 (2024), net income $998,429. Total assets $5,466,007, members' equity $805,182. Minimal debt ($13,858 vehicle note). However, Class A members' equity is negative ($-1,755,172); Class B members hold $2,560,354.
9. **Term**: 10 years with early termination option at year 5 (180 days' notice). Extension option for 5 additional years if in Good Standing.
10. **Noncompete**: 2-year post-term within Protected Area.
11. **Dispute resolution**: Mediation first, then arbitration in Aberdeen, South Dakota. Litigation venue: South Dakota.
12. **Special risks**: Out-of-state dispute resolution, personal guaranty required for entity franchisees (10%+ equity holders).
13. **State addenda**: 9 states (CA, HI, IL, MD, MN, NY, ND, RI, WA) plus Michigan Notice. Significant overrides to forum selection, noncompete, governing law, and general release in multiple states.
14. **Washington AOD**: MPHOA entered Assurance of Discontinuance with WA Attorney General regarding no-hire provision; approved by King County Superior Court 10/7/2019.
15. **Key risk**: This is a conversion-only brand with zero operating history. No Trend hotels have ever opened. Investment estimates are based on industry averages and My Place experience, not Trend-specific data.

## Pass E: Contract Burden, Legal Mechanics (Item 17)

### Term and Renewal
- **Initial term**: 10 years
- **Early termination**: Option at year 5 with 180 days' notice (no penalty if in Good Standing)
- **Extension**: 5-year option if in Good Standing, 180 days' notice, must comply with then-current System Standards, PA adjusted at franchisor's discretion
- **Renewal fee**: Then-current Application Fee + then-current Initial Franchise Fee
- **"Materially different" risk**: Item 17(c) states "When renewing you will not be asked to sign a contract with materially different terms and conditions than your original contract." This is a **positive** provision.
- **No franchisee-initiated termination** (except via early termination option and Washington addendum Section 8(C))

### Termination by Franchisor
- **Without cause**: Not permitted after hotel opens
- **With cause**: 30-day cure period for curable defaults
- **Non-curable defaults** (Section 8(A)): 3 notices of material default in 24 months, loss of possession, unapproved transfers, uncured underlying debt default, failure to pay debts generally, criminal conviction, public endangerment, abandonment, trademark misuse, repeated defaults (even if cured), failing to operate per agreement/standards, contesting ownership of System/Marks, general assignment for benefit of creditors, 5 consecutive days not operating, concealing revenues/false books, refusing reinspection

### Transfer
- Franchisor approval required (will not unreasonably withhold)
- Transfer fee: 50% of then-current Initial Franchise Fee
- No fee for transfer to entity with 51%+ ownership
- Conditions: applications completed, new franchisee qualifies, fee paid, debts paid, training arranged, release signed, refurbishment satisfied, new agreement signed
- Franchisor option to purchase: if cash sale, match the offer; if non-cash, buy at appraised value (30 days to decide)
- No right of first refusal

### Death/Disability
Franchise may be assigned to estate/legal guardian upon fulfillment of conditions satisfactory to franchisor.

### Noncompete
- **During term**: No competing business within Protected Area without written authorization
- **Post-term**: 2-year noncompete within Protected Area (including after assignment)

### Dispute Resolution
- **Mediation**: Mutual obligation to mediate first
- **Arbitration**: If mediation fails, franchisor can require arbitration in Aberdeen, SD
- **Litigation**: Non-arbitration litigation in South Dakota at franchisor's election
- **Governing law**: South Dakota (unless state law requires otherwise)

### Post-Termination Obligations
Complete de-identification of hotel, payment of all amounts due.
