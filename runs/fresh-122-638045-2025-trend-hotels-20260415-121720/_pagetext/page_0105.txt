Note 7 -  Contract Balances 
The Company generally receives payments from customers as performance obligations are satisfied. The 
Company records a receivable when there is an unconditional right to receive payment and only the passage of 
time is required before payment is due. Deferred revenue is recorded when payment is received, or the 
Company has an unconditional right to receive payment, in advance of the satisfaction of the performance 
obligations.
