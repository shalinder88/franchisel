Note 2 -  Line of Credit 
The Company had a revolving line of credit with U.S. Bank that provided for available borrowings of $500,000 
with interest at the one-month Term SOFR rate plus 3.0%. The line of credit matured on December 25, 2023, 
and the Company elected to not renew the line at its maturity. There were no advances on the line during 2022 
or 2023.
