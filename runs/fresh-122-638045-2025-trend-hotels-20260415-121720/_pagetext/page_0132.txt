(C)  
Transfers Not Subject to Transfer Fee 
 
No new Initial Franchise Fee or transfer fee will be assessed, and no new Franchise Agreement will be executed 
in connection with any change in an equity interest pursuant to this Section 17(C).  The transfer, creation or elimination 
of any equity interest, by operation of law or otherwise, will be a default, subject to Sections 8 and 9, unless specifically 
authorized pursuant to Sections 6(N), 17(A), 17(B), 17(E), or as provided below.
