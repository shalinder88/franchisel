## Risk Flags (Early)
1. **Out-of-state dispute resolution** — Illinois venue for mediation/arbitration/litigation (page 4)
2. **No renewal right** — Franchisee has no contractual right to renew or extend (Item 17)
3. **No exclusive territory** — No protected territory, franchisor may compete (Item 12)
4. **Gag clause present** — Current/former franchisees may have signed provisions restricting ability to speak openly (page 55, Item 20)
5. **Royalty increase** — 5% royalty for new restaurants post-Jan 2024, vs 4% for legacy (Item 6 note 2)
6. **Percentage rent range** — 6% to 23% of Gross Sales, with escalation to minimum 11.50% (Item 6 note 3)
7. **Multiple race discrimination lawsuits** — Crawford (77 former franchisees), Byrd, Washington cases (Item 3)
8. **E. coli class action** — Williams v. McDonald's (Item 3)
9. **No financing from franchisor** — McDonald's predecessor may guarantee Bank of America loans; guarantee fee 1.50% (Item 10)
10. **100% dividend payout** — All net income distributed to parent as dividends each year (Exhibit A)
