## Risk Flags (Early)

1. **Out-of-state dispute resolution**: Arbitration and litigation in Illinois (Chicago).
2. **Emerging brand**: Caption by Hyatt franchising began September 2019; only 1 franchised hotel open as of Dec 31, 2024; 1 company-owned.
3. **Very high investment range**: $28.4M–$88.0M total (excluding real estate).
4. **No FPR (Item 19)**: Franchisor makes no financial performance representations.
5. **Territory protection expires**: AOP Term typically only 3 years after opening; after that, nonexclusive.
6. **Renewal on materially different terms**: Successor franchise on then-current agreement, which may be materially different.
7. **Liquidated damages**: Up to 36 months × 8.5% of average monthly GRR; 150% for Consequential Termination.
8. **Guarantor Monetary Threshold**: Guarantors must maintain minimum net worth and liquidity, increasing annually by CPI.
9. **Michigan notice**: Franchisor intends to enforce arbitration provision despite Michigan law potentially voiding it.
