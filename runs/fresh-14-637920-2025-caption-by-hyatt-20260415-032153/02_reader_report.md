- **AOP Term**: Begins on effective date, typically ends 3 years after hotel opening.
- **During AOP Term**: Franchisor and affiliates will not open/authorize another Brand Hotel within AOP, except for acquisitions of groups of 4+ hotels.
- **After AOP Term**: No territorial protection whatsoever. Fully nonexclusive.
- **Operating Brand Hotels**: May receive no AOP at all.
- **No exclusive territory**: Expect competition from other Hyatt Network Hotels and brands at any time.
- **Franchisor reservations**: Can use Internet, telemarketing, and other channels to sell in AOP without compensation.

## Pass E: Contract Burden, Legal Mechanics (Item 17)

### Term and Renewal
- **Initial term**: 20 years from first day of month hotel opens.
- **Renewal**: 10-year successor franchise if: substantially complied during term, currently in full compliance, meets then-current new franchisee standards, passing QA scores for previous 3 years, has rights to maintain possession for at least 10 years.
- **Renewal conditions**: Must renovate/remodel/expand Hotel and FF&E; sign then-current franchise agreement (which may be materially different, including fees); pay PIP fee; sign general releases (if state law allows).
- **"Materially different" risk**: Explicitly stated multiple times that successor agreement may contain materially different terms.

### Termination
- **By franchisee**: Cannot terminate Franchise Agreement or Confidentiality Agreement (except as law allows). Can terminate Central Services Agreement on 10 days' notice for franchisor's uncured material breach. Can terminate Hosting Services Agreement on 30 days' notice for uncured breach, or 6 months' notice without cause.
- **By franchisor without cause**: Cannot terminate Franchise Agreement without cause. Can terminate Central Services, Hosting, and program schedules on 30–90 days' notice.
- **By franchisor with cause — curable**: 24 hours for health/safety; 10 days for insurance or payment failures; 30 days for most other defaults; 60 days for Guarantor Monetary Threshold failure.
- **By franchisor with cause — non-curable**: Bankruptcy, judgments over $100K, ceasing operations, contesting IP, unauthorized transfer, felony conviction, false books, unauthorized disclosure of Confidential Information, repeated defaults.

### Transfer
- Requires franchisor approval for all transfers of Franchise Agreement, Hotel, assets, or Controlling Ownership Interests.
- Permitted without approval: mortgages to lenders, estate planning transfers by owners, non-Controlling Ownership Interest transfers (with notice within 30 days).
- Conditions: transferee qualifies, transfer fee paid, amounts current, transferee may need to sign then-current agreement, you and guarantors sign release, transferee agrees to renovate, capital structure reviewed.
- **Right of first offer**: Only if Exhibit C is signed (for strategic markets).
- **Death/disability**: Representative must transfer to approved transferee within 6 months.

### Noncompete
- **During term**: No covenant not to compete, but neither franchisee nor management company may be Competing Brand Owner.
- **Post-term**: None.

### Dispute Resolution
- **Arbitration**: All disputes must be arbitrated within 10 miles of franchisor's principal business address (currently Chicago, IL).
- **Choice of law**: Illinois law (except Federal Arbitration Act and other federal law).
- **Choice of forum**: Illinois (subject to arbitration and state law).

### Liquidated Damages
- Lesser of 36 months or remaining term × (Average Monthly Revenue × 8.5% [5% royalty + 3.5% commercial services])
- 150% multiplier for Consequential Termination (transfer to Competitor or 3+ franchise agreements terminated for default)
- Casualty termination: lesser of liquidated damages or Net Recovery (insurance - lender - unreturned capital)

### Post-Termination Obligations
Return confidential materials, de-identify Hotel, stop using Hotel System, change telephone listings, stop brand references, cancel registrations, allow inspections, pay amounts owed, pay liquidated damages, stop using services.
