## Unresolveds

1. **No FPR data**: Zero financial performance information from franchisor. A prospective franchisee must rely on the single operating franchised hotel or independent research. This is a significant data gap for a $28M–$88M investment.
2. **AOP Term negotiability**: FDD says "typically 3 years after opening" but exact AOP Term is deal-specific. Prospective franchisee should negotiate.
3. **Guarantor Monetary Threshold**: Specific minimums not disclosed in FDD (determined per deal). Could be material constraint for smaller investors.
4. **Competing Brand Owner definition breadth**: The 5-hotel threshold for "Competing Brand" is relatively low and could restrict management company options.
5. **Renovation cost unpredictability**: Periodic upgrades may be required with no cap on frequency or cost; FF&E reserve may be insufficient.

## Contradictions

1. **Item 20 Table 3 vs. Exhibit G**: Table 3 shows 1 franchised outlet at end of 2024 (Tennessee), but Exhibit G lists 4 franchisees (2 CA, 2 TN). No contradiction — the other 3 are signed but not yet open, consistent with Table 5 showing 3 signed not opened. Table 3 correctly shows only the 1 that actually opened.
2. **Franchisor revenue from franchisee purchases**: Item 8 states "neither we nor any of our affiliates received rebates from suppliers based on franchisee purchases" for fiscal 2024, and "did not derive any revenue from selling or leasing products" to franchisees. However, the franchisor charges substantial System Services fees and IT fees that may include profit on some Non-Mandatory Services. Not technically contradictory but warrants note.
