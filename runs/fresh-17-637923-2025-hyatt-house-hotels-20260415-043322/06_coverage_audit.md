## Missing or Partial Coverage

1. **Financial notes depth** — Notes to Hyatt Hotels Corp financial statements span pages 116-171. Key items identified (debt structure, goodwill impairment, loyalty program) but full note extraction needed in A2.
2. **Contract burden depth** — Franchise Agreement (Exhibit C, pages 188-258) not deep-read clause-by-clause. Deferred to A2.
3. **State addenda structured overrides** — States with riders identified but not structured into override families. Deferred to A2.
4. **Franchisor entity balance sheet** — California state pages include unaudited balance sheet of Hyatt House Franchising, L.L.C. (page 364). Not fully extracted.

## Contradictions

1. **Item 20 outlet count vs Item 19 covered hotels**: Item 20 shows 118 total outlets (109 franchised) at end of 2024. Item 19 uses 115 Covered Hotels (excluding 4 that opened during 2024). This is consistent — not a contradiction, but the difference should be noted.
2. **No material contradictions identified** between FDD sections.

## Unresolveds

1. **AOP Term negotiability**: The FDD states AOP Term is "typically" 3 years after opening but is negotiable. Actual terms may differ significantly.
2. **Guarantor Monetary Threshold amounts**: Determined per-deal; no standardized minimums disclosed in the FDD body.
3. **Goodwill impairment drivers**: $110M impairment in management/franchising segment. The reporting unit's carrying value ($1,116M) exceeded fair value. Specific business drivers not fully explained in the FDD, though the auditor's report references projected cash flows.
4. **Materially different renewal terms**: What specifically may change at renewal is unknowable from the FDD alone.
5. **Franchisor entity financial health**: The FDD provides parent (Hyatt Hotels Corp) consolidated financials, not standalone franchisor entity financials. California addendum includes unaudited balance sheet of the franchisor entity, but this is not the standard financial exhibit.
