RevPAR Index - The RevPAR Index measures a hotel’s RevPAR (revenue per available room) 
relative to an aggregated grouping of hotels (competitive set, market, tract, etc.). An index of 100 
represents a fair share compared to the aggregated group of hotels. An index greater than 100 
represents more than fair share of the aggregated group’s performance. 
  
RevPAR Index is calculated as follows:  
 
(Hotel RevPAR / Comp Set RevPAR) x 100 = RevPAR Index.

New Generation Comparable Hotels 
 
Of the 630 Comparable Hotels described above, 198 were opened during or after 2018, indicating 
the most recent generation or ‘prototype’ of Hilton Garden Inn branded hotels (“New Generation 
Comparable Hotels”). Of these 198 New Generation Comparable Hotels, 2 were Company-Managed 
and 196 were Franchisee-Managed. The financial performance results detailed in this section for 
2023 and 2024 are for the 2024 New Generation Comparable Hotels. 
 
The following charts show Average Room Rate and Average Occupancy for New Generation 
Comparable Hotels and the number and percentage of Company-Managed and Franchisee-
Managed New Generation Comparable Hotels that met or exceeded the average. Average Room 
Rate and Average Occupancy are calculated based on information routinely reported to Hilton 
Worldwide by individual System Hotels.
