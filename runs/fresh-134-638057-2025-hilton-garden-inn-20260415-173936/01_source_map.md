## Risk Flags (Early)

1. **Out-of-state dispute resolution** — Mediation/arbitration/litigation in Virginia; if rejected, New York (p.4)
2. **No renewal right** — Franchise agreement is non-renewable; re-licensing at franchisor's sole discretion with materially different terms possible (p.75)
3. **No post-term noncompete** — Confirmed: no post-term noncompete (p.83)
4. **Liquidated damages** — 60 months of average royalty fees (p.35)
5. **Heavy fee stack** — Royalty 5.5% + Program 4% = 9.5% base; plus Hilton Honors 3.6%, plus numerous technology and quality fees
6. **Michigan notice** — Full Michigan franchise investment law provisions included (pp.5–6)
7. **No exclusive territory** — Standard agreement provides no protected territory (p.65)
8. **Asset-light franchisor** — 0 company-owned hotels; all 750 are franchised
9. **Gag clause present** — Some franchisees signed provisions restricting ability to speak about experience (p.96)
10. **Hilton debt guarantee** — Company's assets pledged as collateral for Hilton parent debt (Note 7, p.145)
