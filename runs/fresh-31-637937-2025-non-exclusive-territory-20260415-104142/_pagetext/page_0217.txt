If Franchisee fails, refuses, or neglects promptly to pay any monies owing to 
Franchisor or its affiliates when due or to submit the financial or other information required by 
Franchisor under this Agreement, Franchisee shall have ten (10) days after its receipt from

cured within the specified time, or such longer period as applicable law may require, this 
Agreement shall terminate without further notice to Franchisee, effective immediately upon the 
expiration of the cure period.
