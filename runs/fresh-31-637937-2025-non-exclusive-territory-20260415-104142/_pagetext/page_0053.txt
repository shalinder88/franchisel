approval of transfer 
Section 14 
a. Transferee must apply to us and 
demonstrate qualifications necessary to 
conduct business. 
b. Transferee must successfully compete 
initial training program at transferee’s 
expense.

d. You must pay referral fee of $15,000, if 
applicable.

paid all you owe us and affiliates.

demonstrate qualifications necessary to 
conduct business.

initial training program at transferee’s 
expense. 
Transferee must sign new franchise 
agreement, which may contain different 
terms.

expires on expiration date of your 
Franchise Agreement.

general release in the form of Exhibit 
G-4 to Franchise Agreement.

competition agreements must be signed.

contract, which we must approve. 
l. You must procure an extended coverage 
errors and omissions insurance policy 
for at least two (2) years with us named 
as an additional insured.
