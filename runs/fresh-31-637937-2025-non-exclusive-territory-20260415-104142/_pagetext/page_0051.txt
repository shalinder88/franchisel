f. Termination by franchisor with

Section 15 
We may terminate only if you default. The 
Franchise Agreement describes defaults 
throughout. Please read it carefully. 
g. "Cause" defined – curable 
defaults 
Sections 15.4 and 15.5 
You have 10 days to cure monetary and 
financial defaults and 30 days to cure all 
other defaults except for non-curable 
defaults listed in the Franchise Agreement 
and h. below.

defaults
