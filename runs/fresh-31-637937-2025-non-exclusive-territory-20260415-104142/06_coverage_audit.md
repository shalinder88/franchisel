## Missing Tables
None — all material tables from Items 5, 6, 7, 19, 20, 21 are extracted.

## Omissions / Gaps
1. **Franchise agreement clause walk** — Exhibit G (pp. 187-231) not walked clause by clause. Key terms covered via Item 17 table, but operative detail deferred to A2.
2. **Pillar To Post standalone financials** — Not available. Only FS Brands consolidated. This is standard for multi-brand parent FDDs but limits visibility into Pillar To Post-specific financial health.
3. **Item 19 cost data** — FPR does not include any cost/expense/EBITDA data. No cost build-up available from the FDD.
4. **Exhibit A/B individual extraction** — Franchisee/former franchisee lists identified by page range but not individually extracted (34+ pages of contact data).

## Unresolveds
1. **(HIGH)** System shrinkage not explained — 73 units lost in 3 years, zero projected openings. FDD does not provide rationale.
2. **(MEDIUM)** Pillar To Post standalone financial position unknown — consolidated parent financials only.
3. **(MEDIUM)** Item 19 FPR shows sales only — prospective franchisee cannot assess profitability from FDD alone.
4. **(LOW)** Exclusive territory count display discrepancy on p.72 — Table 1 shows "2" in end-of-year column for 2024 but should be 36 based on Table 3 totals. Appears to be a net change value misplaced.

## Contradictions
1. **Item 20 Table 1 (Exclusive) — p.72**: Shows 2024 end-of-year as "2" but Table 3 totals (p.82) show 36 at end of 2024. The "2" appears to actually be the net change (-2), not the ending count. This is likely a formatting artifact in the PDF text extraction.
