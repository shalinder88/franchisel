in-fact to negotiate all Provider contracts, cooperative advertising and market development fund

cooperative advertising and market development funds, Residuals and Commissions attributable to Your

may request and authorize the vendors to release information about You and Your Store to WZ LLC.  You 
hereby ratify and approve all acts of WZ LLC as Your attorney-in-fact.  This power, being coupled with an 
interest, is irrevocable during the Term and following the termination of this Agreement and expiration of 
the Term.
