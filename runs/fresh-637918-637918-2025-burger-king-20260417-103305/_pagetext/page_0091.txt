SUMMARY
Any sale, assignment or transfer of your interest in the
BKL.
Our written consent is required; must be in connection
with sale of franchise rights.
Buyer must meet financial, operational, credit, legal
criteria, approval of contract of sale; comply with
ownership and corporate governing instrument
requirements; satisfaction of all obligations at time of
transfer; completion of training; payment of transfer fee;
execution of any assignment, a general release of us by
seller, and a current Franchise Agreement.
We have a right of first refusal to purchase any adjacent
property you control based on the same terms and
conditions of a bona fide offer from a third party. We have
20 business days after receipt of notice and furnishing of
all reasonably requested information in order to notify you
of its intent to accept or reject the offer.

The Agreement may only be modified or amended in
writing.
The BKL (and any applicable addenda) constitute the
entire agreement. Any representations or promises outside
of the disclosure document and the BKL may not be
enforceable.
Arbitration only in cases of condemnation; held in Miami
Dade County.
Florida law applies.
