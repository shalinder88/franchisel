## Key Observations

- **System size**: 6,701 U.S. locations (5,524 franchised + 1,177 company-owned as of Dec 31, 2024)
- **Worldwide**: 19,732 total BURGER KING Restaurants
- **Carrols acquisition**: RBI acquired Carrols Restaurant Group in May 2024, converting 1,023 franchised to company-owned
- **Net change 2024**: -77 total outlets (franchised down 1,116, company-owned up 1,039)
- **Exhibit volume**: 26 exhibit families (A through Z), enormously complex agreement stack
- **State addenda**: 96 pages of state-specific amendments (Exhibit P, pp.706–801)
- **Financial statements**: RBI consolidated + RBILP consolidated, audited by KPMG (109 pages)
- **Gag clause**: YES — "current and former franchisees signed provisions restricting their ability to speak openly" (p.126)
