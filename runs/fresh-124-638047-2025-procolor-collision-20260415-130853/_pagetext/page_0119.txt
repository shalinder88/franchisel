2.1
In this Agreement: 
2.1.1
“Affiliate” means, in respect of any person, any other person that directly, or

under common control with such specified person. The term person used in the 
immediately preceding sentence shall refer to any individual, partnership, firm, 
corporation, association, trust, unincorporated organization, joint venture, or 
other entity.

“Gross Sales” means the entire amount of all revenues derived from the sale of

excluded from Gross Sales all sales of cars, as well as sales derived from the
