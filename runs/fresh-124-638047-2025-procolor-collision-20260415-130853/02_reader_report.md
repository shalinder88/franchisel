# Reader Report — ProColor Collision USA LLC (FDD 03/2025)

## Pass A: Executive Snapshot

1. **Brand**: ProColor Collision — full-service auto collision repair franchise; operated under ProColor Collision USA LLC, a Delaware LLC formed April 23, 2020.
2. **Parent structure**: Owned by Mondofix USA LLC → Mondofix Inc. (Canada). Mondofix is also parent of Fix Auto (654 locations worldwide), Novus Glass (128 US + international), Carrossier ProColor (157 in Canada).
3. **System size**: 30 franchised locations, 0 company-owned as of Dec 31, 2024. Rapid growth trajectory: 6→10→19→30 over 2022-2024.
4. **Geography**: Heavily concentrated in California (24 of 30); also TX (4), MT (1), NC (1). Projecting 27 new franchised + 1 company-owned in 2025 across 12 states.
5. **Investment range**: Conversion: $38,300–$199,500; New build: $316,800–$3,129,500. Initial franchise fee: $20,000 ($10,000 for second+).
6. **Fee burden**: Monthly Franchise Fee = greater of $2,000/month or 3% Gross Sales (new); conversion has split base/growth structure at 1.5%/3%. Marketing Fund: 0.75% (max 1%). Alternative supplier fee up to 2.6%. CCC ONE subscription ~$1,200+/month.
7. **Item 19**: NO financial performance representation made. Zero FPR disclosure.
8. **Financial health**: Audited by Ernst & Young. Cumulative deficit of $4.17M as of Dec 31, 2024. Net loss of $1,896 in 2024 (dramatic improvement from $661K loss in 2023 and $1.34M in 2022). Revenue grew from $507K (2022) to $1.39M (2023) to $2.57M (2024). Still dependent on parent company financial support.
9. **Term**: 5-year initial term + up to 2 renewal terms of 5 years each (max 15 years). Renewal requires signing new franchise agreement which may have materially different terms.
10. **Territory**: Zip-code based, targeting 25% collision spend capacity. Limited exclusivity — franchisor retains broad rights for other brands, channels, M&A activity.
11. **Special risks (state-mandated)**: Out-of-state dispute resolution (Minnesota), short operating history, mandatory minimum payments for conversions.
12. **Noncompete**: During-term only — no involvement in collision repair or franchise systems relating to car collision. NO post-term noncompete.
13. **Litigation**: No ProColor-specific litigation. Two Mondofix matters (resolved).
14. **Terminations**: 3 terminations in 2022, 1 in 2023, 2 in 2024 — notable churn for a small system (6 total terminations out of cumulative ~31 agreements signed through 2024).

## Pass D: Territory, Channels, Encroachment (Items 12-16)

### Item 12 — Territory
- Territory defined by zip codes, based on 25% of available collision spend capacity
- No minimum territory size; determined by mapping software
- **Exclusivity**: Franchisor will not operate or grant rights for ProColor Collision mark within Territory, AS LONG AS franchisee is in compliance
- **Carve-outs**:
  - Insurance/fleet/national account failure: franchisor can appoint other shops in Territory
  - Franchisor retains all rights outside Territory
  - Can operate other brands (Fix Auto, Novus, etc.) inside Territory
  - M&A: can acquire/merge with other networks and operate inside Territory
  - Capacity overflow: if demand exceeds capacity, franchisee has 30 days to decide to expand or open second location; failure = franchisor can open in Territory
- No options or rights of first refusal for additional franchises
- Other franchisees may serve customers from within your Territory
- No internet/telemarketing/direct marketing without approval
- Relocation requires consent, must be within Territory

## Pass E: Contract Burden, Legal Mechanics (Item 17)

### Term and Renewal
- **Initial term**: 5 years
- **Renewals**: Up to 2 additional 5-year terms (max 15 years total)
- **Renewal conditions**: Written notice 210-270 days before expiration; sign new franchise agreement (may have MATERIALLY DIFFERENT terms); pay $5,000 renewal fee; execute general release; complete refresher training; remodel to current standards
- **Materially different terms risk**: YES — explicitly stated

### Termination
- **By franchisee**: Not applicable (no voluntary termination right)
- **By franchisor without cause**: Not applicable
- **By franchisor with cause**: Only on default
- **Curable defaults**: Most defaults, 30 days to cure (5 days for monetary)
- **Non-curable defaults**: Bankruptcy, insolvency, assignment for creditors; liquidation/abandonment; loss of premises; Gross Sales understatement 2+ times in 12 months; criminal act; disqualification from insurance work; unsatisfied judgment; in-term noncompete violation; default under any other agreement
- **Death/disability**: Not addressed in Item 17

### Transfer
- All transfers require franchisor approval
- Conditions: transferee must meet requirements, sign new FA for remaining term (may have different fees/territory); pay $10,000 transfer fee; sign release; appoint franchisor as attorney-in-fact for CCC ONE transfer
- No new initial franchise fee required of transferee
- Franchisor has right of first refusal to match any offer

### Noncompete
- **During term**: No involvement in collision repair business or franchise system/banner/chain related to car collision industry (subject to state law)
- **Post-term**: NOT APPLICABLE — no post-term noncompete disclosed

### Dispute Resolution
- Mediation first (location selected by mediator), then arbitration (Minneapolis, Minnesota, subject to state law)
- Governing law: Minnesota (subject to state law overrides)

### Modification
- No modifications without mutual consent, EXCEPT:
  - Manuals subject to unilateral change by franchisor
  - Any modification accepted by 80% of ProColor Collision Centers is binding on all

### Contract Assignment
- No restriction on franchisor's right to assign

### Unresolveds
1. **Termination rate**: 6 terminations in 3 years for a system of 5-30 units is a high rate. FDD does not disclose reasons for terminations.
2. **Fix Auto US conflict resolution**: Fix Auto US is operated by unaffiliated third party; no policy for resolving conflicts between ProColor and Fix Auto franchisees. Mondofix owns both brands.

4. **CCC ONE vendor lock-in**: 36-month minimum contract with franchisor-designated vendor. Franchisee must appoint franchisor as attorney-in-fact over CCC ONE accounts.
5. **80% modification rule**: Any FA modification accepted by 80% of ProColor locations is binding on all — effectively removes individual consent for contract changes.
