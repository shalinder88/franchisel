NOTE 3. REVENUE

          Revenue is recognized when obligations under the terms of a contract with the customer are satisfied; generally, this
occurs with the transfer of control of products or services. The Company satisfies performance obligations either over time or at
a point in time as discussed in further detail below. Revenue is measured as the amount of consideration expected to be received
in exchange for transferring goods or providing services. Applicable sales tax collected concurrent with revenue-producing
activities is excluded from revenue.
