NOTE 11. SUBSEQUENT EVENTS

         Management evaluated subsequent events and transactions that occurred after the balance sheet date through
June 3, 2025, the date the financial statements were available to be issued.

          In the second quarter of 2025, the Company remitted $20.9 million to Jacks Management for principal and
interest accrued on the Shareholder Loan. Subsequently in the second quarter of 2025, the Company remitted $20.6
million to Bank of China for its $20.0 million May 2025 amortization payment due on the 1L Term Loan, as well as
$0.6 million for its 2023 ECF obligation that was due at the time of its first scheduled amortization payment in
accordance with the August 2024 amendment and restatement to the Facilities Agreement.

         Additionally, in the second quarter, the Company entered into a Revolving Loan ("Facility Letter") with the
Bank of East Asia, Limited (“BEA”) for up to CNY 1,500,000,000 to finance working capital operations. At the
discretion of the Company, each drawdown may be denominated in several pre-selected currencies and may remain
outstanding for a period of 360 days. Interest is calculated depending on the currency elected by the Company at each
drawdown, and is payable in arrears on the due date of each interest period selected. All financial covenants shall be
maintained by the Parent. In addition to the Facility Letter, a Standby Letter of Credit Agreement was signed between
the Parent and the Bank of East Asia (China) Limited, Harbin Branch ("BEA Harbin"), for a term of 5 years.
Subsequently, a Letter of Credit was issued from BEA Harbin to BEA for a term of 360 days for which the Company
will pay a 1.15% fee. The letters of credit serve as sole security for the performance of the Company's obligations
under the revolving credit facility.

          On May 21, 2025, the Company drew JPY 5,818,000,000, or $40.3 million as converted to USD on the date
of the transaction, to finance working capital operations. In accordance with the Facility Letter, interest is payable at
a rate of 1.35% for amounts denominated in JPY, however the Company received a one-time interest rate reduction
to 1.04% on the first drawdown. The Company selected a one-month interest period. Additionally, the Company will
pay 4.2% over the term of this initial drawdown to fully hedge any foreign currency exchange rate risk.
