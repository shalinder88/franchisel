 NOTE 10. LEASES

           The Company has operating leases for retail stores, distribution centers, other leased office locations, vehicles and certain
 equipment with remaining lease terms of one to 13 years, some of which include options to extend the leases for up to 15 years.
 The weighted average remaining lease term was 5.3 years and 5.9 years for the years ended December 31, 2024 and 2023,
 respectively. The weighted average discount rate was 8.1% and 7.2% for the years ended December 31, 2024 and 2023,
 respectively. On the Company’s Consolidated Balance Sheets as of December 31, 2024 and 2023, respectively, the Company had
 lease liabilities of $153.5 million and $162.9 million, of which $43.9 million and $43.4 million are classified as current, and right-
 of-use assets of $135.7 million and $153.4 million.
