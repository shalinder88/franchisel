NOTE 4. INCOME TAXES

         GNC Holdings, LLC files a consolidated federal tax return and various consolidated and separate tax returns as
prescribed by the tax laws of the state, local and international jurisdictions in which it and its subsidiaries operate.

          The Company’s effective tax rate was (3.7)% and 20.0% for the three months ended March 31, 2025 and 2024,
respectively. The Company recognized income tax expense of $0.6 million during the three months ended March 31, 2025
compared with $2.8 million of income tax benefit in the prior year quarter. The Company's income tax is based on income,
statutory tax rates and tax planning opportunities available in the jurisdictions in which it operates. The Company’s tax provision
is calculated by applying the most recent annualized effective tax rate to year-to-date pre-tax ordinary loss. The tax impact of
unusual or infrequent items are recorded discretely in the interim period in which they occur. There were no unusual or infrequent
items recorded in the three months ended March 31, 2025 or 2024.

          As of March 31, 2025, the company has an uncertain tax position in the amount of $0.1 million related to apportionment
positions taken on the 2021 state tax returns. Management believes no additional reserves are required for unrecognized tax
benefits for the quarter ended March 31, 2025.
