Jacks Management (Suzhou) Limited

         In March 2024, the Company entered into a Shareholder Loan agreement with Jacks Management for the principal sum
of $20.0 million, as discussed within Note 7. "Debt / Interest Expense", used to satisfy a portion of the April 2024 1L Term Loan
amortization payment. The Company received the loan proceeds in April 2024 and is required to repay the principal and all
accrued and unpaid interest, as converted to Renminbi equivalent, within one year of receipt ("maturity date"). Pursuant to the
terms of the agreement, any amounts that remain unpaid at the maturity date may be offset against amounts owed by Jacks
Management or its affiliates. The principal amount of the Shareholder Loan is classified as current on the Company's Consolidated
Balance Sheet as of December 31, 2024.

Equity Method Investment

         As of December 31, 2024 and 2023, the Company had a 35% ownership of each of the HK JV and China JV, which are
accounted for under the equity method. The Company recognized revenues generated from transactions with the HK JV and
China JV of $5.4 million, $4.2 million, and $14.4 million for the years ended December 31, 2024, 2023, and 2022, respectively.
The Company had $2.0 million and $17.0 million accounts receivable outstanding as of December 31, 2024 and 2023,
respectively.
