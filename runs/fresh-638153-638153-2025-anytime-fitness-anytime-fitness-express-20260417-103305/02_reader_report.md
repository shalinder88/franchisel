# Reader Report — Anytime Fitness FDD (638153-2025)

## A. Executive Snapshot

1. **Brand**: Anytime Fitness — 24-hour fitness centers offering convenient access, personal/group training, nutrition, coaching, and recovery services.
2. **Entity**: Anytime Fitness Franchisor LLC (Delaware LLC), formed Oct 2021 as part of securitization transaction. Indirect subsidiary of Purpose Brands Holdings LLC (jointly owned by Anytime Worldwide LLC and Ultimate Fitness Holdings LLC).
3. **Predecessor**: Anytime Fitness LLC (AFLLC) — offering franchises since Oct 2002; now serves as manager under management agreement.
4. **System size**: 2,290 franchised + 11 company-owned = 2,301 total outlets at end of 2024. Net shrinkage of 9 units in 2024 (46 opened, 33 terminated, 21 non-renewed, 0 reacquired, 0 ceased operations). 179 franchise agreements signed but not yet opened; 48 projected new openings.
5. **Affiliates**: Orangetheory Fitness (OTF, 1,283 franchised studios), Waxing the City (151 studios), Basecamp Fitness (19+4), The Bar Method (73 studios). All under Purpose Brands umbrella.
6. **Investment range**: $458,826–$907,607 total initial investment for a 4,000–6,300 sq ft center.
7. **Fee structure**: $42,500 initial franchise fee (standard); $820/month royalty (Monthly Fee, can be replaced with up to 8% of Gross Revenue on 30 days' notice); $600/month ad fund; $799/month tech fee (10% annual escalator). Numerous additional fees.
8. **Item 19**: FPR provided — 3 sections. All Coaching Suite centers (1,656): avg total revenue $442,686, median $394,973. SmartCoaching centers (297): avg $531,750. Company-owned P&L (11): EBITDA avg $144,002 (27.21% margin).
9. **Special risks**: 6 flagged — out-of-state dispute resolution, spousal liability, mandatory minimum payments, supplier control, financial condition of guarantor, unopened franchises.
10. **Financial health**: SEB Franchising Guarantor LLC is a $5M cash shell. Anytime Fitness LLC (consolidated) has $111M total assets vs $1.06B total liabilities, member's deficit of ($949M). Long-term debt $955.6M (securitization notes). Revenue $317M, net income $113M. The deep negative equity reflects securitization financing, not operational insolvency. Special risk #5 flags the guarantor's financial condition.
11. **Territory**: Protected territory within up to 3-mile radius, max 30,000 population. Not exclusive — franchisor can operate other brands within territory.
12. **Noncompete**: During term — anywhere, any fitness business. Post-term — 2 years, within territory + 10-mile radius (5 miles in metros >50k).
13. **Gag clause**: YES — current and former franchisees have signed provisions restricting ability to speak openly.
14. **Transfers 2024**: 207 transfers across system (down from 243 in 2023).
15. **Term**: 6-year initial; 5-year renewal. Renewal requires signing new agreement (may contain materially different terms), $7,500 fee, general release, facility update.

## B. Front Matter, Formats, Fees, Investment (Items 1–7)

## D. Territory, Competition, Channels, Encroachment (Items 12–16)

The territory protections are limited. While the franchisor cannot place another Anytime Fitness center in your protected territory, they can:
- Operate or franchise Orangetheory, Basecamp, Bar Method, or Waxing the City studios within your territory
- Sell products/services to your members via any channel including internet
- Provide virtual fitness instruction to your members via website/app
- Modify your territory upon renewal

The prohibition on franchisee internet/app-based selling (no livestreaming, recorded classes, etc.) while franchisor retains full digital distribution rights creates an asymmetric channel restriction.

## E. Contract Burden, Legal Mechanics (Item 17)

### Term and Renewal
- **Initial term**: 6 years
- **Renewal**: One 5-year renewal period if in good standing
- **Renewal conditions**: Sign new agreement (may contain materially different terms); update/relocate to current standards; sign general release (subject to state law); pay $7,500 renewal fee; complete refresher training
- **ADA term**: 1–4 years depending on number of commitments; no renewal right

### Termination
- **By franchisee**: Only if franchisor materially breaches and fails to cure within 30 days (then 10 days' additional notice)
- **By franchisor with cause**: Failure to open in 12 months; default under FA, ADA, or any affiliate agreement (cross-default)
- **Non-curable defaults**: Liquidation/dissolution; failure to secure real estate; 7 consecutive days non-operation; abandonment; loss of business rights; unapproved transfers; fraud/conviction; 3 material breaches in 12 months; false books/records; withholding financial access; misuse of marks
- **Curable defaults**: 30 days to cure most defaults

### Transfer
- Transfer fee: $9,999 (open center) or $25,000 (pre-open)
- Transferee must sign new agreement on then-current form for remaining term (may have different fees/territory)
- Transferee must complete training, update facility
- Franchisor has right of first refusal
- Post-termination purchase right: Book value of assets

### Death/Disability
Heirs can assume rights if they meet transfer requirements.

### Noncompete
- **During term**: No involvement in any fitness center, exercise facility, health club, gym, or business offering exercise classes, PT, fitness equipment, group training, nutrition, or recovery services — anywhere (exception: pre-first-opening employment in non-keyless club open <18 hours/day)
- **Post-term**: 2 years, within protected territory or 10-mile radius of any Anytime Fitness center (5 miles in metros >50,000)

### Dispute Resolution
- Mediation required first (mediator selects location)
- Arbitration in Minneapolis, MN (subject to state law)
- Litigation venue: U.S. District Court for District of Minnesota or Ramsey County District Court
- Governing law: Minnesota (subject to state overrides)
