## Material Gaps

1. **Exhibit B Franchise Agreement clause walk** — Key operative sections read but not all 28 sections individually walked. Remaining gaps in Sections 3-7 (training/advertising detail), Section 12 (compliance detail), and Section 28 (general provisions). Impact: medium — most operative burden recovered via Item 17 chart and direct reads.

2. **Exhibit G Operator's Lease clause walk** — 33 pages not directly read. Rent mechanics, maintenance obligations, default triggers partially recovered via Item 6 footnotes and Item 17. Impact: medium — lease is the single largest economic burden.

3. **Exhibit C/D variant franchise agreements** — Not read. Different economics and terms (Satellite, Walmart) may differ materially. Impact: medium for specialty formats only.

4. **Exhibit N Loan Documents** — 35 pages not directly read. Cross-default, collateral, prepayment terms partially from Item 10. Impact: low-medium.

5. **Item 19 cohort comparability dollar impact** — 4% vs 5% royalty difference identified but dollar impact at each sales tier not computed. Impact: high — this is a material buyer-facing gap.
