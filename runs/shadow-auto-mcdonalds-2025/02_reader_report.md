# Reader Report — McDonald's USA, LLC FDD (638437-2025)

## A. Executive Snapshot

1. **System Size:** 13,559 U.S. restaurants at end of 2024 (12,887 franchised / 672 company-owned); ~95% franchised.
2. **Entity Structure:** McDonald's USA, LLC (Delaware LLC), wholly-owned subsidiary of McDonald's Corporation (Delaware). Formed 2004, assets transferred 2005.
3. **Ownership History:** McDonald's Corporation began granting franchises in 1955. U.S. operations transferred to McDonald's USA, LLC in 2005.
4. **Investment Range:** Traditional $1,471,000-$2,728,000; STO/STR $1,014,500-$1,793,500; Satellite $525,000-$1,193,500.
5. **Fee Burden:** Royalty 5% (new) or 4% (legacy); Percentage Rent generally 6-23% (min 11.50% from yr 8); Advertising min 4% (OPNAD 2.25% + local); Technology fees ~$10,000+/yr aggregate. Total occupancy + royalty can exceed 20% of gross sales.
6. **Item 19 Highlights:** Avg gross sales $4,002,000 (all traditional); median $3,838,000. Pro forma OIBOC at $3M = $734,000 (24.5%). CRITICAL: Pro forma used 4% royalty but new franchisees pay 5%.
7. **Bottom-Quartile Risk:** Lowest annual sales $914,000 (traditional). Pro forma does not deduct rent or royalty. Effective rent 0-33.33%.
8. **Financial Health (Item 21):** E&Y unqualified opinion. 2024 revenue $10.63B, net income $3.46B. Total assets $22.2B. Dividends = 100% of net income to parent. $2.4B intercompany debt converted to equity Feb 2025.
9. **Growth Trajectory:** Net +102 in 2024 (vs +2 in 2023, +19 in 2022). 195 projected openings for 2025.
10. **Territory:** No exclusive territory, no protected area. McDonald's reserves all rights including non-restaurant channels.
11. **Renewal:** No right to renew. Discretionary new term at McDonald's sole discretion via New Term Policy.
12. **Disputes:** IL governing law. No mandatory arbitration/mediation. Significant pending litigation including antitrust no-poach suits, racial discrimination, E. coli class action, McRib marketing class action.
13. **Special Risks:** Out-of-state dispute resolution (IL venue). Michigan notices. Gag clause disclosed. Washington AOD on no-poach.

## D. Territory, Competition, Channels, Encroachment (Items 12-16)

### Protected Territory
None. The Franchise Agreement grants authority to use the McDonald's System at a specific location only. No exclusive grant, exclusive area, or territorial rights of any kind.

### Encroachment Risk
Explicitly stated: McDonald's may establish other franchisee or company-owned outlets that compete with your location. McDonald's reserves all rights to use Marks in other channels of distribution.

### Channel Restrictions
McCafe packaged coffee sold in supermarkets and via e-commerce. McDonald's reserves right to sell other goods/services under same or different marks.

## E. Contract Burden and Legal Mechanics (Item 17)

### Term and Renewal
- Traditional: 20 years. STO/STR: 10 years. BFL: 3 years. Satellite: varies.
- **No right to renew or extend.** New Term Policy (Exhibit K) is not part of Franchise Agreement.
- New term decision made in year 16-17 by New Term Committee.
- New term conditioned on consistent NFS performance, available real estate tenure, and economic viability.
- New term: current franchise agreement form, same royalty rate, new initial fee, rent per current new term rent policy.
- If not offered new term: may sell to qualified buyer.
- "Materially different" risk: new term requires signing then-current franchise agreement.

### Transfer
- Requires McDonald's prior written consent (not to be arbitrarily withheld).
- ROFR: 10 days to match any offer.
- Transfer conditions: new franchisee qualifies, assumes full liability, original remains personally liable for remainder of term.
- Royalty automatically adjusts to then-current rate upon transfer.
- Death/disability: may assign to spouse/heirs/relatives if qualified; McDonald's may manage up to 12 months, then option to purchase at FMV.

### Termination
**Material breaches (Section 18 — non-curable defaults):**
- Fail to maintain clean/wholesome operation
- Bankruptcy/insolvency
- Non-payment (30 days)
- Judgment > $5,000 unsatisfied 30+ days
- Loss of possession
- Acquiring competing interest
- Duplicating McDonald's System
- Unauthorized disclosure of System/manuals
- Unauthorized trademark use
- Knowing sale of unapproved foods
- Unauthorized transfer
- Denying McDonald's access
- Failure to pay supplier invoices promptly
- Misrepresentation re: franchise ownership
- Conduct damaging McDonald's reputation (but legitimate political activity protected)
- Felony conviction
- Intentional understatement of Gross Sales

**Other breaches (Section 19):** Not grounds for termination except for repeated breaches. McDonald's may seek injunction/damages. Any uncured breach is sufficient to withhold transfer approval.

**Effect of termination:** McDonald's may enter and take possession. Option to purchase furniture/fixtures/equipment at FMV for 30 days. No payment for intangible assets. Must return manuals, cease using System/marks.

### Noncompete
- **During term:** No involvement in any competing or similar restaurant business.
- **Post-term:** 18 months, 10-mile radius from the restaurant.
- Exception: <2% ownership in publicly traded company.
- Note: North Dakota addendum says noncompetes generally unenforceable there.

### Dispute Resolution
- No mandatory arbitration or mediation in Franchise Agreement.
- Open door policy and Ombudsman process as common practice (not contractual).
- Governing law: Illinois.
- No choice of forum clause (except state-specific addenda).
- Costs of enforcement: McDonald's entitled to attorneys' fees if it prevails.

### Litigation History
See Item 3 above — extensive pending and concluded cases.
