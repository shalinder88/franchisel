## Pass A — Executive Snapshot

1. **Franchisor entity**: American Dairy Queen Corporation (ADQ), a Delaware corporation incorporated in 1962. Wholly owned subsidiary of International Dairy Queen, Inc. (IDQ), which is wholly owned by Berkshire Hathaway, Inc.
2. **System size**: Over 7,700 DQ restaurants and stores globally. As of 12/31/2024: 1,971 DQ Grill & Chill (direct-licensed US), 567 subfranchised G&C/Brazier, 568 Texas DQ, 727 DQ Treat (US), 510 G&C Canada, 184 Treat Canada, 2,853 international, plus Orange Julius.
3. **Concept**: Quick-service food restaurant with seating selling the full DQ soft-serve, treat, food and beverage menu. "Street" (freestanding/strip) and "Captive-venue" (mall/airport/campus) formats.
4. **Investment range**: $1,516,200 – $2,543,050 (excludes land). Building/construction $800K–$1.4M; equipment $550K–$700K.
5. **Fee burden**: Initial franchise fee $45,000. Ongoing: 4% royalty + 5–6% sales promotion program fee = **9–10% of gross sales** ongoing fee load. Transfer fee $6,000. Renewal fee $22,500.
6. **Item 19 highlights**: 2024 average gross sales $1,427,281 (321 qualifying restaurants, 44% at or above). Median $1,355,422. Manageable profit (before occupancy, royalties, insurance) averaged 28.92% of gross sales for 55 restaurants with usable P&Ls in 2024.
7. **Growth trajectory**: Modest net growth: +13 (2022), +2 (2023), +2 (2024) for direct-licensed G&C. 30 new openings vs. 28 terminations in 2024. Subfranchised outlets declining (-6 in 2023, +1 in 2024). Texas DQ declining (-11 in 2024).
8. **Territory**: Site-only franchise, NO exclusive territory. ADQ reserves broad rights to compete via any channel including internet, other brands, captive venues. MultiTRA grants non-exclusive development area with carve-outs.
9. **Term**: 20 years initial, one 10-year renewal (or remaining lease term if shorter). Renewal requires signing then-current agreement which "may differ materially, including higher or additional fees."
10. **Noncompete**: During term: no quick-service restaurant serving hamburgers (without alcohol) or business with >10% revenue from frozen treats. Post-term: 500 meters from authorized location for 1 year (Street); same building/venue (Captive-venue).
11. **Dispute resolution**: Arbitration in Minneapolis, MN (subject to state law overrides). Litigation: Federal District Court for the District of Minnesota or Hennepin County.
12. **Special risks**: Out-of-state dispute resolution flagged. Michigan notice requires good cause for termination, fair compensation on non-renewal.
13. **Financial health**: IDQ total assets $355.9M, total liabilities $275.6M, stockholder's equity $80.3M. Net income $97.9M (2024). IDQ guarantees ADQ's performance.
14. **Termination fee**: Two times the royalty for last 12 months (or prorated formula). This is a material fee.

## Pass E — Contract Burden, Legal Mechanics (Item 17)

### Franchise Agreement Summary

| Provision | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Initial term** | 20 years (new restaurant); lesser of 20 years or remaining transferor term (transfer) |
| **Renewal** | One additional term: shorter of 10 years or remaining lease term |
| **Renewal conditions** | Written notice; sign then-current agreement (may differ materially including higher fees); modernize to current standards; good standing; pay $22,500 renewal fee; sign release |
| **Termination by franchisee** | For material breach, 30 days' written notice, cure within 30 days; effective 60 days after notice |
| **Termination by franchisor** | For cause only. Cure periods: 24 hours (trademark goodwill/health/safety); 10 days (late reports/payments); 30 days (other curable defaults) |
| **Non-curable defaults** | Loss of premises; failure to reopen after destruction/relocation; abandonment; insolvency; conviction; intentional underreporting; third default within 12 months; OFAC designation |
| **Post-termination** | Stop using trademarks; pay all amounts due; comply with noncompete; maintain confidentiality; pay termination fee |
| **Transfer** | All transfers require ADQ consent (not unreasonably withheld). Conditions: transferee qualifies; transfer fee paid; amounts current; release signed; transferee signs then-current agreement (may have materially different terms); modernization completed |
| **Right of first refusal** | ADQ can match any offer to purchase franchise, restaurant, franchisee entity, or owner |
| **Death/disability** | Must comply with all transfer requirements |
| **Noncompete (during)** | No involvement in quick-service restaurant serving hamburgers (without alcohol) or business generating >10% from frozen treats ("Competitive Business") |
| **Noncompete (post)** | 500 meters from location, 1 year (Street); same building/venue (Captive-venue) |
| **Modification** | Must be signed by both parties; ADQ can unilaterally change menu, Operations Manual, trademarks |
| **Dispute resolution** | Arbitration in Minneapolis, MN (except certain claims); subject to state law |
| **Choice of forum** | Fed. Dist. Ct. for District of MN or Hennepin County, Minneapolis |
| **Choice of law** | Law of state where authorized location is situated |
| **Assignment by franchisor** | No restriction |

### MultiTRA Summary
- Term varies; no right to renew or extend
- Not transferrable by franchisee
- Termination by franchisee only for material breach (60 days to cure)
- Non-curable defaults: voluntary bankruptcy, unapproved transfers, felony conviction, trademark impairment
- Choice of law: state where trade area is located

### Key Contract Risks
1. **"Materially different" renewal risk**: Renewal requires signing then-current agreement, which may have materially different terms including higher or additional fees. This is a significant re-pricing risk.
2. **No territory protection**: Site-only franchise with no territorial rights. ADQ and affiliates can open competing units or sell via any channel without compensation.
3. **Broad noncompete**: Covers any QSR serving hamburgers (without alcohol) or any business with >10% frozen treat revenue during term.
4. **Termination fee**: Two times last 12 months' royalty — material financial penalty.
5. **Unilateral modifications**: ADQ can change menu, Operations Manual, and trademarks without franchisee consent.
6. **Release required**: On renewal and transfer.
