Project Lonestar, Inc. vs. American Dairy Queen Corporation (United States District Court, 
Northern District of Texas, Dallas Division, #3:25-cv-00339-L, filed February 11, 2025). Project 
Lonestar, Inc. initiated a lawsuit against American Dairy Queen Corporation seeking injunctive 
relief and damages.  On April 9, 2025, Plaintiff filed an amended complaint alleging breach of 
contract, interference with contract and/or interference with prospective business relationship by 
ADQ, and fraud related to the transfer process for several of their franchised restaurants.  ADQ 
denies the claims and has asserted counterclaims against Project Lonestar, Inc., Elite Restaurant 
Group Inc. and Elite Ltd. for unpaid license, advertising, and termination fees. Discovery is 
underway, and ADQ will continue to vigorously defend itself and pursue its claims.
