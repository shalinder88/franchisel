3. HELP DESK SUPPORT SERVICES SERVICE LEVELS 
 
Help Desk Support Cases will be held open and PAR will continue the Communication Cadence with the 
Participating Location until final resolution is confirmed by the Participating Location regardless of 
whether PAR has made an External Case Referral. If PAR follows up with the Participating Location 
more than three (3) times attempting to confirm resolution without a response, then PAR will close the 
case noting that PAR did not receive a response. 
 
All Service Levels set forth in this Section will be measured on a monthly basis cumulative of all Help 
Desk Support Services provided to all Participating Locations in the Dairy Queen system. 
 
PAR will use commercially reasonable efforts to respond to all Participating Location(s) requests for Help 
Desk Support Services within the (a) Response Times, (b) Resolution Times and (b) Communication 
Cadence Times set forth in the chart below.
