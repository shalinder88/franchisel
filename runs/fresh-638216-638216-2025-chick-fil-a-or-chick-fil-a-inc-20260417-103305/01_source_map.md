## Key Observations

1. **License program FDD** — This is the Licensee program (non-traditional locations: universities, hospitals, airports, workplaces), NOT the traditional Operator franchise program. Chick-fil-A offers Operator franchises under a separate FDD.
2. **No initial fee** — Chick-fil-A charges $0 initial license fee.
3. **10% license fee** on gross receipts (7% for transportation venues).
4. **No protected territory** — no exclusive or protected territory granted.
5. **No renewal right** — license term determined by CFA; no contractual right to renew.
6. **No transfer** without CFA prior written approval; CFA has right but not obligation to approve.
7. **Item 19** — Two categories disclosed: college/university campuses (312 units, median $897,932, avg $1,395,745) and hospitals/businesses/airports (97 units, median $3,152,764, avg $3,451,788).
8. **Item 20** — 425 Licensed Units at end of 2024; 2,684 franchised/company Operator Restaurants. Total system: 3,109 domestic restaurants.
9. **Gag clause** — Operators (NOT Licensees) signed provisions restricting speech in past 3 years; Licensees did NOT sign such provisions.
10. **Financial statements** — Audited by PwC; total assets $16.59B (2024); net earnings $1.044B (2024); total revenues $9.063B (2024).
11. **State addenda** — Extensive (64 pages), covering numerous states with overrides to Items 11, 17.
12. **Item 3 Litigation** — Delivery pricing class action settled for $4.4M in 2024.
13. **Item 4 Bankruptcy** — Pier 1 Imports bankruptcy disclosed (director Bachelder connection).
